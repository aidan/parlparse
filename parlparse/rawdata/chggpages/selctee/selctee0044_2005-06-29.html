Select Committee Membership

Select Committees are appointed for the duration of a parliament and need to be re-appointed after the general election.  Following the election on 5 May 2005  the House will appoint MPs to serve on each committee; for most select committees this has not yet occurred.  Details of the new committee memberships will be published below as soon as they have been appointed.

Until new members are appointed the details of membership from before the general election can be found on the individual committee pages.
