The meeting was called to order at 10.10 a.m.
Agenda item 10 (continued)
Report of the Secretary-General on the work of the Organization
Report of the Secretary-General on prevention of armed conflict (A/55/985 and Corr.1)
Draft resolution (A/55/L.91)

The President

Members will recall that the debate on the report of the Secretary-General on prevention of armed conflict took place at the 106th, 107th and 108th plenary meetings, held on 12 and 13 July 2001.

The Assembly will now take a decision on draft resolution A/55/L.91, entitled "Prevention of armed conflict".

May I take it that the Assembly decides to adopt the draft resolution?

Draft resolution A/55/L.91 was adopted (resolution 55/281).

The President

The General Assembly has thus concluded this stage of its consideration of agenda item 10.

The meeting rose at 10.15 a.m.
