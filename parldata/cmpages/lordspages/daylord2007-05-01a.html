Roads: Motorway Bridges

Lord Fearn asked Her Majesty's Government:

    Whether motorway bridges are inspected for safety on a regular basis. [HL3388]

Lord Bassam of Brighton: Motorway bridges in England are inspected regularly in accordance with standard BD63/07, “Inspection of Highway Structures”, to ensure their continuing safety to carry traffic. The categories of inspection include safety, general, principal, special and inspection for assessment and these are undertaken at different intervals.
