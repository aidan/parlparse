Lord Avebury asked Her Majesty's Government:

    What information the Organisation for Security and Co-operation in Europe (OSCE) mission in Grozny has about the number of Russians held by Chechnya combatants, and of Chechens by the Russian authorities; and what access to the detainees on either side has been granted to the International Committee of Red Cross (ICRC).
