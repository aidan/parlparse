The UNHCR recommends that: non-Arab ethnic Darfuris are granted refugee status; some form of subsidiary protection is granted to those not granted refugee status; particular attention is paid to vulnerable groups such as female heads of household, medical cases or victims of past persecution; and that the Article 1(F) exclusion clause may be applied in individual cases. We agree that particular attention should be paid to claims from vulnerable groups of Darfuris and that the Article 1(F) exclusion clause should be applied in appropriate cases.
