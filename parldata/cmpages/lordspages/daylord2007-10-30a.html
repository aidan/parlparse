Young Offender Institutions: Stoke Heath

Baroness Stern asked Her Majesty's Government:

    How many prisoners were injured in the disturbance at Stoke Heath Young Offender Institution on 29 September; and how many of those were aged under 18; and [HL5616]

    How many officers involved in dealing with the disturbance at Stoke Heath young offender institution on 29 September were from other prisons; and [HL5617]

    Whether PAVA incapacitant spray was used in the disturbance at Stoke Heath young offender institution on 29 September. [HL5618]

The Parliamentary Under-Secretary of State, Ministry of Justice (Lord Hunt of Kings Heath): One prisoner was injured by other prisoners for refusing to join in the disturbance. He was under 18. A total of 42 staff from other establishments were involved in resolving the disturbance. Incapacitant spray was not used.
