Visas

Question

Asked by Baroness Hanham

    To ask Her Majesty's Government why the date of application for student visas is being set at the date of biometric registration and not the date of initial application and fee payment. [HL2323]

The Parliamentary Under-Secretary of State, Home Office (Lord West of Spithead): In accordance with the Immigration and Nationality (Fees) Regulations 2007, where a visa application is to be accompanied by a specified fee, the application is not considered to be valid until that fee has been paid. I therefore confirm that a visa application is deemed to be lodged on the date that the required fee is paid.
