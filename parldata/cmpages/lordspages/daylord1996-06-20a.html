Baroness Blatch: My right honourable friend the Home Secretary will be announcing the results of the 1996-97 Closed Circuit Television (CCTV) Challenge Competition on Friday 21st June. Details of the successful bids will be placed in the Library. The Government have stated their intention of making £45 million available for CCTV. Fifteen million pounds of this will be available in 1997-98.
