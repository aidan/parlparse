The House met at half-past Ten o'clock.

PRAYERS.

1    Adjournment (Government's strategy to improve the life chances of disabled people),—A Motion was made, and the Question being proposed, That this House do now adjourn—(Mrs Anne McGuire);

    And it being Six o'clock, the Motion for the adjournment of the House lapsed without Question put.

2    Adjournment,—Resolved, That this House do now adjourn.—(Mr Frank Roy.)

    And accordingly, the House, having continued to sit till Six o'clock, adjourned till Monday 8th May.

[Adjourned at 6 p.m.

Michael J. Martin

Speaker
