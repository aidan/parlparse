TRANSPORT

Newly Qualified Drivers

The Secretary of State for Transport (Mr. Alistair Darling): During my opening speech on Second Reading of the Road Safety Bill on Tuesday 11 January 2005, Official Report, at column 223, I said,

    "Indeed, one in five accidents involve a driver who has passed their test in the precedingyear".

What I should have said was that as many as one in five new drivers has an accident within the first year of passing the driving test.
