Written Answers to Questions

Thursday 25 November 2004

SCOTLAND

Departmental Websites

Mr. Nigel Evans: To ask the Secretary of State for Scotland for how many written questions tabled in the last parliamentary session his Department had been
 
25 Nov 2004 : Column 2W
 
unable to provide a substantive answer before the end of the session. [199648]

Mrs. McGuire: None, all the Office's outstanding written questions were answered before Prorogation.

Paul Holmes: To ask the Secretary of State for Scotland whether his Department's .gov.uk websites comply with the World Wide Web Consortium's Web Content Accessibility Guidelines adopted by the Government in 2001; and if he will make a statement. [199688]

Mrs. McGuire: The Scotland Office website (www.scotlandoffice.gov.uk) conforms to the Priority 1 requirements of the W3C Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 1.0.
