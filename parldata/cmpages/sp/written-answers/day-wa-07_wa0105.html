Written Answers Friday 5 January 2007

Scottish Executive

Parliamentary Questions

Alex Neil (Central Scotland) (SNP): To ask the Scottish Executive when it will provide a substantive answer to my question S2W-29639 which was due for answer on 20 November 2006.

 

(S2W-30747)

The answer to question S2W-29639 was provided on 22 December 2006.
