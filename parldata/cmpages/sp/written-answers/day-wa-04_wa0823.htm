Written Answers

Monday 23 August 2004

 

Scottish Parliamentary Corporate Body

Parliamentary Tours

Donald Gorrie (Central Scotland) (LD): To ask the Scottish Parliamentary Corporate Body how many applications it has received from companies expressing an interest in providing guided tour services of the new Parliament building and, of these, how many are from organisations outwith Scotland.

 

(S2W-9838)

Duncan McNeil (on behalf of the Scottish Parliamentary Corporate Body): As the SPCB is currently tendering for the provision of tour guide services, disclosure of information regarding the number of applications received at this time is sensitive and could prejudice the commercial interests of the SPCB. However, this information will be made available after the contract is awarded.
