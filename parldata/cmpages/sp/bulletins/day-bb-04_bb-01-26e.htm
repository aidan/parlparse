Business Bulletin No. 16 / 2004

Monday 26th January 2004

Section E : Written questions lodged on Friday 23rd January 2004
  

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S2W-5685 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive how many responses it received to the consultation on the proposed Gaelic Language Bill.

*The member has provided the following Gaelic translation:

A dh' fhaighneachd de Riaghaltas na h-Alba co mheud freagairt a fhuair e dhan obair cho-chomhairleachaidh air Bile na Gàidhlig.*

S2W-5686 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive when it intends to publish the Gaelic Language Bill.

*The member has provided the following Gaelic translation:

A dh' fhaighneachd de Riaghaltas na h-Alba cuine a tha e an dùil Bile na Gàidhlig fhoillseachadh.*

S2W-5687 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive what importance it places on the provision of Gaelic translations on signage in public offices.

*The member has provided the following Gaelic translation:

A dh' fhaighneachd de Riaghaltas na h-Alba dè cho cudthromach agus a tha e dhan Riaghaltas gum bi eadar-theangachaidhean Gàidhlig air soidhnichean ann an oifisean poblach.*
