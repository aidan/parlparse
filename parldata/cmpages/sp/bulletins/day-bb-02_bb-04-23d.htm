Business Bulletin No. 64/2002

Tuesday 23 April 2002

Section D : Oral Questions selected for answer on 25 April 2002
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive's Cabinet. (S1F-1836)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he plans to raise. (S1F-1844)

3. Iain Smith: To ask the First Minister what priority the Scottish Executive attaches to sustainable development. (S1F-1853)

4. Johann Lamont: To ask the First Minister what progress is being made in addressing any problems of youth disorder in local communities. (S1F-1854)

5. Mary Scanlon: To ask the First Minister how the additional NHS funding from the UK 2002 Budget will benefit people in Scotland. (S1F-1841)

6. Colin Campbell: To ask the First Minister what plans the Scottish Executive has to recruit additional police personnel. (S1F-1846)
