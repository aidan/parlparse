Business Bulletin No. 22/2002

Tuesday 5 February 2002

Section D : Oral Questions
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister what issues he intends to raise when he next meets the Prime Minister. (S1F-01627)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he plans to raise. (S1F-01633)

3. Pauline McNeill: To ask the First Minister what role trade unions have in improving public services. (S1F-01636)

4. Margaret Smith: To ask the First Minister whether any member of the expert group on the MMR vaccine has any links with any pharmaceutical company that produces the vaccine. (S1F-01642)

5. Mr Kenneth Gibson: To ask the First Minister what assistance the Scottish Executive will provide to local authorities in order to assist in repairing damage caused by the recent severe weather conditions. (S1F-01629)

6. Johann Lamont: To ask the First Minister what the prospects are for economic regeneration of the River Clyde. (S1F-01634)
