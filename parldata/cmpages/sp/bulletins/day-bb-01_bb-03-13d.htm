BUSINESS BULLETIN 43 / 2001

Tuesday 13 March 2001

Section D - Oral questions selected for answer on 15 March 2001

 

 

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister what issues he intends to raise at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive’s Cabinet. (S1F-918)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he plans to raise. (S1F-912)

3. Bristow Muldoon: To ask the First Minister what contribution the Budget announced by the Chancellor of the Exchequer last week will make to achieving the objectives of the Scottish Executive. (S1F-915)

4. Alex Johnstone: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive plans to reassure farmers and their representative bodies that the foot and mouth disease outbreak is under control. (S1F-924)

5. Bruce Crawford: To ask the First Minister what conditions were inserted into the trunk road management and maintenance contracts to protect the pension rights of employees transferring under the Transfer of Undertakings (Protection of Employment) regulations. (S1F-910)

6. Cathie Craigie: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive plans to encourage more people to take up engineering apprenticeships. (S1F-927)
