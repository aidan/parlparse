Announcements

 

Meeting of the Enterprise and Lifelong Learning Committee

 

The Enterprise and Lifelong Learning Committee will be holding a meeting on Friday 16 July at 12 noon in Committee Room 1, Committee Chambers. The meeting is to examine the current situation at the Kvaerner Govan shipbuilding yard. The Minister for Enterprise and Lifelong Learning has been invited to attend.
