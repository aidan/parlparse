Section F : Motions and Amendments
  

A full list of current motions is available to view each Monday in paper copy at the Chamber Desk or alternatively on the Scottish Parliament web site as Current Motions. The full text of all motions lodged the previous week will appear in the Business Bulletin the following Monday.

 

Items marked with an asterisk (*) are new or have been altered. Asterisks in the text show the extent of alterations made.

*S2M-191.1 Nicola Sturgeon: Modernising Justice—As an amendment to motion (S2M-191) in the name of Cathy Jamieson, insert at end ", and further recognises that the efficient administration of criminal justice depends on all parts of the system, not least on the Crown Office and Procurator Fiscal Service having adequate resources to meet the increasing demands placed on it."

*S2M-191.2 Miss Annabel Goldie: Modernising Justice—As an amendment to motion (S2M-191) in the name of Cathy Jamieson, leave out from "supporting" to end and insert "and, while welcoming proposals to make more efficient the processing and disposal of criminal cases, expresses concern at the proposed extension to the 110 day rule, the resources implication for sheriff courts of handling an increased workload and the continuing inadequacy of resources available to the police and the Crown Office and Procurator Fiscal Service."

*S2M-191.3 Colin Fox: Modernising Justice—As an amendment to motion (S2M-191) in the name of Cathy Jamieson, leave out from "notes" to end and insert "calls on the Scottish Executive to reverse cuts to legal aid resources and take immediate steps to reduce the workload of the Crown Office and Procurator Fiscal Service and sheriff courts."
