Business Bulletin No. 32/2003

Tuesday 18 February 2003

Section D : Oral Questions selected for answer on 20 February 2003
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive's Cabinet. (S1F-2515)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he next plans to meet the Prime Minister and what issues he intends to raise. (S1F-2518)

3. Sarah Boyack: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive will take account of the introduction of congestion charging in London in the development of transport policy. (S1F-2524)

4. Mr Duncan Hamilton: To ask the First Minister whether there is an adequate standard of transport infrastructure in the Highlands and Islands. (S1F-2514)

5. Mrs Margaret Smith: To ask the First Minister when the Scottish Executive will respond to the Office of Fair Trading report on community pharmacies. (S1F-2526)

6. Janis Hughes: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive will encourage healthy eating in schools. (S1F-2520)
