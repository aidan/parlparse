Business Bulletin No. 186/2002

Tuesday 17 December 2002

Section D : Oral Questions selected for answer on 19 December 2002

  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister when he next plans to meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he intends to discuss. (S1F-2368)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive’s Cabinet. (S1F-2364)

3. Pauline McNeill: To ask the First Minister whether a reduction in the level of subsidy to rail operators by the Strategic Rail Authority would have an impact on the ScotRail franchise. (S1F-2371)

4. Ian Jenkins: To ask the First Minister whether the Scottish Executive supports the issuing of guidelines on filming of schoolchildren. (S1F-2374)

5. Tricia Marwick: To ask the First Minister what input the Scottish Executive has had to the Bain review of the fire service. (S1F-2362)

6. Mr Duncan McNeil: To ask the First Minister what steps the Scottish Executive will take to help Argyll and Clyde NHS Board to build on the findings of the support team report into the effectiveness of the board’s health systems. (S1F-2367)
