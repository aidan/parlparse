Section E : Written questions lodged on Thursday 9th October 2003
  

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S2W-3307 Stewart Stevenson: To ask the Scottish Executive when the Minister for Education and Young People will reply to my letter of 22 August 2003 regarding fee waivers for training courses.
