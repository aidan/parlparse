Business Bulletin No. 162 / 2003

Wednesday 26th November 2003

Section E : Written questions lodged on Tuesday 25th november 2003
  

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S2W-4390 Mr Ted Brocklebank: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it plans to send a letter of congratulations to the English team on their result in the Rugby World Cup final in light of the impact on Scottish rugby.

S2W-4391 Rhona Brankin: To ask the Scottish Executive which local authority areas it will consider as locations for its relocated departments and agencies.

S2W-4392 Rhona Brankin: To ask the Scottish Executive how many jobs it expects to relocate under its policy of civil service job dispersal.

S2W-4393 Rhona Brankin: To ask the Scottish Executive what timetable it has set for the relocation of its departments and agencies.

S2W-4394 Rhona Brankin: To ask the Scottish Executive which of its departments and agencies are to be considered for relocation.

S2W-4395 Alex Fergusson: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it has any plans to explore the potential of "combi fuel".

S2W-4396 Alex Neil: To ask the Scottish Executive what representations it has made to Her Majesty's Government in respect of the draft Constitutional Treaty on the Future of Europe (Referendum) Bill.

S2W-4397 Alex Neil: To ask the Scottish Executive when it expects to announce the outcome of the review of the area tourist board network.

S2W-4398 Alex Neil: To ask the Scottish Executive what the estimated expenditure required to achieve its performance targets for reducing class sizes is, in each of the next three years, and how much of this expenditure has been allocated, broken down by (a) local authority area and (b) primary and secondary education target.

S2W-4399 Alex Neil: To ask the Scottish Executive what targets it has set for class sizes and what its performance against such targets has been to date.

S2W-4400 Mr Jamie Stone: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it has to encourage the reintroduction of flights from Wick to Shetland.

S2W-4401 Mr Jamie Stone: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it has assessed the demand for improved services from Wick Airport.

S2W-4402 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive why the draft Water Services (Scotland) Bill does not contain provisions to promote greater water conservation similar to those in the Water Act 2003.

S2W-4403 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive what contracts or other documents have established the legal relationship between Scottish Water and Scottish Water Solutions Ltd and whether such documents will be made publicly available.

S2W-4404 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive what the remuneration is of each director of Scottish Water Solutions Ltd.

S2W-4405 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive whether Scottish Water Solutions Ltd is required to lay an annual report before the Parliament.

S2W-4406 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive what external and independent advice was received by Scottish Water when shortlisting bidding consortia during the process leading to the establishment of Scottish Water Solutions Ltd.

S2W-4407 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive what information it has on other jurisdictions where water utilities have entered into relationships with the private sector similar to that between Scottish Water and Scottish Water Solutions Ltd.

S2W-4408 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive what powers the Auditor-General has to examine how Scottish Water Solutions Ltd manages its finances to ensure that it achieves value for money.

S2W-4409 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive whether the constitution of Scottish Water Solutions Ltd will be made public.

S2W-4410 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive what mechanisms will be established to monitor the 1,500 projects to be commissioned by Scottish Water Solutions Ltd.

S2W-4411 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive whether Scottish Water will be able to establish further joint ventures such as that with Scottish Water Solutions Ltd if the Draft Water Services (Scotland) Bill is enacted.

S2W-4412 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive what action is being taken by Scottish Water to reduce the levels of trihalomethanes in drinking water supplies.

S2W-4413 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive whether the former water authorities would have been prevented by legislation from establishing a joint venture such as that with Scottish Water Solutions Ltd, prior to the enactment of the Water Industry (Scotland) Act 2002.

S2W-4414 John Farquhar Munro: To ask the Scottish Executive how many people are on the waiting list for an organ transplant and what the average waiting time is for such an operation.

S2W-4415 Mr Jamie Stone: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it has to underpin and augment financial support for home-based respite care in north and west Sutherland.

S2W-4416 Rob Gibson: To ask the Scottish Executive what work has been carried out by the Scottish Enterprise Community Land Unit since its inception.

S2W-4417 Pauline McNeill: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-3052 by Nicol Stephen on 21 October 2003, what representations it has received on re-regulating the bus industry.

S2W-4418 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive whether a review of access to water and waste services is being carried out as part of work being undertaken for the Clyde Waterfront development project and what steps will be taken to deal with any development constraints in the context of Scottish Water's Quality and Standard III process.

S2W-4419 Shiona Baird: To ask the Scottish Executive what action it is taking to promote awareness among all employers of the coming into force on 2 December 2003 of the Employment Equality (Religion and Belief) Regulations 2003.

S2W-4420 Pauline McNeill: To ask the Scottish Executive how it will respond to any concerns by surveyors that the pilot sellers' surveys may operate in the interest of the seller.

S2W-4421 Pauline McNeill: To ask the Scottish Executive whether there is any added liability placed on a seller in the pilot sellers' survey schemes.

S2W-4422 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive when Scottish Water expects drinking water supplies will meet relevant national standards in relation to trihalomethanes.

S2W-4423 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive why the Procurator Fiscal decided to take no further action on the report submitted to him in respect of an alleged breach of section 76C of the Water (Scotland) Act 1980 by Scottish Water in North Ayrshire on 16 April 2002.

S2W-4424 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive what safeguards are in place to prevent water or sewerage service retail licensees from charging high amounts for services under the provision of the Draft Water Services (Scotland) Bill.

S2W-4425 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive what customer protection is provided for in the Draft Water Services (Scotland) Bill.

S2W-4426 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive what benefits it has identified in respect of disallowing competition in the sewage treatment sector.

S2W-4427 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive why it considers that the household sector would not benefit from competition in the provision of sewerage services.

S2W-4428 Pauline McNeill: To ask the Scottish Executive what action it will take to assist Glasgow City Council to improve Scottish Qualifications Agency exam performance amongst its pupils.

S2W-4429 Pauline McNeill: To ask the Scottish Executive how it will address underperformance in Scottish Qualifications Agency exams amongst pupils from areas with the highest poverty levels

S2W-4430 Jim Mather: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will provide a table showing how the balance outstanding on the Skye Bridge contract reduces annually by net discounted tolls.
