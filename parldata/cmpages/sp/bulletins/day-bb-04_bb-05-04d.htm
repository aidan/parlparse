Business Bulletin No. 74/2004

Tuesday 4 May 2004

Section D : Oral Questions
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister when he next plans to meet the Prime Minister and what issues he intends to raise. (S2F-859)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive's cabinet. (S2F-860)

3. Tommy Sheridan: To ask the First Minister what issues he will prioritise for discussion with the Prime Minister at his next meeting with him. (S2F-875)

4. Mr Kenneth Macintosh: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive plans to improve Scotland's tertiary education sector. (S2F-870)

5. Robert Brown: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive is addressing the health gap between rich and poor. (S2F-873)

6. Murdo Fraser: To ask the First Minister whether the Scottish Executive is satisfied with the growth of the Scottish economy. (S2F-857)
