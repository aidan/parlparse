BUSINESS BULLETIN 158 / 2000

Tuesday 24 October 2000

Section D - Oral questions selected for answer on 26 October 2000

 

 

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister what progress the Scottish Executive is making in meeting its key targets. (S1F-600)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he next intends to meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he intends to raise with him. (S1F-601)

3. Mrs Mary Mulligan: To ask the First Minister what steps will be taken by the Scottish Executive to mark the life and achievements of the late First Minister, Donald Dewar. (S1F-603)

4. Fergus Ewing: To ask the First Minister when there will be a determination as to the application for planning permission for a quarry at Lingerbay, Harris. (S1F-602)

5. Johann Lamont: To ask the First Minister what steps are being taken by the Scottish Executive in conjunction with Her Majesty’s Government to end child poverty. (S1F-610)

6. Phil Gallie: To ask the First Minister what discussions the Scottish Executive has had with colleagues in Her Majesty's Government with respect to the effects of the European Convention on Human Rights on Scottish law. (S1F-596)
