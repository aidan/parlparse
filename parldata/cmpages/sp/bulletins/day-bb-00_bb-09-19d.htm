BUSINESS BULLETIN 139 / 2000

Tuesday 19 September 2000

Section D - Oral questions selected for answer on 20 September 2000

 

 

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr Alex Salmond: To ask the First Minister when he last met the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues were discussed. (S1F-544)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister what issues were discussed at the most recent meeting of the Scottish Executive’s Cabinet. (S1F-543)

3. George Lyon: To ask the First Minister what contingency plans the Scottish Executive has made or intends to make to ensure that frontline services are protected in the event of future disruption to fuel supplies. (S1F-548)

4. Elaine Thomson: To ask the First Minister whether the Scottish Executive is taking action to ensure that Scotland’s science and research base is good enough to attract inward investment to Scotland. (S1F-549)

5. Michael Russell: To ask the First Minister whether there will be any increase or decrease in the number of Non-Departmental Public Bodies resulting from its legislative programme for the next parliamentary year. (S1F-542)

6. Mr Andy Kerr: To ask the First Minister what cross-cutting measures have been brought forward to address the impact of housing conditions and the environment on the health of Scotland’s people. (S1F-550)
