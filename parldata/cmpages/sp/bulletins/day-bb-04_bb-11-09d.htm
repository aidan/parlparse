Business Bulletin No. 161/2004

Tuesday 9 October 2004

Section D : Oral questions selected for answer on 11 November 2004
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Nicola Sturgeon: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Prime Minister and what issues will be discussed. (S2F-1182)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive’s Cabinet. (S2F-1183)

3. Tommy Sheridan: To ask the First Minister what issues are the Scottish Executive’s highest priority for discussion with the Prime Minister. (S2F-1199)

4. Sarah Boyack: To ask the First Minister what benefits the proposed rail links to Edinburgh and Glasgow airports will have for the tourist industry and economy. (S2F-1193)

5. Dennis Canavan: To ask the First Minister what progress has been made on the Fresh Talent Initiative. (S2F-1195)

6. Rob Gibson: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive will evaluate the effectiveness of the Convention of the Highlands and Islands. (S2F-1187)
