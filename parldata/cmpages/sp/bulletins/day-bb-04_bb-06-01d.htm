Business Bulletin No. 92 / 2004

Tuesday 1st June 2004

Section D : Oral questions selected for answer on Thursday 3rd June 2004
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister when he next plans to meet the Prime Minister and what issues he intends to raise. (S2F-903)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive's Cabinet. (S2F-914)

3. Alex Neil: To ask the First Minister whether the Scottish Executive plans to introduce legislation to amend the current law on rape. (S2F-911)

4. Mr John Home Robertson: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive is addressing pensioner poverty. (S2F-905)

5. Mike Pringle: To ask the First Minister how the increase in heroin use in Edinburgh is being addressed. (S2F-909)

6. Margo MacDonald: To ask the First Minister whether any additional emergency measures are planned to combat the advance of obesity amongst the general population and, in particular, children. (S2F-918)
