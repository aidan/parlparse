Business Bulletin No. 126/ 2004

Thursday 9 September 2004

Section A : Daily Business List
  

Meetings of Committees

All meetings take place in the Scottish Parliament, Holyrood, Edinburgh unless otherwise indicated.

Environment and Rural Development Committee

19th Meeting, 2004

The Committee will meet at 10.00 am in Committee Room 1

1. Item in private: The Committee will consider whether to take item 6 in private.

2. Declaration of interests: The new member of the Committee will be invited to declare any relevant interests.

3. Choice of Deputy Convener: The Committee will choose a new Deputy Convener.

4. Water Services etc. (Scotland) Bill: The Committee will take evidence at Stage 1 from—

Panel 1

Dr John Sawkins, Senior Lecturer in Economics, Heriot-Watt University;

Ian Jones, Chairman, Quayle Munro Holdings plc;

Alan Thomson, Chartered Institution of Water and Environmental Management;

Panel 2

Ian Duff, Chief Economist, Scottish Council for Development and Industry;

John Downie, Parliamentary Officer, Federation of Small Businesses in Scotland;

Bill Anderson, Campaigns Manager, Forum of Private Business, Scotland; and

Panel 3

Alan Barclay, General Manager – Port Dundas Whisky, Diageo;

Dr Ray Mountford, Commercial Development Manager, BP Grangemouth;

David Calder, Head of Manufacturing, Ciba Speciality Chemicals.

5. Subordinate legislation: The Committee will consider the following negative instruments—

the Waste Management Licensing Amendment (Scotland) Regulations 2004, (SSI 2004/275); and

the Environmental Protection (Restriction on Use of Lead Shot) (Scotland) Regulations 2004, (SSI 2004/289).

6. Research into Sustainable Development: The Committee will consider a draft report.

Finance Committee

21st Meeting, 2004

Thursday 9 September 2004

The Committee will meet at 10.00 am in Committee Room 2

1. Committee's Debate on Relocation of Public Sector Jobs: The Committee will discuss its forthcoming debate on its report into the Relocation of Public Sector Jobs.

2. Fire (Scotland) Bill: The Committee will take evidence on the Bill’s Financial Memorandum from—

Pat Watters, President of COSLA; Councillor Julie Sturrock, Dundee Council; Stephen Hunter, Firemaster, Tayside Fire Brigade; Eileen Baird, Strathclyde Fire Brigade; and Barbara Lindsay, Corporate Manager, COSLA.

3. Water Services (Scotland) Bill: The Committee will take evidence on the Bill’s Financial Memorandum from—

Dr Jon Hargreaves, Chief Executive; Douglas Millican, Finance Director; and Ian McMillan, Non-Executive Board Member, Scottish Water; and

Alan Sutherland, Water Industry Commissioner; and Dr John Simpson, Director of Cost and Performance, Office of the Water Industry Commissioner.

4. Budget Process 2005-06: The Committee will consider a paper by the Budget Adviser on trend series data provided by the Scottish Executive.

5. Item in private: The Committee will decide whether to consider the appointment of a Budget Adviser in private at its next meeting.

Health Committee

19th Meeting, 2004

The Committee will meet at 2.00 pm in Committee Room 1

1. Subordinate legislation: The Committee will consider the following negative instrument—

the National Health Service (Transfer of Property between Health Boards) (Scotland) (No. 2) Regulations 2004, (SSI 2004/285)

and may take evidence from—

Mike Baxter, Directorate of Performance Management and Finance, Scottish Executive Health Department;

David Hastie, Directorate of Performance Management and Finance, Scottish Executive Health Department; and

Jan Marshall, Legal and Parliamentary Services, OSSE.

2. Workforce Planning inquiry: The Committee will take evidence from—

Panel 1

Professor Sir John Temple, Chair, Short Life Working Group on ‘Securing Future Practice: Shaping the New Medical Workforce for Scotland’;

Dr Robin Cairncross, Senior Medical Officer, Scottish Executive Health Department; and

Panel 2

Professor David Kerr, Chair, Advisory Group on the National Framework for Service Change;

Derek Feeley, Head of National Planning, Scottish Executive Health Department.

Justice 1 Committee

27th Meeting, 2004

The Committee will meet at 2.00 pm in Committee Room 4

1. Emergency Workers (Scotland) Bill (in private): The Committee will consider a draft Stage 1 report.

Justice 2 Committee

24th Meeting, 2004

The Committee will meet at 2.00 pm in Committee Room 2

1. Item in private: The Committee will decide whether to take item 4 in private.

2. Prisoner Escort and Court Custody Services Contract: The Committee will consider a paper by the Clerk.

3. Fire (Scotland) Bill: The Committee will take evidence on the general principles of the Bill from—

Ian Snedden, Head of Fire Services Division, Jill Clark, Bill Team Leader, Robert Marshall, Solicitor, Scottish Executive and Brian McKenzie, Assistant Inspector of Fire Services.

4. Fire (Scotland) Bill: The Committee will consider written evidence received and consider who to invite to give oral evidence on the Bill at Stage 1.
