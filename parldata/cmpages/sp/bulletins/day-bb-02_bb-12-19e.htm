Business Bulletin No. 188/2002

Thursday 19 December 2002

Section E : Written questions lodged on 18 December 2002

  

Questions marked with a triangle () are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S1W-32719 Stewart Stevenson: To ask the Scottish Executive how many passenger rail journeys are undertaken each year.

S1W-32720 Stewart Stevenson: To ask the Scottish Executive what proportion of passenger rail journeys undertaken do not exceed ten minutes.
