Business Bulletin No. 58 / 2004

Tuesday 30th March 2004

Section D : Oral questions selected for answer on Thursday 1st April 2004
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive's cabinet. (S2F-784)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he next plans to meet the Prime Minister and what issues he intends to discuss. (S2F-787)

3. Richard Baker: To ask the First Minister what action is being taken to ensure that the activities of gangmasters are properly regulated. (S2F-795)

4. Shona Robison: To ask the First Minister whether people who contracted hepatitis C through contaminated blood products will have to waive their right to legal action in order to receive an ex gratia payment. (S2F-803)

5. George Lyon: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive will ensure that the new EU constitution will allow proper regional fisheries management. (S2F-800)

6. Janis Hughes: To ask the First Minister whether there will be any further restrictions on smoking in light of responses to the consultation on smoking in public places. (S2F-799)
