11th Meeting, 2002

The Committee will meet at 2.00 pm in Debating Chamber, Assembly Hall, The Mound, Edinburgh.

1. Items in Private: The Committee will consider whether to take agenda items 8 and 9 in private.

2. Committee Work Programme: The Committee will consider its proposed work programme for the period September to December 2002.

6. Meeting with Public Accounts/Audit Committees of the United Kingdom Parliament and the Devolved Institutions: The Committee will consider a draft submission to the Conveners’ Liaison Group regarding visits to the Public Accounts/Audit Committees of the UK Parliament and the Devolved Institutions.
