Section E – Written questions lodged on 28 June 2010

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

Questions in which a member has indicated a declarable interest are marked with an "R".

S3W-34905 Cathy Peattie: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will list the freight facilities grantsawardedin the last five years showing the amount of traffic that it was anticipated would switch from road to rail or water as a result of the work being funded.

S3W-34906 Margaret Smith: To ask the Scottish Executive how many schools are using temporary accommodation to teach in, broken down by local authority.

S3W-34907 Margaret Smith: To ask the Scottish Executive how many children excluded from mainstream education for behavioural issues in academic year 2009-10 received counselling or assistance following exclusion to specifically address this behaviour, expressed as a percentage.

S3W-34908 Margaret Smith: To ask the Scottish Executive what action it is taking to encourage local authorities to borrow from the £10 million made available for early retirement packages for teachers.

S3W-34909 Margaret Smith: To ask the Scottish Executive what action it is taking to reduce the number of unemployed post-probation teachers.

S3W-34910 Margaret Smith: To ask the Scottish Executive how much funding in the current financial year each local authority is dedicating to the development and implementation of the Curriculum for Excellence.

S3W-34911 Margaret Smith: To ask the Scottish Executive how many local authorities will have schools offering the language baccalaureate in the academic year 2010-11.

S3W-34912 Margaret Smith: To ask the Scottish Executive how many local authorities will have schools offering the science baccalaureate in the academic year 2010-11.

S3W-34913 Margaret Smith: To ask the Scottish Executive how many pupils were presented for the language baccalaureate in the academic year 2009-10.

S3W-34914 Margaret Smith: To ask the Scottish Executive how many pupils were presented for the science baccalaureate in the academic year 2009-10.
