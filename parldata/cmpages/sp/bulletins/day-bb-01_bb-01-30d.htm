BUSINESS BULLETIN 18 / 2001

Tuesday 30 January 2001

Section D - Oral questions selected for answer on 1 February 2001

 

 

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister when he last met the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues were discussed. (S1F-814)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when the Scottish Executive’s Cabinet will next meet and what issues will be discussed. (S1F-813)

3. Johann Lamont: To ask the First Minister what initiatives the Scottish Executive is promoting with the aim of eradicating domestic violence. (S1F-817)

4. Nick Johnston: To ask the First Minister what measures the Scottish Executive will take to ensure that lives are not put at risk through the implementation of Acute Services reviews. (S1F-807)

5. Fergus Ewing: To ask the First Minister what pledge or undertaking the Scottish Executive will make with regard to crofters and hill farmers in Less Favoured Areas. (S1F-827)

6. Ian Jenkins: To ask the First Minister what plans the Scottish Executive has to address the backlog of repairs and maintenance of school buildings. (S1F-823)
