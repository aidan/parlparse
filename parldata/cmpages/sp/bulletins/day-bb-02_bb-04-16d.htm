Business Bulletin No. 59/2002

Tuesday 16 April 2002

Section D : Oral questions selected for answer on 18 April 2002
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he plans to raise. (S1F-1821)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Prime Minister and what issues he intends to raise. (S1F-1819)

3. Bill Butler: To ask the First Minister what benefit will accrue to Scotland from the Chancellor of the Exchequer's budget. (S1F-1829)

4. Bruce Crawford: To ask the First Minister what support the Scottish Executive intends to make available to the workers and communities affected by the closure of the Longannet deep coal mine. (S1F-1811)

5. Mr Mike Rumbles: To ask the First Minister what action the Scottish Executive is taking to ensure that no jobs are lost following the collapse of the proposed SPL-TV. (S1F-1831)

6. Janis Hughes: To ask the First Minister what social and economic benefits the concordat between the Scottish Executive and the Scottish Trades Union Congress will bring.(S1F-1828)
