Tuesday 23 January 2001

Section A – Daily Business List

 

 

Meetings of Committees

All meetings take place in Committee Chambers, Edinburgh unless otherwise indicated.

Public Petitions Committee

1st Meeting, 2001

The Committee will meet at 10.00 am in Committee Room 1

1. Declaration of Interests: The Committee will invite new members to declare any relevant interests.

2. Appointment of Deputy Convener: The Committee will choose a Deputy Convener.

3. New Petitions: Consideration of new petitions.

4. Current Petitions: Consideration of current petitions.

5. Inadmissible Petitions: The Committee will discuss a paper listing those new petitions which are considered to be inadmissible.

6. Fraudulent Petition signatures: The Committee will consider a paper on the action to be taken where signatures attached to a petition are found to be fraudulent.

7. Convener's Report.
