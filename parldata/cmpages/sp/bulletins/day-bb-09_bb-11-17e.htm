Business Bulletin No. 169/2009: Tuesday 17 November 2009
Contents An A B C D E F G H I J

Section E – Written questions lodged on 16 November 2009

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

Questions in which a member has indicated a declarable interest are marked with an "R".

S3W-28953 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S3W-27919 by Alex Neil on 29 October 2009, when the balance of the money paid to the Scottish-Islamic Foundation was repaid.

S3W-28954 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S3W-27919 by Alex Neil on 29 October 2009, for what purposes the £200,000 was awarded to the Scottish-Islamic Foundation; against what criteria the decision was made, and by which minister.

S3W-28955 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S3W-27919 by Alex Neil on 29 October 2009, whether ministers asked that money be repaid by the Scottish-Islamic Foundation.

S3W-28956 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive what correspondence there was between the Scottish-Islamic Foundation and ministers or their officials regarding the repayment of money to the Scottish Government.

S3W-28957 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S3W-27919 by Alex Neil on 29 October 2009, what targets or objectives agreed between it and the Scottish-Islamic Foundation as conditions of funding when the £200,000 was awarded will not be met following the repayment of £128,000.

S3W-28958 Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S3W-27919 by Alex Neil on 29 October 2009, whether further applications for funding have been received from the Scottish-Islamic Foundation.

S3W-28959 Jackie Baillie: To ask the Scottish Executive whether Netcare staff at the Scottish Regional Treatment Centre at Stracathro Hospital will be transferred to the NHS under the transfer of undertakings (protection of employment) regulations (TUPE) following the recent announcement about its return to the NHS.

S3W-28960 Jackie Baillie: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will ensure that all 54 Netcare staff at the Scottish Regional Treatment Centre at Stracathro Hospital are redeployed by NHS Tayside following the recent announcement about its return to the NHS.

S3W-28961 Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive when the first meeting of the Scottish Policing Board will take place.

S3W-28962 Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive how often the Scottish Policing Board will meet following its first meeting.

S3W-28963 Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive whether the Scottish Policing Board will have the power to set (a) policies, (b) strategies and (c) standards for police forces.

S3W-28964 Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive whether all police authorities and joint boards will have representation on the Scottish Policing Board.

S3W-28965 Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive what consideration it has given to concerns that a Scottish Policing Board would infringe on the legal functions of the tripartite arrangement.

S3W-28966 Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive how many individuals have disputed information from a criminal record check carried out by Disclosure Scotland since 2002, broken down by whether the dispute was based on (a) identity, (b) accuracy or (c) irrelevancy.

S3W-28967 Andy Kerr: To ask the Scottish Executive what reports are available to show what work the Scottish Futures Trust has done to identify continued financing for the Glasgow Airport Rail Link.

S3W-28968 Rob Gibson: To ask the Scottish Executive what details it can provide of meetings since 18 September 2009 between the head of the GM section of the Food Standards Agency and the head of abc, the umbrella group for the agricultural biotechnology industry.

S3W-28969 Rob Gibson: To ask the Scottish Executive what details it can provide of meetings since 18 September 2009 between employees of the Food Standards Agency and members of abc, the umbrella group for the agricultural biotechnology industry.

S3W-28970 Rob Gibson: To ask the Scottish Executive in which years since it became operational the Kessock Bridge has been resurfaced.

S3W-28971 Rob Gibson: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans are in hand to resurface the Kessock Bridge.

S3W-28972 Rob Gibson: To ask the Scottish Executive in which years since it became operational the Cromarty Bridge has been resurfaced.

S3W-28973 Rob Gibson: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans are in hand to resurface the Cromarty Bridge.

S3W-28974 Rob Gibson: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans are in hand for major bridges on the Highland trunk road network, other than the Kessock Bridge and Cromarty Bridge, to be resurfaced.

S3W-28975 Michael Matheson: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it plans to alter the qualification criteria for the Blue Badge scheme and whether it considers that local authorities should have discretion in the provision of such badges.

S3W-28976 Lewis Macdonald: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S3W-28108 by Jim Mather on 30 October 2009, in what respect it considers that publishing details of registrations for a public prize competition for the award of public money may lead to a breach of confidentiality.

S3W-28977 Patrick Harvie: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S3W-13639 by Kenny MacAskill on 9 June 2008, when it intends to review the Family Law (Scotland) Act 2006.

S3W-28978 Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will publish the list of lead applicants, including details of project titles and towns, who submitted unsuccessful applications to the second tranche of the Town Centre Regeneration Fund.

S3W-28979Δ Aileen Campbell: To ask the Scottish Executive what progress has been made in taking forward the intention, announced at the Rural Gathering on 25 September 2009, to pursue transfer of the measure dealing with supporting community facilities from the Rural Priorities scheme to the LEADER programme and increase the potential LEADER intervention rate.

S3W-28980 Johann Lamont: To ask the Scottish Executive what its position is on the recommendation of the Review of Concessionary Fares not to extend the scheme to people on the lower rate of mobility benefit.

S3W-28981 Johann Lamont: To ask the Scottish Executive what the estimated cost is of extending the concessionary fares scheme to people on the lower rate of mobility benefit.

S3W-28982 Johann Lamont: To ask the Scottish Executive which local authorities operate a waiting list for access to direct payments.

S3W-28983 Johann Lamont: To ask the Scottish Executive how long on average a disabled person is on a waiting list for direct payments, broken down by local authority.

S3W-28984 Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S3W-28500 by Shona Robison on 6 November 2009, what the outcome was of considerations on human trafficking during the construction phase by the multi-agency group, led by Strathclyde Police, covering all aspects of security for the 2014 Commonwealth Games.

S3W-28985 Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive what the remit is of the multi-agency group, led by Strathclyde Police, covering all aspects of security for the 2014 Commonwealth Games.

S3W-28986 Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive when the multi-agency group, led by Strathclyde Police, covering all aspects of security for the 2014 Commonwealth Games last met.

S3W-28987 Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive how often the multi-agency group, led by Strathclyde Police, covering all aspects of security for the 2014 Commonwealth Games meets.

S3W-28988 Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive how much has been spent by the multi-agency group, led by Strathclyde Police, covering all aspects of security for the 2014 Commonwealth Games on identifying victims of human trafficking for sexual exploitation during the construction phase.

S3W-28989 Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive what it plans to do to increase conviction rates for human trafficking, given that there are no current convictions for trafficking in Scotland.

S3W-28990 Johann Lamont: To ask the Scottish Executive what steps have been taken to disseminate to local authorities the findings of the Prevention of Homelessness Partnership Evaluation in south-west Glasgow.

S3W-28992 Jackie Baillie: To ask the Scottish Executive what the (a) capital and (b) revenue cost is of the new patient management system.

S3W-28996 Jackie Baillie: To ask the Scottish Executive whether the successful bidder for the new patient management system was the lowest cost tender.

S3W-28997 Jackie Baillie: To ask the Scottish Executive where the successful bidder for the new patient management system is (a) headquartered and (b) has branches operating in Scotland.

S3W-29004 Jackie Baillie: To ask the Scottish Executive who assessed and determined the bids for the new Southern General Hospital.

S3W-29014 Jackie Baillie: To ask the Scottish Executive whether the successful bid to build the new Southern General Hospital was received before the deadline for receipt of tenders.

S3W-29015 Jackie Baillie: To ask the Scottish Executive how it is supporting jobs in the Scottish construction industry by awarding the contract for the building of the new Southern General Hospital to a company headquartered outwith Scotland.
