BUSINESS BULLETIN 5 / 2001

Thursday 11 January 2001

Section E - Written questions lodged on 10 January 2001

 

 

S1W-12372 Brian Adam: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it plans to seek urgently a derogation from the ban on fishmeal in sheep and cattle feeds.

S1W-12373 Fergus Ewing: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it plans to change the working title of the First Minister to "Prime Minister".

S1W-12374 Karen Gillon: To ask the Scottish Executive when it expects a decision to be made on the extension of rail services to Larkhall.

S1W-12375 Karen Gillon: To ask the Scottish Executive what support is being provided to the workforce of DAKS Simpson in Larkhall following recent redundancies.

S1W-12376 Christine Grahame: To ask the Scottish Executive how many claimants who are currently receiving funds from the Independent Living Fund also pay part of their own care costs; what this figure is as a percentage of the total number of claimants receiving funds from the fund in Scotland, and what the total amount of care costs contributed by such claimants is.

S1W-12377 Alasdair Morgan: To ask the Scottish Executive what percentage of the motorway network is surfaced with porous asphalt.

S1W-12378 Alasdair Morgan: To ask the Scottish Executive how many fatal accident inquiries have been held in each of the last ten years.

S1W-12379 Alasdair Morgan: To ask the Scottish Executive what the current establishment is of the procurator fiscal service, how many vacancies there are and in what grades.

S1W-12380 Mr Alex Salmond: To ask the Scottish Executive when the Deputy Minister for Rural Affairs will reply to my letter of 21 November 2000 regarding third country imports of fish.

S1W-12381 Donald Gorrie: To ask the Scottish Executive what action it is taking to bring together into one network the currently separate networks of the Public Library Network, Learning Direct Scotland, the National Grid for Learning, the Joint Academic Network and the further education colleges network.

S1W-12382 Fergus Ewing: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will place in the Scottish Parliament Information Centre a copy of the negotiating position used by the Deputy Minister for Rural Development at the recent EU Fisheries Council and what the reasons are for its position on this matter.
