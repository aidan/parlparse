BUSINESS BULLETIN 34/1999

Thursday 15 July 1999
 

Announcements

 

Enterprise and Lifelong Learning Committee Meeting Cancelled

 

In the light of recent developments, the Enterprise and Lifelong Learning Committee of the Scottish Parliament has decided not to proceed with its meeting on the Kvaerner Govan situation, planned for 12.00 noon in Committee Room 1 on Friday 16 July.

 

The Minister for Enterprise and Lifelong Learning, Henry McLeish MSP, has agreed to keep the Committee fully informed of developments over the next few days and weeks.
