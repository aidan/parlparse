BUSINESS BULLETIN 101 / 2001

Tuesday 19 June 2001

Section D - Oral questions selected for answer on 21 June 2001

 

 

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive’s Cabinet. (S1F-1159)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Prime Minister and what issues he will raise. (S1F-1157)

3. Des McNulty: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive will ensure that the needs of commuters are balanced with the need to reduce traffic congestion in urban areas. (S1F-1163)

4. Nicola Sturgeon: To ask the First Minister when the Scottish Executive expects tobacco advertising to be banned in Scotland. (S1F-1155)

5. Mary Scanlon: To ask the First Minister whether an inquiry will be carried out into the use of children’s bones without parental consent for research into nuclear radiation in the 1960s. (S1F-1161)

6. Mr Duncan McNeil: To ask the First Minister what further progress is being made in reducing disruptive behaviour in schools and creating a school environment where all children can pursue their education. (S1F-1164)
