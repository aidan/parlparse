Enterprise and Lifelong Learning Committee

3rd Meeting, 2000

The Committee will meet at 1.30 pm in the Chamber, Assembly Hall, the Mound, Edinburgh

1. Petrol Price Inquiry - Witnesses: The Committee will consider the issue of differential petrol pricing in remote rural areas of Scotland and will take evidence from the following witnesses:

The Highlands and Islands Hydrocarbon Action Group: Cr Alison Magee, Ken McCorquodale, Cr Donald Maclean, Harry Miller

Arran Council for Voluntary Service: Jim Lees, Sheila Johnstone, Mike Lunan

Office of Fair Trading: Nancy Race, Alan Williams

Shell UK: Simon Graham, Billy Laing

BP Oil UK Limited: John Mumford, Ian Thomson, Steve Moore

Esso Petroleum Company, Limited: Ian Wells, Brian McBride, Bill Keilor

The Petrol Retailers Association: Ray Holloway

 

2. Petrol Price Inquiry - Discussion: The Committee will discuss the issues arising from the evidence taken and consider further action required.
