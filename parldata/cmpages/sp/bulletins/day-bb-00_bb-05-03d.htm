BUSINESS BULLETIN 73 / 2000

Wednesday 3 May 2000

Section D - Oral questions selected for answer on 4 May 2000

 

 

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr Alex Salmond: To ask the First Minister what are the Scottish Executive’s current main priorities. (S1F-284)

 

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister whether he has any plans to reshuffle his cabinet. (S1F-292)

 

3. Des McNulty: To ask the First Minister what plans the Scottish Executive has to mark the anniversary of the first Scottish Parliamentary elections. (S1F-296)

 

4. Richard Lochhead: To ask the First Minister, further to the answer to question S1W-4291 by Sarah Boyack on 26 April 2000, what assistance the Scottish Executive is considering making available to pensioners and low income households to assist them in paying their water bills. (S1F-289)

 

5. Nora Radcliffe: To ask the First Minister what steps the Scottish Executive plans to take to tackle the effects of the flooding in the north and east of Scotland last week. (S1F-288)

 

6. Elaine Thomson: To ask the First Minister what progress has been made towards achieving the commitments in the Partnership for Scotland during the first year of the Scottish Parliament. (S1F-298)
