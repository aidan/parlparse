Business Bulletin No. 139/2004

Tuesday 28 September 2004

Section D : Oral Questions seected for answer on Thursday 30 September 2004

  

First Minister’s Question Time

1. Nicola Sturgeon: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Prime Minister and what issues will be discussed. (S2F-1097)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive’s Cabinet. (S2F-1088)

3. Tommy Sheridan: To ask the First Minister how many Scots live in relative income poverty and what percentage of the total population this represents. (S2F-1108)

4. Rhona Brankin: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive’s spending review will help further and higher education colleges and universities meet their forthcoming challenges. (S2F-1095)

5. Alex Neil: To ask the First Minister what the up-to-date position is on discussions with the Home Secretary in respect of devolving some immigration powers from Her Majesty’s Government to the Scottish Executive. (S2F-1098)

6. John Farquhar Munro: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive will protect and promote the status of Gaelic. (S2F-1096)
