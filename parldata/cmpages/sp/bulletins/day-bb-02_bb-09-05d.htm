Business Bulletin No. 122/2002

Thursday 5 September 2002

Section D : Oral Questions selected for answer on 12 September 2002
  

Question Time

1. Maureen Macmillan: To ask the Scottish Executive what discussions it has had with sportscotland regarding support for skateboarding. (S1O-5516)

2. Mary Scanlon: To ask the Scottish Executive what is being done to address the 36% rise in chlamydia diagnosis in females in 2001. (S1O-5537)

3. Ms Margo MacDonald: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will introduce guidance for local authorities and NHS boards entering into any form of Public Private Partnership to ensure that charges levied by contractors are set at a level which takes account of social responsibility as well as solely commercial considerations. (S1O-5519)

4. Mr Kenneth Macintosh: To ask the Scottish Executive what progress is being made in introducing the Supporting People regime. (S1O-5550)

5. Sarah Boyack: To ask the Scottish Executive how it is supporting homeless women who have experienced domestic abuse. (S1O-5547)

6. Mr Kenneth Gibson: To ask the Scottish Executive what steps it is taking to reduce under-age drinking. (S1O-5549)

7. Scott Barrie: To ask the Scottish Executive what measures it is proposing to improve the protection of vulnerable children. (S1O-5540)

8. Alex Johnstone: To ask the Scottish Executive what action it can take to prevent local authorities reducing the scope for parental choice in selecting schools in rural areas. (S1O-5517)

9. Mr Jamie Stone: To ask the Scottish Executive how Highland NHS Board will ensure that the same level of accident and emergency services previously provided at Thurso Hospital is provided in the future. (S1O-5548)

10. Ms Sandra White: To ask the Scottish Executive what discussions it has had with Glasgow City Council concerning the provision of social work services in the city. (S1O-5535)

11. Cathy Peattie: To ask the Scottish Executive when it will publish proposals for legislative changes to tighten up the controls on the spreading of abattoir waste on agricultural land. (S1O-5551)

12. Stewart Stevenson: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it has for the future development of seaports, harbours and jetties. (S1O-5505)

13. Johann Lamont: To ask the Scottish Executive what impact its plans to prioritise mental health are having on the provision and delivery of services. (S1O-5553)

14. Lord James Douglas-Hamilton: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it has to combat knife crime. (S1O-5513)

15. Nora Radcliffe: To ask the Scottish Executive what progress is being made on drawing together the national waste strategy. (S1O-5527)

16. Mr Lloyd Quinan: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it has begun to put in place any contingency plans to protect the public should there be a national strike in the fire service. (S1O-5534)

17. Dennis Canavan: To ask the Scottish Executive what consultations it has had with the police about the possible use of baton rounds. (S1O-5515)

18. Christine Grahame: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it has any concerns regarding the provision of public services in the Scottish Borders. (S1O-5530)

19. Rhona Brankin: To ask the Scottish Executive whether preparations for the introduction of off-peak concessionary travel for older people are on schedule. (S1O-5525)

20. Mr Andrew Welsh: To ask the Scottish Executive what progress has been made in cutting NHS waiting lists. (S1O-5542)

21. Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it has any plans to undertake a review of NHS dental fees. (S1O-5538)

22. Dr Sylvia Jackson: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it has any plans to introduce further measures to assist key public sector workers to gain access to the housing market in pressured rural areas. (S1O-5552)

23. David Mundell: To ask the Scottish Executive how it will respond to the reports of the inquiries into the foot-and-mouth disease outbreak. (S1O-5504)

24. Alex Fergusson: To ask the Scottish Executive what its response is to the report of the Performance Audit Group on the performance of Amey Highways Ltd. (S1O-5509)

25. Brian Fitzpatrick: To ask the Scottish Executive what benefits Eco-schools will bring. (S1O-5556)

26. Tavish Scott: To ask the Scottish Executive what proposals it has to develop the inshore fishing industry. (S1O-5508)

27. Mr Jamie McGrigor: To ask the Scottish Executive what progress has been made with the management of fishing of deepwater species. (S1O-5512)

28. Michael Russell: To ask the Scottish Executive when it will report on primary class size numbers as of August 2002. (S1O-5510)

29. Bill Aitken: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it has a strategy to reduce the number of drug abusers who are currently reliant upon methadone. (S1O-5555)

30. Bruce Crawford: To ask the Scottish Executive when it will make an announcement about new GM crop trials. (S1O-5514)
