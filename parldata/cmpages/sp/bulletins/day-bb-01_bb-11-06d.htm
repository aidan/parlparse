Business Bulletin No. 159/2001

Tuesday 6 November 2001

Section D : Oral Questions
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister what issues were discussed at the last meeting of the Cabinet. (S1F-1351)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister whether he has any plans to reshuffle the Cabinet. (S1F-1350)

3. Pauline McNeill: To ask the First Minister what evidence the Scottish Executive has of any increase in the number of racial attacks since 11 September 2001. (S1F-1355)

4. John Farquhar Munro: To ask the First Minister what progress is being made in restoring animal exports to Europe. (S1F-1363)

5. Mr Lloyd Quinan: To ask the First Minister what plans the Scottish Executive has to introduce additional live vaccines for human immunisation. (S1F-1357)

6. Paul Martin: To ask the First Minister what measures are being taken to support manufacturing industry. (S1F-1356)
