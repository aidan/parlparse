Section E – Written questions lodged on 2 June 2010

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

Questions in which a member has indicated a declarable interest are marked with an "R".

S3W-34019 Dr Richard Simpson: To ask the Scottish Executive how many acute occupied bed days in NHS Forth Valley were directly connected to a diagnosis of assault by sharp object in each of the last five years for which information is available.
