Business Bulletin No. 146/2002

Wednesday 9 October 2002

Section E : Written questions lodged on 8 October 2002

  

Questions marked with a triangle () are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S1W-30302 Mr Gil Paterson: To ask the Scottish Executive what research has been carried out into the effects on children of experiencing domestic abuse.

S1W-30303 Mr Gil Paterson: To ask the Scottish Executive what research has been carried out into links between experiencing domestic abuse and mental illness in young people.
