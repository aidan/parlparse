Business Bulletin No. 173/2001

Monday 26 November 2001

Section A : Daily Business List
  

All meetings take place in Committee Chambers, Edinburgh unless otherwise indicated.

Transport and the Environment Committee

30th Meeting, 2001

The Committee will meet at 10.30 am in Committee Room 1

1. Water Industry (Scotland) Bill (in private): The Committee will consider a second draft Stage 1 Report on the Water Industry (Scotland) Bill.

2. Lines of Questioning (in private): The Committee will consider possible lines of questioning for witnesses on its aquaculture inquiry.

3. Aquaculture Inquiry: The Committee will take evidence as part of its inquiry into aquaculture from—

Jamie Lindsay, Scottish Quality Salmon

David Sandison, Shetland Salmon Farmers’ Association

Bruce Mainland, Orkney Salmon Company

Michael Cuncliffe and Ian Pritchard, The Crown Estate

Colin Wishart, Highland Council

Councillor Bill Manson and Willie Shannon, Shetland Islands Council

Patricia Henton and Andy Rosie, Scottish Environment Protection Agency

Dominic Counsell and Matt Dalkin, Scottish Natural Heritage.

4. Public Petition: The Committee will consider Petition PE411 by the Scottish Co-operative and Mutual Forum on the mutualisation of the Scottish Water Authority.

5. Public Petition: The Committee will consider a report by the reporter on Petition PE327 by the Blairingone and Saline Action Group on organic waste spread on land.
