Business Bulletin No. 119 / 2003

Tuesday 16th September 2003

Section A : Daily Business List
  

Meetings of Committees

All meetings take place in Committee Chambers, Edinburgh unless otherwise indicated.

Audit Committee

4th Meeting, 2003

The Committee will meet at 9.30 am in the Chamber, Assembly Hall, The Mound, Edinburgh

1. Item in private: The Committee will consider lines of questioning for the witnesses at agenda item 4.

2. Items in private: The Committee will consider whether to hold agenda items 5, 6 and 7 in private.

3. Scottish Further Education Funding Council: The Committee will receive a briefing from the Auditor General for Scotland on his report entitled ‘Scottish Further Education Funding Council – Performance management of the further education sector in Scotland’ (AGS/2003/7).

4. Inquiry into AGS Report on Individual Learning Accounts: The Committee will take evidence from –

Mr Eddie Frizzell, Head of Enterprise, Transport and Lifelong Learning Department; Ms Laura Barjonas, Team Leader, Funding for Learners Division; Mr David Stewart, Head of Finance Administration and Pay Policy, Finance and Central Services Department, Scottish Executive

Mr Alan Sinclair, Director of Learning and Skills; Mr Ian Carmichael, Director of Finance; Ms Fiona Stewart, Senior Project Executive, Skills Development Department, Scottish Enterprise

Mr Sandy Cumming, Chief Executive; Mr Alex Paterson, Director of Developing Skills; Mrs Sandra Dunbar, Head of Audit and Compliance, Highlands and Islands Enterprise

Mr Frank Pignatelli, Chief Executive, Scottish University for Industry.

5. Individual Learning Accounts: The Committee will consider the evidence taken at agenda item 4.

6. Scottish Further Education Funding Council: The Committee will consider its approach to the report by the Auditor General for Scotland entitled ‘Scottish Further Education Funding Council – Performance management of the further education sector in Scotland’ (AGS/2003/7).

7. GP Prescribing: The Committee will consider arrangements for its inquiry into the report by the Auditor General for Scotland on GP prescribing entitled ‘Supporting prescribing in general practice – a progress report’ (AGS/2003/5).

Finance Committee

6th Meeting, 2003

The Committee will meet at 10.00 am in Committee Room 2

1. Budget process 2004-05: The Committee will undertake preliminary consideration of the draft budget for 2004-05. The Committee will also discuss its draft guidance for subject Committees’ stage two reports and two papers by the Budget Adviser.

2. Budget process 2004-05: The Committee will consider a paper by the Clerk outlining the proposals for the Committee’s consideration of the 2004-05 budget.

3. Consideration of Draft Reports: The Committee will consider the issue of discussing draft reports in private or public.

4. Vulnerable Witnesses (Scotland) Bill: The Committee will take evidence on the Financial Memorandum of the bill from-

Barbara Brown, Head of Civil Justice and Evidence Branch; Lesley Napier, Head of Bill Team, and Merlin Kemp, member of Bill Team, Scottish Executive.

Lindsey Anderson, Principal Depute, Policy Group, and Stephen Woodhouse, Finance, Crown Office.

5. Vulnerable Witnesses (Scotland) Bill: The Committee will discuss the evidence it has taken on the Financial Memorandum of the bill, in advance of its report to the Justice Two Committee.

6. Budget process 2004-05: The Committee will consider an issues paper setting out the arrangements for the Committee’s external meeting on 10 November.
