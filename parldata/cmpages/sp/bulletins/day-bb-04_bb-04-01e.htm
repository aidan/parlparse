Business Bulletin No. 60 / 2004

Thursday 1st April 2004

Section E : Written questions lodged on Wednesday 31st March 2004
  

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S2W-7283 Tricia Marwick: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will take account of the report and recommendations of the Council of Europe in respect of the United Kingdom's application of the European Charter for Regional or Minority Languages during the final drafting of its Gaelic Language Bill.

*The member has provided the following Gaelic translation:

A dh' fhaighneachd de Riaghaltas na h-Alba  am beachdaich e air aithisg agus molaidhean Comhairle na h-Eòrpa air cur-an-gnìomh Cairt Eòrpach nam Mion-chànanan nuair a bhios e a' sgrìobhadh an dreachd mu dheireadh den Bhile Ghàidhlig aige.*

S2W-7284 Tricia Marwick: To ask the Scottish Executive what specific actions it is taking to ensure that it complies "as a matter of priority", as requested by the Council of Europe, with the recommendations made by the council in respect of the United Kingdom's application of the European Charter for Regional or Minority Languages.

*The member has provided the following Gaelic translation:

A dh' fhaighneachd de Riaghaltas na h-Alba dè na ceumannan sònraichte agus a tha e a' gabhail gus dèanamh cinnteach gun gabh e ri molaidhean Comhairle na h-Eòrpa a thaobh cur-an-gnìomh Cairt Eòrpach nam Mion-chànanan mar "chùis prìomhachais", mar a chaidh iarraidh leis a' chomhairle.*

S2W-7285 Tricia Marwick: To ask the Scottish Executive what its response is to the statement in the report by the Council of Europe on the United Kingdom's application of the European Charter for Regional or Minority Languages that "there appears to be less emphasis on minority language policy on the part of the Scottish Executive" compared with the position in Wales.

*The member has provided the following Gaelic translation:

A dh' fhaighneachd de Riaghaltas na h-Alba dè an fhreagairt a th' aige dhan abairt anns an aithisg le Comhairle na h-Eòrpa air cur-an-gnìomh Cairt Eòrpach nam Mion-chànanan anns an Rìoghachd Aonaichte gu bheil e "coltach nach eil Riaghaltas na h-Alba a' cur an uiread de chuideam air poileasaidh mhion-chànanan"  an taca ris an t-suidheachadh anns a' Chuimrigh.*

S2W-7286 Tricia Marwick: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will investigate why it does not fully comply with its obligations under the European Charter for Regional or Minority Languages and publish the results of such an investigation.

*The member has provided the following Gaelic translation:

A dh' fhaighneachd de Riaghaltas na h-Alba an dèan e sgrùdadh air carson nach eil e a' coilionadh a dhleastanasan fo Chairt Eòrpach nam Mion-chànanan gu h-iomlan agus am foillsich e toraidhean an rannsachaidh seo.*

S2W-7287 Tricia Marwick: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will make a public commitment to comply fully with its obligations under the European Charter for Regional or Minority Languages.

*The member has provided the following Gaelic translation:

A dh' fhaighneachd de Riaghaltas na h-Alba an ainmich e gu poblach gun coilion e gu h-iomlan a dhleastanasan fo Chairt Eòrpach nam Mion-chànanan.*
