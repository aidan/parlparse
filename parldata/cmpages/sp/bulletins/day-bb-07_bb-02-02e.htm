Business Bulletin No. 20/2007: Friday 2 February 2007
Contents An A B C D E F G H I J

Section E – Written questions lodged on 1 February 2007

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S2W-31577 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive what action it is taking in conjunction with local authorities to tackle climate change.

S2W-31586 Shiona Baird: To ask the Scottish Executive how much it has invested in social enterprises in each of the last five years.

S2W-31587 Shiona Baird: To ask the Scottish Executive how much Scottish Enterprise has invested in social enterprises in each of the last five years.

S2W-31588 Shiona Baird: To ask the Scottish Executive how much Highlands and Islands Enterprise has invested in social enterprises in each of the last five years.

S2W-31589 Shiona Baird: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it has any plans for new funding for social enterprises, analogous to the Community Asset Fund announced by the Chancellor of the Exchequer in December 2006.

S2W-31590 Rob Gibson: To ask the Scottish Executive how many managers have left their posts in each department at each airport controlled by Highlands and Islands Airports Ltd in each year since 2003.

S2W-31591 Rob Gibson: To ask the Scottish Executive how many air traffic controllers have left posts in each airport controlled by Highlands and Islands Airports Ltd in the last 12 months and how many have been replaced.

S2W-31592 Mr Stewart Maxwell: To ask the Scottish Executive what the estimated cost is for the public sector to bid for the right to build and operate the replacement HM Prison Low Moss.

S2W-31593 Mr Jamie Stone: To ask the Scottish Executive how many NHS dentists there are in NHS Highland and what the average number is of patients per dentist.

S2W-31594 Richard Lochhead: To ask the Scottish Executive how many homes were sold by each local authority in each of the last 10 years, also expressed as a percentage of stock in each case and showing (a) national totals and (b) cumulative change over the period.

S2W-31595 Richard Lochhead: To ask the Scottish Executive how many (a) private and (b) public sector homes were built in each local authority area in each of the last 10 years, showing (i) cumulative changes over the period and (ii) national totals.

S2W-31596 Brian Adam: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-12521 by Malcolm Chisholm on 8 December 2004, how many people under 60 receive the disability living allowance and how much it would cost to include them in the central heating programme, broken down into private tenants, owner occupiers and social housing tenants.

S2W-31597 Brian Adam: To ask the Scottish Executive how many installations under the central heating programme and Warm Deal have been completed in each year since inception of the central heating programme, broken down by local authority area.

S2W-31598 Brian Adam: To ask the Scottish Executive how many central heating programme and Warm Deal installations have been (a) approved and (b) installed since the operation of the programme transferred to Scottish Gas, broken down by local authority area.

S2W-31599 Brian Adam: To ask the Scottish Executive how many central heating programme contractors have been (a) approved and (b) appointed by Scottish Gas and where they are located, broken down by local authority area.

S2W-31600 Mr Jamie Stone: To ask the Scottish Executive what progress NHS Highland is making in achieving a maximum 26-week waiting time for orthodontics.

S2W-31601 Alex Neil: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-31044 by Elish Angiolini QC on 31 January 2007, whether a charge of perverting the course of justice would be appropriate in respect of the actions of the SCRO experts if evidence came to light that their evidence given at any trial or inquiry or in any report was false.

S2W-31602 Alex Neil: To ask the Scottish Executive how many people have been prosecuted in Scotland under section 1 of the Dangerous Dogs Act 1991 in each year since it was introduced.

S2W-31603 Stewart Stevenson: To ask the Scottish Executive which PFI health projects have been cancelled in each year since 1999.

S2W-31604 Stewart Stevenson: To ask the Scottish Executive on how many occasions in each year since 1999 a minister has had duties removed to avoid a potential conflict of interest.

S2W-31605 Stewart Stevenson: To ask the Scottish Executive what its total expenditure was on (a) newspapers, magazines and periodicals, (b) landscape gardening and flora and (c) stationery in each of the last three years.

S2W-31606 Stewart Stevenson: To ask the Scottish Executive what percentage of people were removed from an NHS waiting list in each year since 1997, broken down by NHS board.

S2W-31607 Stewart Stevenson: To ask the Scottish Executive what the average prison sentence was for (a) violent and (b) non-violent crimes in each of the last five years and what average sentence was served by prisoners in each category who were released in each of these years.

S2W-31608 Stewart Stevenson: To ask the Scottish Executive what the conviction rate was for (a) violent and (b) all crimes recorded in each of the last 10 years, broken down by police force area.

S2W-31609 Stewart Stevenson: To ask the Scottish Executive how many of those convicted of sexual offences have absconded while (a) on parole, (b) on early release and (c) serving a suspended sentence in each of the last seven years, broken down by police force area.

S2W-31610 Roseanna Cunningham: To ask the Scottish Executive how many incidents involving people trapped in moving water have taken place in each fire and rescue service area in each of the last five years and how many of these incidents have resulted in deaths.

S2W-31611 Mr Stewart Maxwell: To ask the Scottish Executive what the Intellectual Assets Centre’s total budget was in each financial year since its inception and how much of that budget has been allocated by (a) Scottish Enterprise, (b) Highlands and Islands Enterprise and (c) the Executive in each of those years.

S2W-31612 Mr Stewart Maxwell: To ask the Scottish Executive how many businesses have been assisted to protect their assets by the Intellectual Assets Centre in each year since its inception.

S2W-31613 Mr Stewart Maxwell: To ask the Scottish Executive when the qualifying criteria for national performing arts company status will be published.

S2W-31614 Fiona Hyslop: To ask the Scottish Executive whether NHS Lanarkshire would have been legally entitled to exclude non-GP companies limited by shares from the recent tendering process for GP medical services in Harthill.

S2W-31615 Mr Jamie Stone: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it is developing to assist town centres and retail businesses located in town centres that are being effected by large out-of-town retail outlets.

S2W-31616 Fergus Ewing: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it plans to introduce track access charges for the proposed (a) Edinburgh Airport rail link, (b) Glasgow Airport rail link, (c) Waverley line or (d) Airdrie to Bathgate rail link or for any other rail project under construction or proposed for construction.

S2W-31617 Fergus Ewing: To ask the Scottish Executive what direction or guidance it has given to Transport Scotland in respect of the preparation of a high-level output statement for Network Rail for the next control period.

S2W-31618 Fergus Ewing: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it has intimated any view on track access charges on the Stirling-Alloa-Kincardine rail line and, if so, to whom.

S2W-31619 Rob Gibson: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will provide details of Network Rail’s estate in Scotland.

S2W-31620 Rob Gibson: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will provide details of the extent to which Network Rail’s estate is currently used for rail services.

S2W-31621 Rob Gibson: To ask the Scottish Executive to what extent brownfield sites form part of Network Rail’s estate, giving the location of any such sites.

S2W-31622 Rob Gibson: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it has provide access to areas of Network Rail’s estate for other public uses.

S2W-31623 Rob Gibson: To ask the Scottish Executive what discussions it has had about the use of redundant areas of Network Rail’s estate.

S2W-31624 Fergus Ewing: To ask the Scottish Executive what the timescales are for the production of a (a) high-level output statement showing the funding available for the railway infrastructure and (b) list of infrastructural requirements in respect of the Scottish rail network for the duration of the next control period.
