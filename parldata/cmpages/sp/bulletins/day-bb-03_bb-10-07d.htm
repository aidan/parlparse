Business Bulletin No. 134 / 2003

Tuesday 7th October 2003

Section D : Oral Questions selected for answer on Thursday 9th October 2003
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive’s Cabinet. (S2F-263)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Prime Minister and what issues he intends to discuss. (S2F-264)

3. Karen Gillon: To ask the First Minister what the policy objectives of the Scottish Executive are in relation to the launch of its consultation on the implementation of Common Agricultural Policy reform. (S2F-269)

4. Stewart Stevenson: To ask the First Minister what plans the Scottish Executive has to establish a publicly accessible and complete register showing land ownership in Scotland. (S2F-268)

5. George Lyon: To ask the First Minister whether the Scottish Executive will review its plans to tender Caledonian MacBrayne ferry services in light of the European Court of Justice’s decision on government subsidies for essential services. (S2F-276)

6. Chris Ballance: To ask the First Minister whether its national healthy living campaign will lead to healthy eating habits. (S2F-284)
