Business Bulletin No. 162/2002

Tuesday 12 November 2002

Section D : Oral Questions selected for answer on on 14 November 2002
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive's Cabinet. (S1F-2243)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he next plans to meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he intends to raise. (S1F-2244)

3. Des McNulty: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive will take forward its plans to end the two-tier workforce for public private partnership projects. (S1F-2248)

4. Stewart Stevenson: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive will respond to EU announcements on fishing made on 11 November 2002. (S1F-2262)

5. Donald Gorrie: To ask the First Minister what the Scottish Executive's policy is on the fingerprinting of children in schools. (S1F-2257)

6. Trish Godman: To ask the First Minister whether the Scottish Executive is planning any legislative measures to deal with nuisance neighbours. (S1F-2252)
