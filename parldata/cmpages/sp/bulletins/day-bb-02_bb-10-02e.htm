Section E : Written questions lodged on 1 October 2002

  

Questions marked with a triangle () are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S1W-30093 Roseanna Cunningham: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S1W-28732 by Mr Jim Wallace on 26 September 2002, why it is commercially confidential to specify whether the premium paid to the Authority in the event that a private prison cannot find insurance at a commercial price is greater than the premium previously paid to a private provider for the same insurance.
