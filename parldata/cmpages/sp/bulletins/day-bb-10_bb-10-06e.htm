Business Bulletin No. 146/2010: Wednesday 6 October 2010
Contents An A B C D E F G H I J

Section E – Written questions lodged on 5 October 2010

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

Questions in which a member has indicated a declarable interest are marked with an "R".

S3W-36701 Tavish Scott: To ask the Scottish Executive when the European Fisheries Fund programme awards due to be announced in September 2010 are to be announced.

S3W-36702 Tavish Scott: To ask the Scottish Executive what percentage of the Scotland Rural Development Programme funding in (a) 2007-08, (b) 2008-09, (c) 2009-10 and (d) to date in 2010-11 was or has been spent on administrative work by (i) the Scottish Government, (ii) Scottish Natural Heritage and (iii) other public bodies.

S3W-36703 Tavish Scott: To ask the Scottish Executive what the Scotland Rural Development Programme budget is for 2010-11 and what sums have been (a) spent and (b) committed for projects in each Regional Proposal Assessment Committee region, broken down into the various delivery schemes used.

S3W-36704 Gavin Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive how many direct measures were issued for (a) assault and (b) assault to injury in May 2010, broken down by sheriff court.

S3W-36705 Gavin Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive how many direct measures were issued for (a) assault and (b) assault to injury in June 2010, broken down by sheriff court.

S3W-36706 Gavin Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive how many direct measures were issued for (a) assault and (b) assault to injury in July 2010, broken down by sheriff court.

S3W-36707 Gavin Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive how many direct measures were issued for (a) assault and (b) assault to injury in August 2010, broken down by sheriff court.

S3W-36708 Gavin Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive how many direct measures were issued for (a) assault and (b) assault to injury in September 2010, broken down by sheriff court.

S3W-36709 Mary Mulligan: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S3W-35789 by Alex Neil on 30 August 2010, what the outcomes were of the seminars on the hub model for distribution of funding under the Housing Options programme.

S3W-36710 Duncan McNeil: To ask the Scottish Executive what meetings it has had with the Spanish manufacturing firm, Gamesa, regarding investment in the Scottish renewables industry and what future meetings it has planned.

S3W-36711 Duncan McNeil: To ask the Scottish Executive how it will assist Inverclyde Renewables Alliance in developing Inverclyde as a renewables manufacturing location following its inclusion in the west coast cluster in the National Renewables Infrastructure Plan.

S3W-36712 Duncan McNeil: To ask the Scottish Executive how much has been allocated to the No Knives Better Lives campaign, broken down by (a) year and (b) local authority area.

S3W-36713 Cathy Peattie: To ask the Scottish Executive how many sites listed in the Scottish Water Register of Assets contain private water pumps owned by domestic customers; how many pumps are on each site; how many pumps are shared and how many customers can access each pump.

S3W-36714 Cathy Peattie: To ask the Scottish Executive how many sites listed in the Scottish Water Register of Assets containing private water pumps owned by domestic customers have been declared surplus to requirements and sold off by public auction in each of the last 10 years; how many pumps were on each site; how many pumps were shared and how many customers had access to each pump.

S3W-36715 Gavin Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive what proportion of prisoners at HMP Edinburgh was assessed as having an alcohol problem in 2009-10.

S3W-36716 Gavin Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive what proportion of prisoners at HMP Edinburgh was assessed as having a substance abuse problem in 2009-10.

S3W-36717 Gavin Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive what proportion of prisoners at HMP Addiewell has been assessed as having an alcohol problem in each year since it opened.

S3W-36718 Gavin Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive what proportion of prisoners at HMP Addiewell has been assessed as having a substance abuse problem in each year since it opened.

S3W-36719 Gavin Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive how many prisoners in HMP Edinburgh have been tested for drugs in each year since 2008, broken down by the number of positive tests and substances identified.

S3W-36720 Gavin Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive how many prisoners in HMP Addiewell have been tested for drugs in each year since it opened, broken down by the number of positive tests and substances identified.

S3W-36721 Gavin Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive what assessment it has made of the training available to Scottish Prison Service staff at HMP (a) Edinburgh and (b) Addiewell.

S3W-36722 Gavin Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive what its assessment is of the prisoner progression and community reintegration facilities available at HMP (a) Edinburgh and (b) Addiewell.

S3W-36723 Jamie Stone: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will reallocate money to fund the Community and Renewable Energy Scheme and, if so, when.
