BUSINESS BULLETIN 9/1999

Thursday 3rd June 1999

Section E –  New written questions lodged on Wednesday 2 June 1999
 

S1W-33 Alex Neil: To ask the Scottish Executive what its plans are for dealing with fuel poverty in Scotland.

 

S1W-34 Mr Kenny MacAskill: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it intends to initiate a Public Inquiry into the operation and management of the following organisations: (a) Craigmillar Festival Society; (b) Craigmillar Opportunities Trust; and (c) Craigmillar Initiatives.

 

S1W-35 Rhoda Grant: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it has to decentralise Civil Service jobs.

 

S1W-36 Ms Nicola Sturgeon: To ask the Scottish Executive what proportion of the £600 million referred to in the "Partnership for Scotland" agreement for investment in the capital infrastructure of schools does it intend apply to the support of Public Private Partnerships.
