Business Bulletin No. 48 / 2004

Tuesday 16th march 2004

Section D : Oral questions selected for answer on Thursday 18th March 2004
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive's cabinet. (S2F-732)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he next plans to meet the Prime Minister and what issues he intends to raise. (S2F-741)

3. Tommy Sheridan: To ask the First Minister what the top priority is for the Scottish Executive's cabinet. (S2F-748)

4. Paul Martin: To ask the First Minister what action can be taken to address the issues highlighted in Health and Well-being Profiles for each Scottish Parliamentary Constituency, 2004. (S2F-742)

5. Stewart Stevenson: To ask the First Minister when changes in the regulation of the white fish industry will be announced. (S2F-736)

6. Iain Smith: To ask the First Minister whether a review of security measures on Scottish public transport and other possible terrorist targets will be undertaken. (S2F-752)
