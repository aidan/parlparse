Section E – Written questions lodged on 21 May 2008

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

Questions in which a member has indicated a declarable interest are marked with an "R".

S3W-13250 John Park: To ask the Scottish Executive when it will announce its decision on the Proposed Modifications to Finalised Fife Structure Plan (2006) arising from the Re-appraisal of the Housing Land Requirement (2007) by Fife Council.

S3W-13257 Jim Tolson: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it believes that short-assured tenancies are appropriate for housing vulnerable individuals.

S3W-13259 Jim Tolson: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it believes that a six-month short-assured tenancy is enough to give vulnerable individuals a secure start after a period of homelessness.

S3W-13262 Nigel Don: To ask the Scottish Executive what the fuel costs have been for the (a) police, (b) ambulance and (c) fire service in each year since 1999.
