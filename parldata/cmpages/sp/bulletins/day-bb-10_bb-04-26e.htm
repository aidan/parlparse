Business Bulletin No. 68/2010: Monday 26 April 2010
Contents An A B C D E F G H I J

Section E – Written questions lodged on 23 April 2010

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

Questions in which a member has indicated a declarable interest are marked with an "R".

S3W-33348 Marlyn Glen: To ask the Scottish Executive what the European age-standardised incidence rate of survival for colorectal cancer is at (a) one year and (b) five years in each of the last three four-year periods in Tayside, broken down by gender.

S3W-33349 Marlyn Glen: To ask the Scottish Executive what the European age-standardised incidence rate of survival for (a) breast and (b) prostate cancer is at (i) one year and (ii) five years in each of the last three four-year periods in Tayside.

S3W-33350 Murdo Fraser: To ask the Scottish Executive when it expects to make a decision on the proposed Calliacher wind farm development.

S3W-33351 Murdo Fraser: To ask the Scottish Executive when it expects to make a decision on the proposed Logiealmond wind farm development.

S3W-33352 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it was represented at the European Tourism Stakeholders Conference on 14-15 April 2010.

S3W-33353 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive what action it is undertaking to encourage small and medium-sized enterprises to apply for funding for eco-innovation projects under the 2010 Competitiveness and Innovation Programme.

S3W-33354 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive what discussions it has had with stakeholders in Scotland regarding the promotion of the European Year for Combating Poverty and Social Exclusion.

S3W-33355 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive what discussions it has had with stakeholders in Scotland regarding the promotion of the European Year of Volunteering 2011.

S3W-33356 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive what action it is undertaking to encourage school pupils to participate in volunteering projects.

S3W-33357 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive what funds are available to social care providers to develop telecare technologies.

S3W-33358 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it plans to review the Care Commission’s complaints procedure.

S3W-33359 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive how it ensures the confidentiality of patients asked to take part in the Better Together: Scotland’s Patient Experience Programme.

S3W-33360 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive what companies were hired to implement the Better Together: Scotland’s Patient Experience Programme surveys.

S3W-33361 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive what the budget is for the Better Together: Scotland’s Patient Experience Programme surveys.

S3W-33362 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive what discussions it has had with the UK Government regarding the application fee and subsequent annual fee for companies wishing to set up a battery compliance scheme for recycling batteries.

S3W-33363 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive what discussions it has had with stakeholders regarding the Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment Regulations 2006 and the fees levied to companies involved in electronics recycling.

S3W-33364 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive what guidance it has issued to NHS boards regarding the prostate-specific antigen (PSA) test

S3W-33365 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive what research has been carried out on the effectiveness of the prostate-specific antigen (PSA) test.

S3W-33366 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it plans to review the screening arrangements for the detection of prostate cancer.

S3W-33367 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive what guidance it has issued to NHS boards regarding digital rectal examinations.

S3W-33368 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive what action it is taking regarding the (a) digitisation, (b) online accessibility and (c) preservation of cultural works.

S3W-33369 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive what discussions it has had with the UK Government and relevant Scottish stakeholders regarding the Anti-Counterfeiting Trade Agreement negotiations.

S3W-33370 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it issues guidance to police authorities regarding the purchase of police vehicles.

S3W-33371 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it monitors police authority spending in relation to the purchase of police vehicles.

S3W-33372 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it encourages police authorities to reduce their carbon footprints by purchasing fuel-efficient vehicles.

S3W-33373 Δ Joe FitzPatrick: To ask the Scottish Executive what the outcome is of the leak inquiry that the First Minister requested on 21 January 2010.
