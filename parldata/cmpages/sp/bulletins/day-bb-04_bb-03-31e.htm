Business Bulletin No. 59 / 2004

Wednesday 31st March 2004

Section E : Written questions lodged on Tuesday 30th March 2004
  

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

 

S2W-7288 Jim Mather: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it has to ensure 100% broadband coverage across Scotland, in the light of the decision to award BT a contract to achieve that level of coverage in Northern Ireland by the end of 2005.

*The member has provided the following Gaelic translation:

A dh' fhaighneachd de Riaghaltas na h-Alba dè na planaichean a th' aige gus dèanamh cinnteach gum bi còmhdachadh 100% den bhann-leathann ann ann an Alba, mar thoradh air a' cho-dhùnadh cùmhnant a thoirt do BhT gus dèanamh cinnteach gum bi an ìre sin de chòmhdachadh ann an Èirinn a Tuath ro dheireadh na bliadhna 2005.*

S2W-7289 Jim Mather: To ask the Scottish Executive, if there is not 100% broadband coverage across Scotland by the end of 2005, what new steps it will take to restore Scotland's competitive advantage over Northern Ireland.

*The member has provided the following Gaelic translation:

A dh' fhaighneachd de Riaghaltas na h-Alba dè na ceumannan ùra a ghabhas e, mas e is nach eil còmhdachadh 100% ann den bhann-leathann ann an Alba ro dheireadh na bliadhna 2005, gus dèanamh cinnteach gum bi eaconomaidh na h-Alba nas farpaisiche na Èirinn a Tuath a-rithist.*
