Business Bulletin No. 151/2004

Tuesday 26 October 2004

Section D : Oral Questions selected for answer on Thursday 28 October 2004

  

First Minister’s Question Time

1. Nicola Sturgeon: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Prime Minister and what issues will be discussed. (S2F-1144)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive’s Cabinet. (S2F-1145)

3. Tommy Sheridan: To ask the First Minister whether he will accept the invitation from Rose Gentle, the mother of Gordon Gentle, killed in Basra on 28 June 2004, to attend the demonstration in Pollok on 30 October 2004 in support of British soldiers being brought back from Iraq. (S2F-1159)

4. Mr Frank McAveety: To ask the First Minister what action is being considered to address concerns surrounding the increase in knife crime in Scotland and, particularly, in the Strathclyde Region. (S2F-1137)

5. Fergus Ewing: To ask the First Minister what role the Scottish Executive will play in decisions about how many more casinos there should be in Scotland. (S2F-1153)

6. Miss Annabel Goldie: To ask the First Minister whether the Scottish Executive is satisfied with the adequacy of funding provided to NHS Argyll and Clyde. (S2F-1160)
