Business Bulletin No. 122/2001

Tuesday 4 September 2001

Section E : Written questions lodged on Monday 3 September 2001
  

S1W-17884 Mr Keith Harding: To ask the Scottish Executive what the cost was of the DTZ Pieda research project on a community ownership information pack carried out on its behalf.

S1W-17885 Mrs Margaret Ewing: To ask the Scottish Executive when it intends to reschedule the cancelled exhibition on the planned Fochabers/Mosstodloch bypass and what the reasons were for the cancellation of this exhibition

S1W-17886 Mrs Margaret Ewing: To ask the Scottish Executive when it expects work to commence on the lowering of kerbs in the High Street, Fochabers.

S1W-17887 Mrs Margaret Ewing: To ask the Scottish Executive whether there have been any delays in issuing draft orders in relation to the planned Fochabers/Mosstodloch bypass and what the reasons have been for any such delays.

S1W-17888 George Lyon: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S1W-15269 by Ross Finnie on 8 May 2001, whether the information requested on a breakdown of the payments made to farmers and crofters in the Argyll and Bute constituency under the new Less Favoured Area scheme for this year is now available and, if so, whether it will now provide it and, if not, whether it has any plans in the future to collect this information.

S1W-17889 Dr Elaine Murray: To ask the Scottish Executive what legislation, including subordinate legislation, exists to control the exterior decoration of properties.

S1W-17890 Mr John Farquhar Munro: To ask the Scottish Executive what role it expects to play in improving and maintaining affordable air links from the Highlands and Islands to major urban centres.

S1W-17891 Mr John Farquhar Munro: To ask the Scottish Executive what representations it has made to British Regional Air Lines about a possible reduction in air services between Inverness and Gatwick.

S1W-17892 Fergus Ewing: To ask the Scottish Executive what steps it has taken or plans to take to address any animal welfare issues this autumn resulting from the animal movement restrictions and lack of auction sales and whether it will provide any assistance for the purchase of additional fodder or other purposes.

S1W-17893 Fergus Ewing: To ask the Scottish Executive what support it will provide for the disposal of venison carcasses and whether it will provide additional support for the marketing of Scottish wild venison throughout the UK.

S1W-17894 Fergus Ewing: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will support the campaign to relocate the Scottish Arts Council to Inverness.

S1W-17895 Fergus Ewing: To ask the Scottish Executive how many civil service or non-departmental public body jobs have been relocated to Inverness since May 1999.

S1W-17896 Fergus Ewing: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it has to relocate civil servants to Inverness.

S1W-17897 Fergus Ewing: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it has to relocate any executive agencies or non-departmental public bodies either in whole or in part to Inverness.

S1W-17898 Fiona McLeod: To ask the Scottish Executive whether there is any recorded incidence of wind farms interfering with airport radar.
