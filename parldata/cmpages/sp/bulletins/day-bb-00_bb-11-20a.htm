34th Meeting, 2000

The Committee will meet at 2.00 pm in Committee Room 8, Glasgow City Chambers, George Square, Glasgow

1. Item in private: The Committee will decide whether to take item 6 in private.

Dr David Colvin, Chairman of SACRO (Safeguarding Communities – Reducing Offending), Susan Matheson, Chief Executive, SACRO, Janice Hewitt, Director, APEX Scotland, David McKenna, Assistant Director, Victim Support Scotland and Dr Jacqueline Tombs, member of Consortium.

972: Initiative by the Federal Republic of Germany for a Framework Decision on criminal law protection against fraudulent or unfair anti-competitive conduct in relation to the award of public contracts in the common market;

1190: Draft Framework Decision on the standing of victims in criminal procedure.
