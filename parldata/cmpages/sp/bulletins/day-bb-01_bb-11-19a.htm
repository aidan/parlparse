Business Bulletin No. 168/2001

Monday 19 November 2001

Section A : Daily Business List
  

Meetings of Committees

All meetings take place in Committee Chambers, Edinburgh unless otherwise indicated.

Finance Committee

23rd Meeting, 2001

The Committee will meet at 10.00 am in Kirkcudbright Town Hall, Kirkcudbright

1. Items in Private: The Committee will consider whether to take agenda item 3 in private.

2. 2002/03 Budget Process: The Committee will consider the Scottish Executive's draft budget and take evidence from-

Mrs Norma Hart, Chief Executive, Dumfries and Galloway Tourist Board;

John Kilgour, Chairman, Federation of Small Businesses, Dumfries and Galloway branch;

Colin Williamson, Chief Executive, Scottish Enterprise Dumfries and Galloway;

Margaret Burton, Chairperson, Dumfries and Galloway Chamber of Commerce;

Bob Carruth, Regional Policy Adviser, National Farmers' Union.

At approximately 11.15 am

Julian Cowie, Director of Finance, Dumfries and Galloway Council;

Ruth Paterson, Stewartry Council for Voluntary Services;

Mike McLurg, Director, Nithsdale Council for Voluntary Services.

At approximately Midday

Laura Morrison, Director of Finance, Dumfries and Galloway Health Board;

Russell Marchant, Principal, Barony College, Dumfries;

Tony Jakimciw, Principal, Dumfries and Galloway College, Dumfries.

3. 2002/03 Budget Process: The Committee will discuss its lines of questioning for agenda item 4.

Not before 2.30 pm

4. 2002/03 Budget Process: The Committee will take evidence on the Scottish Executive's draft budget from Angus MacKay MSP, Minister for Finance and Local Government.

5. The Budget (Scotland) Act 2001 (Amendment) Order 2001 (SSI 2001/draft): The Committee will debate motion S1M-2423 in the name of Angus MacKay MSP, Minister for Finance and Local Government to approve the draft order.
