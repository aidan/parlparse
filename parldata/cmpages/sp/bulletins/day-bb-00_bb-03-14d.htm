BUSINESS BULLETIN 47 / 2000

Tuesday 14 March 2000

Section D –  Oral questions selected for answer on 16 March 2000

 

  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr Alex Salmond: To ask the First Minister whether he plans to reshuffle Ministerial responsibilities in the near future. (S1F-207)

 

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he last met the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues were discussed. (S1F-204)

 

3. Mr Kenneth Macintosh: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive intends to address the geographical differences in educational attainment in Scotland. (S1F-212)

 

4. Fergus Ewing: To ask the First Minister whether the amounts set by local authorities for council tax bills for the financial year 2000-01 are on average higher than those in England for houses of equal value. (S1F-199)

 

5. Tommy Sheridan: To ask the First Minister what proposals the Scottish Executive has to attract inward investment to Glasgow. (S1F-216)

 

6. Margaret Jamieson: To ask the First Minister what steps are being taken to tackle heart disease, particularly amongst women, in Scotland (S1F-213)
