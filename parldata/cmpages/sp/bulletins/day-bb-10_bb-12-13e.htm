Section E – Written questions lodged on 10 December 2010

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

Questions in which a member has indicated a declarable interest are marked with an "R".

S3W-38236 Murdo Fraser: To ask the Scottish Executive how much it has invested in studies on the effects of milder winters.

S3W-38237 Murdo Fraser: To ask the Scottish Executive at what temperatures large-scale onshore wind turbines are unable to operate.

S3W-38238 Murdo Fraser: To ask the Scottish Executive what impact prolonged and severe wintry weather, including snow and ice, has on onshore wind turbines performance.

S3W-38239 Patricia Ferguson: To ask the Scottish Executive whether budget allocations to local authorities are needs based.

S3W-38240 Patricia Ferguson: To ask the Scottish Executive how it arrived at the budget allocated to Glasgow City Council.

S3W-38241 Patricia Ferguson: To ask the Scottish Executive whether all local authorities have had the same reduction in budget levels.

S3W-38242 Patricia Ferguson: To ask the Scottish Executive which local authorities have received budgets reduced by more than the 2.6% agreed with the leadership of COSLA.

S3W-38244 Pauline McNeill: To ask the Scottish Executive what a 3.6% reduction amounts to in terms of Glasgow City Council’s budget.
