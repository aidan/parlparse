Business Bulletin No. 56/2002

Wednesday 3 April 2002

Section A : Daily Business List
  

Meeting of the Parliament

12.00 pm Tributes to Her Majesty Queen Elizabeth The Queen Mother

S1M-2962 Mr Jack McConnell: Motion of Condolence-That the Parliament expresses its deep regret and sadness at the death of Her Majesty Queen Elizabeth The Queen Mother, conveys its sincere condolences to Her Majesty The Queen and other members of the Royal Family in their sad bereavement, affirms the great affection and esteem in which Her Majesty Queen Elizabeth The Queen Mother was held, and expresses gratitude for her life of outstanding public service and her long and close association with Scotland and the Scottish people.

Supported by: John Swinney, David McLetchie, Jim Wallace
