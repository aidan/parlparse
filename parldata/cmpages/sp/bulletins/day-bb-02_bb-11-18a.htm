Business Bulletin No. 166/2002

Monday 18 November 2002

Section A : Daily Business List
  

Meetings of Committees

All meetings take place in Committee Chambers, Edinburgh unless otherwise indicated.

Finance Committee

22nd Meeting, 2002

The Committee will meet at 11.30 am in Council Chambers, Tigh na Sgire, Portree, Isle of Skye

1. Budget process 2003-04: The Committee will consider the outcome of workshop sessions involving local organisations held before the start of the meeting.

Not before 2.00 pm

2. Budget process 2003-04: The Committee will take evidence from—

Peter Peacock MSP, Deputy Minister for Finance and Public Services;

David Palmer, Finance Co-ordination, Scottish Executive.

3. The Budget (Scotland) Act 2002 (Amendment) Order 2002 (SSI 2002/draft): The Committee will debate motion S1M-03567, in the name of Andy Kerr MSP, Minister for Finance and Public Services, to approve the draft order.

4. Items in private: The Committee will decide whether to consider the draft report on the Financial Memorandum for the Homelessness etc. (Scotland) Bill in private at its next meeting.
