Business Bulletin No. 176/2002

Tuesday 3 December 2002

Section D : Oral Questions selected for answer on 5 December 2002
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister when he next plans to meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he intends to discuss. (S1F-2313)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive's Cabinet. (S1F-2325)

3. Paul Martin: To ask the First Minister what measures the Scottish Executive will take to deal with any increase in the number of racist attacks. (S1F-2321)

4. Michael Russell: To ask the First Minister when the appointment of a chairperson for the board of the national theatre will be announced. (S1F-2315)

5. Dorothy-Grace Elder: To ask the First Minister whether the Scottish Executive has any plans to review the methods of alerting any patients that may have been at risk of exposure to variant Creutzfeldt-Jakob disease. (S1F-2332)

6. John Farquhar Munro: To ask the First Minister what representations the Scottish Executive has made to Her Majesty's Government regarding the passage of ships with dangerous cargoes past Scotland's coastline. (S1F-2328)
