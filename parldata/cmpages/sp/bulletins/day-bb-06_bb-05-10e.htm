Business Bulletin No. 74/2006: Wednesday 10 May 2006
Contents An A B C D E F G H I J

Section E – Written questions lodged on 9 May 2006

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S2W-25755 Stewart Stevenson: To ask the Scottish Executive when the Minister for Environment and Rural Development will reply to my letter dated 5 April 2006 regarding the Local Capital Grants Scheme.

S2W-25756 Shona Robison: To ask the Scottish Executive who is responsible for redundant sewers on private property and what legislation defines such responsibility.

S2W-25757 Mr Adam Ingram: To ask the Scottish Executive how many children under six have been diagnosed with a mental illness in each NHS board area in each of the last five years, broken down by (a) type of illness and (b) age.

S2W-25758 Mr Adam Ingram: To ask the Scottish Executive what funding packages which specifically target parents of children under five have been allocated to the Health Education Board for Scotland for the promotion of mental health issues since 2001.

S2W-25759 Mr Adam Ingram: To ask the Scottish Executive how many males aged (a) 14 to 16, (b) 17 to 19 and (c) 20 to 22 suffered from chronic alcohol dependency in each year since 2000, broken down by NHS board area.

S2W-25760 Mr Adam Ingram: To ask the Scottish Executive how many females aged (a) 14 to 16, (b) 17 to 19 and (c) 20 to 22 suffered from chronic alcohol dependency in each year since 2000, broken down by NHS board area.

S2W-25761 Mr Adam Ingram: To ask the Scottish Executive how many psychiatric beds were available for 16 to 18-year-olds in each NHS board area in each year since 2001.

S2W-25762 Mr Adam Ingram: To ask the Scottish Executive what average cost has been incurred in respect of psychiatric in-patients in each year since 2000, broken down by NHS board.

S2W-25763 Mr Adam Ingram: To ask the Scottish Executive how many staffed beds there were in psychiatric units in each NHS board area in each year since 2000.

S2W-25764 Mr Adam Ingram: To ask the Scottish Executive what research it has carried out in the last two years into the link between mental health and social exclusion among the general population.

S2W-25765 Mr Adam Ingram: To ask the Scottish Executive how many police officers were unfit for duty as a result of stress in each year since 2001, broken down by force area and expressed also as a percentage of the total number of police officers.

S2W-25766 Mr Adam Ingram: To ask the Scottish Executive how many nurses reported as unfit for work as a result of stress in each year since 2001, broken down by NHS board area and expressed also as a percentage of the total number of nurses.

S2W-25767 Mr Adam Ingram: To ask the Scottish Executive how many GPs reported as unfit for work as a result of stress in each year since 2001, broken down by NHS board area and expressed also as a percentage of the total number of GPs.

S2W-25768 Mr Adam Ingram: To ask the Scottish Executive how many NHS board managers reported as unfit for work as a result of stress in each year since 2001, broken down by board area and expressed also as a percentage of the total number of board managers.

S2W-25769 Mr Adam Ingram: To ask the Scottish Executive how many of its staff at grade C1 or above reported as unfit for work as a result of stress in each year since 2001, broken down by department and expressed also as a percentage of the total number of staff employed at C1 or above.

S2W-25770 Mr Adam Ingram: To ask the Scottish Executive how many of its staff at grade B3 or below reported as unfit for work as a result of stress in each year since 2001, broken down by department and expressed also as a percentage of the total number of staff employed at B3 or below.

S2W-25771 Mr Adam Ingram: To ask the Scottish Executive what training and guidance it has in place to support its staff who suffer from stress.

S2W-25772 Mr Adam Ingram: To ask the Scottish Executive how many hours were spent by its staff attending away days in each year since 2000, broken down by department.

S2W-25773 Mr Adam Ingram: To ask the Scottish Executive what the average hourly wage is of its staff.

S2W-25774 Mr Adam Ingram: To ask the Scottish Executive how much was spent on facilities, materials, transport and other related activities and support for its staff who attended away days in each year since 2000, broken down by department.

S2W-25775 Mr Mark Ruskell: To ask the Scottish Executive whether Scottish Water has been set targets to reduce leakage rates and what reductions the Executive hopes will be achieved by the end of the first phase of the Quality and Standards III investment programme.

S2W-25776 Colin Fox: To ask the Scottish Executive what the traffic regulations are in respect of the use of Greenways.

S2W-25777 Colin Fox: To ask the Scottish Executive who is responsible for monitoring the use of Greenways.

S2W-25778 Colin Fox: To ask the Scottish Executive what the traffic regulations are in respect of the use of private hire cabs.

S2W-25779 Colin Fox: To ask the Scottish Executive what the traffic regulations are in respect of the use of Greenways by private hire cabs.

S2W-25780 Colin Fox: To ask the Scottish Executive who is responsible for any changes to the type of vehicles that can use Greenways and what the criteria are for making any such changes.

S2W-25781 Ms Rosemary Byrne: To ask the Scottish Executive why the Scottish Funding Council has decided to award targeted growth to address the issue of college growth in areas where there is low participation to all such areas except the south of Scotland.

S2W-25782 Carolyn Leckie: To ask the Scottish Executive what regulations and guidelines are in place governing the secondment of the employees of public bodies to the private and independent sectors.

S2W-25783 Carolyn Leckie: To ask the Scottish Executive what information it has regarding the arrangement between the Care Commission and BUPA in relation to the secondment of staff.

S2W-25784 Carolyn Leckie: To ask the Scottish Executive how many of the staff of public bodies have been seconded to private and independent organisations in each year since 1999.

S2W-25785 Carolyn Leckie: To ask the Scottish Executive what the cost has been to the public purse of secondment arrangements between public bodies and private and independent organisations in each year since 1999, also broken down by public body entering such arrangements.
