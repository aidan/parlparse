BUSINESS BULLETIN 187 / 2000

Tuesday 5 December 2000

Section D - Oral questions selected for answer on 7 December 2000

 

 

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister when he next plans to meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he intends to raise. (S1F-710)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Prime Minister and what issues he plans to raise. (S1F-708)

3. Cathy Jamieson: To ask the First Minister what plans the Scottish Executive has to enhance the role of arts and sport in schools. (S1F-715)

4. Brian Adam: To ask the First Minister what plans the Scottish Executive has to preserve jobs in the catching and processing sectors of Scotland’s fishing industry. (S1F-701)

5. Mr Duncan McNeil: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive plans to promote Scotland in Europe. (S1F-713)

6. Ms Margo MacDonald: To ask the First Minister how it plans to recruit the required number of nurses for hospitals to cope with seasonal admissions this winter. (S1F-716)
