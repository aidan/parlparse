Business Bulletin No. 112 / 2004

Tuesday 29th June 2004

Section E : Written questions lodged on friday 25th June 2004
  

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S2W-9346 Susan Deacon: To ask the Scottish Executive what steps are being taken to support the effective sharing of data between different agencies.
