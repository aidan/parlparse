Section E – Written questions lodged from 21 to 25 August 2006

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S2W-27867 Mr Kenny MacAskill: To ask the Scottish Executive what average (a) hourly rate and (b) expenses were paid for the services of (i) partners, (ii) qualified solicitors and (iii) unqualified solicitors involved in representation of the defenders in the Napier case.

S2W-27868 Shona Robison: To ask the Scottish Executive what funding is available for pain clinics.

S2W-27869 Shona Robison: To ask the Scottish Executive how much funding has been allocated to each pain clinic in each of the last five years.

S2W-27870 Shona Robison: To ask the Scottish Executive how many pain clinics there are in Scotland.

S2W-27871 Shona Robison: To ask the Scottish Executive what the average waiting time is to be treated in each pain clinic.

S2W-27872 Shona Robison: To ask the Scottish Executive what action it is taking to assist pain clinics across Scotland.

S2W-27873 Shona Robison: To ask the Scottish Executive what the projected future funding is for pain clinics.

S2W-27874 Dr Elaine Murray: To ask the Scottish Executive whether there is any minimum frequency for testing the effluent from sewage treatment works with a design population equivalent of 15,000 or over and, if so, what maximum permitted length of time is allowed between taking measurements.

S2W-27875 Murdo Fraser: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-27564 by Tavish Scott on 17 August 2006, when it expects that the further work being undertaken to assess the implications and practicalities of dualling between Perth and Pitlochry will be completed.

S2W-27876 Murdo Fraser: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-27564 by Tavish Scott on 17 August 2006, what issues are being considered in the further work being undertaken to assess the implications and practicalities of dualling between Perth and Pitlochry.

S2W-27877 Fergus Ewing: To ask the Scottish Executive whether information is made public about individuals who have had sexual offences prevention orders granted against them and, if not, (a) how the public is made aware of any restrictions on the liberty of a sex offender and (b) whether the Executive will consider making such information public.

S2W-27878 Fergus Ewing: To ask the Scottish Executive what the total cost of supervision of sex offenders has been, including the associated cost of police and council services and housing costs, in each year since 1999, broken down by police force area.

S2W-27879 Christine Grahame: To ask the Scottish Executive what advice it offers to individuals and businesses who work with animal hides in relation to the potential risk from anthrax, in light of the recent death in the Scottish Borders.

S2W-27880 Christine Grahame: To ask the Scottish Executive what advice there was prior to July 2006 for individuals and businesses who work with animal hides in relation to the potential risk from anthrax.

S2W-27883 Christine Grahame: To ask the Scottish Executive how many laboratory facilities there are in Scotland to test for anthrax and where they are located.

S2W-27884 Christine Grahame: To ask the Scottish Executive on how many occasions since 1999 officials from its Justice Department with responsibility for civil contingencies planning have met to discuss plans in the event of possible accidental leakage of chemical weapons disposed of in Beaufort’s Dyke and what the worst estimates are of the number of likely casualties as a result of such an accidental discharge.

S2W-27885 Christine Grahame: To ask the Scottish Executive on how many occasions since 1999 officials from its Justice Department with responsibility for civil contingencies planning have met to discuss plans in the event of a possible accidental explosion of nuclear weapons stored in Scotland and what the worst estimates are for the number of likely casualties as a result of such an accident.

S2W-27887 Mr Kenny MacAskill: To ask the Scottish Executive what information it has on the contribution to the Scottish economy of workers from the A8 countries.

S2W-27888 Mr Kenny MacAskill: To ask the Scottish Executive what information it has on the skills possessed by workers from the A8 countries who have entered the Scottish job market.

S2W-27889 Mr Kenny MacAskill: To ask the Scottish Executive what information it has on employment levels amongst migrants to Scotland from the A8 countries.

S2W-27890 Mr Kenny MacAskill: To ask the Scottish Executive what information it has on the age profile of migrants to Scotland from the A8 countries.

S2W-27891 Mr Kenny MacAskill: To ask the Scottish Executive what its position is on whether Bulgarian and Romanian citizens should be allowed access to the Scottish job market if the two countries enter the European Union in 2007.

S2W-27892 Mr Kenny MacAskill: To ask the Scottish Executive what discussions it has had with the UK Government concerning the decision over whether to allow Bulgarian and Romanian citizens access to the UK job market if the two countries enter the EU in 2007 and whether it will provide details of these discussions.
