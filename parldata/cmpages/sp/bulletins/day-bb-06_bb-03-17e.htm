Business Bulletin No.46/2006: Friday 17 March 2006
Contents An A B C D E F G H I J

Section E – Written questions lodged on 16 March 2006

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S2W-24334 Shiona Baird: To ask the Scottish Executive what the reasons were for the establishment of Forward Scotland.

S2W-24335 Shiona Baird: To ask the Scottish Executive who appoints the directors of Forward Scotland.

S2W-24336 Shiona Baird: To ask the Scottish Executive what the sources are of Forward Scotland’s income and what proportion of it is derived from the Executive or its agencies.

S2W-24337 Shiona Baird: To ask the Scottish Executive what measures are in place to ensure that its grants, or other payments, to Forward Scotland are subject to ministerial and parliamentary scrutiny.

S2W-24338 Shiona Baird: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it gives any guidance to Forward Scotland in relation to the operation of its functions.

S2W-24339 Shiona Baird: To ask the Scottish Executive under what legislation it established Forward Scotland and whether it operates as a public body in respect of general duties to protect the environment.

S2W-24340 Shiona Baird: To ask the Scottish Executive to whom Forward Scotland is accountable for the expenditure of funds provided by the Executive.

S2W-24341 Shiona Baird: To ask the Scottish Executive what proportion of funding provided by it to Forward Scotland was spent on administration in each year since 1996 and what measures it has in place to ensure that this is comparable to the proportion of funding spent on administration by public bodies.

S2W-24342 Shiona Baird: To ask the Scottish Executive what activities it funds Forward Scotland to carry out and what its reasons are for not selecting public bodies to carry out these functions.

S2W-24343 Murdo Fraser: To ask the Scottish Executive whether there are any ongoing teacher training programmes to enable teachers to recognise the symptoms of Tourette Syndrome or associated disorders in children.

S2W-24344 Murdo Fraser: To ask the Scottish Executive how many patients have been diagnosed with Tourette Syndrome in each of the last five years, broken down by NHS board.

S2W-24345 Murdo Fraser: To ask the Scottish Executive what information is available for teachers and parents to help them to better understand Tourette Syndrome.

S2W-24346 Mrs Nanette Milne: To ask the Scottish Executive how many individuals are awaiting a community care needs assessment in each local authority area.

S2W-24347 Mrs Nanette Milne: To ask the Scottish Executive what the average waiting time has been for a community care needs assessment in each year since the introduction of free personal care, broken down by local authority area.

S2W-24348 Mrs Nanette Milne: To ask the Scottish Executive what the average time has been between individuals’ community care needs assessments and receipt of payments towards personal care in each year since the introduction of free personal care, broken down by local authority area.

S2W-24349 Brian Adam: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it has any concerns about NHS boards seeking to "poach" nursing staff from other NHS boards within Scotland.

S2W-24350 Euan Robson: To ask the Scottish Executive when it will announce or confirm the budget for the Scottish Community and Householder Renewables Initiative for (a) 2006-07 and (b) 2007-08.

S2W-24351 Bruce Crawford: To ask the Scottish Executive what tonnage of radioactive waste was stored in Scotland at 31 December (a) 2005 and (b) 1955.

S2W-24352 Bruce Crawford: To ask the Scottish Executive what tonnage of radioactive waste was stored in Scotland at 31 December 2005, broken down into (a) high-level, (b) intermediate-level and (c) low-level waste.

S2W-24353 Bruce Crawford: To ask the Scottish Executive what tonnage of (a) high-level, (b) intermediate-level and (c) low-level radioactive waste is stored in Scotland, broken down by location.

S2W-24354 Bruce Crawford: To ask the Scottish Executive what capacity is available for the storage of (a) high-level, (b) intermediate-level and (c) low-level radioactive waste, broken down by location.

S2W-24355 Bruce Crawford: To ask the Scottish Executive what specific reports it has commissioned into the future of the Forth Road Bridge; what the remit was of each report; when each report was commissioned, and when the results will be made public.

S2W-24356 Stewart Stevenson: To ask the Scottish Executive when the Minister for Environment and Rural Development will reply to Alex Salmond MP’s letter dated 2 February 2006 regarding Macduff Harbour Development.

S2W-24357 Margo MacDonald: To ask the Scottish Parliamentary Corporate Body whether an appropriate defects liability certificate for the Holyrood complex has been issued and, if so, by whom and on what date the certificate was issued.

S2W-24358 Margo MacDonald: To ask the Scottish Parliamentary Corporate Body on which of the contracts for the Holyrood complex retentions are still being held.

S2W-24359 Margo MacDonald: To ask the Scottish Parliamentary Corporate Body whether the status of the assurance given to the Finance Committee by the Clerk/Chief Executive on 17 December 2002 (Official Report c. 2433) in respect of the public indemnity insurance on the Holyrood project principally held by RMJM is unchanged from that described by him at that time.

S2W-24360 Margo MacDonald: To ask the Scottish Parliamentary Corporate Body from which companies contracted to work on the Holyrood project the Parliament is time barred from claiming damages, or cannot do so for any other reason.

S2W-24361 Margaret Smith: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it considers that handheld radar speed guns are a fully reliable means of measuring the speed of motorcycles.

S2W-24362 Margaret Smith: To ask the Scottish Executive whether safety camera partnerships should be required to cross-check speed camera photographic evidence before taking action against motorcyclists suspected of speeding.

S2W-24363 Margaret Smith: To ask the Scottish Executive whether motorists accused of speeding should be allowed automatic access to the evidence against them held by a safety camera partnership.

S2W-24364 John Swinburne: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-21188 by Malcolm Chisholm on 5 December 2005, whether it has concluded its consultation on the Executive’s fuel poverty programmes and, if so, what plans it has to increase the maximum grant entitlement under the Warm Deal scheme.

S2W-24365 John Swinburne: To ask the Scottish Executive how many complaints of (a) abuse, (b) neglect and (c) malnourishment of older people in care homes have been made to the Care Commission in each year since it was established.

S2W-24366 John Swinburne: To ask the Scottish Executive how many complaints made to the Care Commission have resulted in formal enforcement action being taken in each year since it was established.

S2W-24367 John Swinburne: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will consider introducing legislation to give the Care Commission a statutory duty to inform local authorities of (a) all complaints made against care homes within their areas and (b) the outcomes of the investigations of such complaints.

S2W-24368 Derek Brownlee: To ask the Scottish Executive how many copies of Supporting People Matters - Issue 20 were printed; to which individuals and organisations they were distributed, and what the total cost was of (a) printing, (b) distribution and (c) any other expenditure associated with the publication.

S2W-24369 Bruce Crawford: To ask the Scottish Executive what discussions have been held with EWS about it giving up guaranteed space on the Forth Rail Bridge as a result of the Stirling-Alloa-Kincardine rail link.

S2W-24370 Bruce Crawford: To ask the Scottish Executive what specific instructions or guidance it will issue to Transport Scotland with regard to the planning of a potential new crossing across the River Forth.

S2W-24371 Bruce Crawford: To ask the Scottish Executive what tonnage of low-level radioactive waste has been produced in Scotland each year since 1999 and what locations were the source of this waste.

S2W-24372 Bruce Crawford: To ask the Scottish Executive what tonnage of intermediate-level radioactive waste has been produced in Scotland in each year since 1999 and what locations were the source of this waste.

S2W-24373 Bruce Crawford: To ask the Scottish Executive what tonnage of high-level radio active waste has been produced in Scotland in each year since 1999 and what locations were the source of this waste.

S2W-24374 Bruce Crawford: To ask the Scottish Executive what the total sum of European Structural Fund spending was in Scotland in each financial year since 1999, broken down by programme.

S2W-24375 Bruce Crawford: To ask the Scottish Executive what it estimates that the total sum of European Structural Fund spending will be in Scotland in financial years 2005-06 and 2006-07, broken down by programme.

S2W-24376 Ms Rosemary Byrne: To ask the Scottish Executive what progress it is making in increasing the number of foster carers across Scotland.

S2W-24377 Ms Rosemary Byrne: To ask the Scottish Executive how it plans to spend the underspend in its foster care budget.

S2W-24378 Ms Rosemary Byrne: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it has to produce a protocol for foster carers.

S2W-24379 Mr Jamie McGrigor: To ask the Scottish Executive which bodies are responsible for the marking of underwater hazards such as rocks, reefs and crannogs in lochs Lomond, Ness and Awe and other freshwater lochs.

S2W-24380 Mr Mark Ruskell: To ask the Scottish Executive how much public money has been spent on the Uist Wader Project in (a) total and (b) each year since 2000.

S2W-24381 Mr Mark Ruskell: To ask the Scottish Executive how many hedgehogs have been killed under the Uist Wader Project in (a) total and (b) each year since 2000, broken down by island.

S2W-24382 Mr Mark Ruskell: To ask the Scottish Executive how many hedgehogs the Uist Wader Project estimates remain on each island.

S2W-24383 Mr Mark Ruskell: To ask the Scottish Executive whether the Uist Wader Project plans to kill hedgehogs in autumn 2006 and, if so, by what methods and in what locations.

S2W-24384 Mr Mark Ruskell: To ask the Scottish Executive when the eradication of hedgehogs from the Uists and Benbecula is expected to be completed.

S2W-24385 Mr Mark Ruskell: To ask the Scottish Executive what the current estimate is for the total cost of completing the eradication of hedgehogs from the Uists and Benbecula.

S2W-24386 Mr Mark Ruskell: To ask the Scottish Executive how many hedgehogs killed under the Uist Wader Project were found to have the remains of bird eggs or chicks in their systems.

S2W-24387 Mr Mark Ruskell: To ask the Scottish Executive how many hedgehogs killed under the Uist Wader Project were found to be pregnant and at what stages of pregnancy.

S2W-24388 Mr Mark Ruskell: To ask the Scottish Executive what funding will be provided for scientific research to investigate Scottish Natural Heritage’s concerns about the animal welfare consequences of relocating hedgehogs from the Uists.
