Business Bulletin No.45/2006: Thursday 16 March 2006
Contents An A B C D E F G H I J

Section D - Oral questions selected for answer on 23 March 2006

Question Time

General

1. Donald Gorrie: To ask the Scottish Executive what enquiries it has made to ascertain whether the voluntary sector believes that the Executive is fulfilling the commitments made in The Scottish Compact. (S2O-9427)

2. Fiona Hyslop: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will transfer responsibility for Careers Scotland from Scottish Enterprise to align it with the Executive’s Education Department in order to promote earlier intervention with pupils to address Scotland’s record of having the highest percentage in OECD-surveyed countries of 16 to 19-year-olds not in education, employment or training. (S2O-9422)

3. Paul Martin: To ask the Scottish Executive how many interim anti-social behaviour orders in Glasgow have been applied for and granted by the courts. (S2O-9381)

4. Mike Rumbles: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will consider granting rights of appeal under the Planning etc. (Scotland) Bill to individuals who are in receipt of a neighbour notification as a result of a planning application. (S2O-9424)

5. Mr Alasdair Morrison: To ask the Scottish Executive what progress has been made on the introduction of a social aid package that will benefit Scotland’s island residents by reducing the cost of air travel between Scottish islands and the mainland. (S2O-9389)

6. Not Lodged

7. Tommy Sheridan: To ask the Scottish Executive how many local authority and housing association homes have been (a) sold and (b) built in Glasgow, and across Scotland, in the last five years. (S2O-9411)

8. Mr Charlie Gordon: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will consider providing additional financial assistance to local authorities where strategic rationalisation of schools’ provision is ongoing in order to ease pressures on planned maintenance budgets for other schools in such areas where no rationalisation is possible. (S2O-9373)

9. Mr John Swinney: To ask the Scottish Executive what progress it is making in improving energy efficiency. (S2O-9347)

10. Kate Maclean: To ask the Scottish Executive what steps have been taken through the planning system since 1999 to increase the amount of public open space. (S2O-9379)

Health and Community Care

1. Dr Elaine Murray: To ask the Scottish Executive what arrangements should be put in place before elderly patients are discharged from hospital and which agencies are responsible for ensuring that adequate home care is provided. (S2O-9392)

2. Mr David Davidson: To ask the Scottish Executive on what date all local authorities will be in a position to fully deliver free personal care for elderly people. (S2O-9359)

3. Derek Brownlee: To ask the Scottish Executive whether the section of the Partnership Agreement relating to health will have been implemented in full by 2007. (S2O-9352)

4. Rob Gibson: To ask the Scottish Executive when the Minister for Health and Community Care last met the Chairman and Chief Executive of NHS Western Isles and what issues they discussed. (S2O-9349)

5. Margaret Jamieson: To ask the Scottish Executive what advice has been issued to NHS boards regarding the prescribing of Herceptin. (S2O-9377)

6. Bill Butler: To ask the Scottish Executive what efforts it is making to increase the level of public participation in the NHS. (S2O-9367)

7. Jackie Baillie: To ask the Scottish Executive what progress is being made with the development of a new Vale of Leven medical centre and a new Garelochhead medical centre. (S2O-9363)

8. Richard Lochhead: To ask the Scottish Executive what safeguards are in place to ensure that all areas receive adequate ambulance cover. (S2O-9345)

9. Dennis Canavan: To ask the Scottish Executive what measures it is taking to prevent skin cancer. (S2O-9351)

10. Mr Jim Wallace: To ask the Scottish Executive what the total cost was to the Scottish Ambulance Service in 2005 of hiring Ministry of Defence or Coastguard Service helicopters to undertake air ambulance missions (a) in the whole of Scotland and (b) originating from Orkney. (S2O-9426)

Environment and Rural Development

1. Iain Smith: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will make a submission to the Competition Commission inquiry into the grocery market in respect of relationships between Scottish farmers and major supermarkets. (S2O-9429)

2. Jim Mather: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will make a more substantial response than it has to date to the criticisms of Scottish Water’s financial strategy and the role of the Executive and the Water Industry Commissioner in framing that strategy that have been published by Jim and Margaret Cuthbert in their recent paper. (S2O-9344)

3. Murdo Fraser: To ask the Scottish Executive when it expects to appoint a new Chairman of Scottish Water. (S2O-9361)

4. Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive, in the context of its Rural Development Strategy, what role rural postal services play in promoting a more diverse rural economy and thriving rural communities. (S2O-9423)

5. Bruce Crawford: To ask the Scottish Executive what capacity exists for the storage of additional high, intermediate and low-level radioactive waste in Scotland, expressed in tonnes for each category. (S2O-9412)

6. Rosie Kane: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it has to introduce a scheme to clean up toxic waste disposal sites. (S2O-9410)

7. Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive what action it is taking to address issues of animal cruelty. (S2O-9393)

8. Carolyn Leckie: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it has to initiate an investigation into the link between landfill sites and environmental pollution and, in particular, whether there is a relationship with birth defects. (S2O-9397)

9. Bill Aitken: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it has any plans to bring forward the planned test of contingency planning measures as the threat from avian flu increases. (S2O-9358)

10. Nora Radcliffe: To ask the Scottish Executive when it expects to publish a consultation on environmental courts. (S2O-9430)
