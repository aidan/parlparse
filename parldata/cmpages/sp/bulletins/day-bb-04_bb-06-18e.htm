Friday 18th June 2004

Section E : Written questions lodged on Thursday 17th June 2004
  

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S2W-9038 Eleanor Scott: To ask the Scottish Executive how many responses it has had to its consultation on the European Commission's consultation on its chemical regulation, the Regulation, Evaluation and Authorisation of Chemicals (REACH).

*The member has provided the following Gaelic translation:

A dh' fhaighneachd de Riaghaltas na h-Alba co mheud freagairt agus a fhuair a obair cho-chomhairleachaidh air obair cho-chomhairleachaidh Coimisean na h-Eòrpa air an riaghladh aige air ceimigean, Riaghladh, Measadh agus Ùghdarachadh Cheimigean (REACH).*
