Business Bulletin No. 102 / 2004

Tuesday 15th June 2004

Section D : Oral questions selected for answer on Thursday 17th June 2004
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive's cabinet. (S2F-922)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he next plans to meet the Prime Minister and what issues he intends to raise. (S2F-928)

3. Tommy Sheridan: To ask the First Minister what the main priorities are for discussion at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive's Cabinet. (S2F-940)

4. Richard Baker: To ask the First Minister how the new ScotRail franchise will help develop an integrated transport system. (S2F-931)

5. Murdo Fraser: To ask the First Minister what discussions the Scottish Executive has had with Her Majesty's Government in respect of the G8 summit at Gleneagles in 2005. (S2F-924)

6. Donald Gorrie: To ask the First Minister whether the Scottish Executive will take steps to address the impact of marches on local people, the police and community relations. (S2F-942)
