Business Bulletin No.127/2001

Tuesday 11 September 2001

Section D : Oral Questions
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister when he next plans to meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he intends to discuss. (S1F-1221)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Prime Minister and what issues he will raise. (S1F-1223)

3. Cathie Craigie: To ask the First Minister what progress is being made in implementing the provisions of the Housing (Scotland) Act 2001. (S1F-1231)

4. Mr Kenneth Gibson: To ask the First Minister what the remit of the Minister for Social Justice will be in relation to asylum seekers. (S1F-1228)

5. Mr Jamie Stone: To ask the First Minister what recent discussions it has had with Her Majesty's Government regarding a Bill to ban tobacco advertising. (S1F-1237)

6. Phil Gallie: To ask the First Minister whether the Scottish Executive's draft budget for 2003-04 will require any use of its tax-varying power. (S1F-1219)
