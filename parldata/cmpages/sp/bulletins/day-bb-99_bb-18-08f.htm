BUSINESS BULLETIN 40/1999

Wednesday 18 August 1999

Section F – Motions and amendments lodged between 13 August and 17 August 1999 

 

The full text of all outstanding motions and amendments will appear in the Business Bulletin every Monday.

Items or names marked with an asterisk (*) are new. Asterisks in the text show the extent of alterations made.

Motions which members wish to be considered for debate as members’ business in the Parliament are marked with a hash symbol (#).

 

*S1M-93 Tavish Scott: Cutty Sark Tallships Race in Lerwick— That the Parliament congratulates Sail Shetland; Lerwick Port Authority; the sponsors; Balta-sail, and all involved in Shetland’s hosting of the Cutty Sark Tall Ships’ Race in Lerwick between 9-12 August; notes that the event was a showcase for Shetland’s indigenous industries: seafood; agriculture; knitwear; crafts; tourism, and for the exceptional abilities of Shetland’s musicians; and recognises that above all the event was a magnificent illustration of how a small island community can work together to present a positive, exciting and vibrant event to the outside world.

 

S1M-91 One Hundreth Anniversary of the Founding of Albion Motors (lodged on 11 August) Alasdair Morgan*
