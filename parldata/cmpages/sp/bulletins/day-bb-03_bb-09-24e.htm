Business Bulletin No. 125 / 2003

Wednesday 24th September 2003

Section E : Written questions lodged on Tuesday 23rd September 2003
  

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S2W-2867 Tommy Sheridan: To ask the Scottish Executive what progress it has made in meeting its target to reduce persistent young offending by 10% by 2006.

S2W-2868 Tommy Sheridan: To ask the Scottish Executive whether its proposed expenditure on 20 mph zones around schools will allow every primary school to secure a 20 mph limit and, if not, what additional expenditure would be required to achieve this objective.

S2W-2869 Tommy Sheridan: To ask the Scottish Executive how much it will allocate to fire service training for each of the next three years.

S2W-2870 Tommy Sheridan: To ask the Scottish Executive whether provision for construction of the M74 is included in its budget for motorways and trunk roads for the next three years and over how many years the total expenditure on construction of the M74 will be spread.

S2W-2871 Tommy Sheridan: To ask the Scottish Executive by how much the legal aid budget will be increased in each of the next three years, expressed also as a percentage.

S2W-2872 Tommy Sheridan: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it has set any targets for the seizure of Class A drugs by weight over the next three years and, if so, what these targets are.

S2W-2873 Tommy Sheridan: To ask the Scottish Executive how many eviction actions have been raised in each court in each of the last three years and how many evictions subsequently took place.

S2W-2874 Tommy Sheridan: To ask the Scottish Executive how many individuals were (a) affected by and (b) became homeless as a result of eviction actions raised, broken down by court, in each of the last three years.

S2W-2875 Stewart Stevenson: To ask the Scottish Executive how many NHS staff have contracted hepatitis C (a) during the course of their work and (b) in total in each year for which figures are available, broken down by staff group.

S2W-2876 Mr Brian Monteith: To ask the Scottish Executive whether sufficient funding is given to Stirling Council to maintain the road infrastructure in its area.

S2W-2877 Mr Brian Monteith: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it has to improve safety measures on the A84 and A85 between Stirling and Tyndrum.

S2W-2878 Mr Adam Ingram: To ask the Scottish Executive what the current timetable is for the second stage of the review of initial teacher education.

S2W-2879 David McLetchie: To ask the Scottish Executive how many of the 239 prisoners recalled from licence in 2002, as referred to in Prison Statistics Scotland 2002, were recalled for committing a crime and what the categories of those crimes were.

S2W-2880 Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive what action it is taking to deliver new routes from Glasgow Airport.

S2W-2883 Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive how much business is generated for the Scottish economy by (a) Glasgow and (b) Edinburgh.

S2W-2884 Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive how many people are employed (a) directly and (b) indirectly in (i) Glasgow Airport and (ii) Edinburgh Airport.

S2W-2885 Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it has carried out any research into the most effective use of the Route Development Fund.

S2W-2886 Donald Gorrie: To ask the Scottish Executive what proposals it has to introduce legislation to permit class actions.
