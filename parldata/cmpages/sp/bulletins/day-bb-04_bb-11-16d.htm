Business Bulletin No. 166/2004

Tuesday 16 November 2004

Section D : Oral questions selected for answer on 18 November 2004
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Nicola Sturgeon: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive’s cabinet. (S2F-1204)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Prime Minister and what issues will be discussed. (S2F-1201)

3. Shiona Baird: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he intends to discuss. (S2F-1206)

4. Christine May: To ask the First Minister what effect a community right of appeal would have on new building developments and planning applications to local authorities. (S2F-1208)

5. Nora Radcliffe: To ask the First Minister what the Scottish Executive’s position is on the fluoridation of public water supplies. (S2F-1212)

6. Mr David Davidson: To ask the First Minister how many cases of hospital-acquired infection there are and what targets the Scottish Executive has set for its control. (S2F-1215)
