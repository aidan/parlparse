BUSINESS BULLETIN 48 / 2001

Tuesday 20 March 2001

Section D - Oral questions selected for answer on 22 March 2001

 

 

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister when he next plans to meet the Prime Minister and what issues he intends to raise. (S1F-941)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when the Scottish Executive’s Cabinet will next meet and what issues will be discussed. (S1F-934)

3. David Mundell: To ask the First Minister what progress has been made in implementing the proposals for the eradication of foot and mouth disease announced by the Minister for Rural Development on 15 March 2001. (S1F-938)

4. Mr Alex Salmond: To ask the First Minister what the Scottish Executive’s current position is in relation to the volume of juvenile haddock being discarded at sea. (S1F-946)

5. Marilyn Livingstone: To ask the First Minister what progress is being made in widening access to further education. (S1F-936)

6. Michael Russell: To ask the First Minister, further to the answer to question S1W-14238 by Mr Sam Galbraith on 16 March 2001, what increase in funding there has been for Scottish Opera between the previously projected allocations for the years 2000-01 to 2003-04 and the amounts now allocated. (S1F-931)
