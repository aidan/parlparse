Business Bulletin No. 145/2002

Tuesday 8 October 2002

Section D : Oral Questions selected for answer on 10 October 2002
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Prime Minister and what issues he intends to raise. (S1F-2155)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he next plans to meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he intends to raise. (S1F-2162)

3. Bruce Crawford: To ask the First Minister whether the Scottish Executive will ensure that conventional crops within the immediate vicinity of GM crop trial sites are tested for contamination by GM materials. (S1F-2156)

4. Sarah Boyack: To ask the First Minister whether the Scottish Executive plans to take any action in the light of the suggestion of the Association of Scottish Police Superintendents regarding begging. (S1F-2170)

5. Nora Radcliffe: To ask the First Minister what guidance the Scottish Executive issues to planning authorities in respect of the consideration of proposals for wind farms. (S1F-2168)

6. Murdo Fraser: To ask the First Minister what support the Scottish Executive will give to the development of Gaelic-medium schools. (S1F-2157)
