Business Bulletin No. 12 / 2004

Tuesday 20th January 2004

Section D : Oral questions selected for answer on Thursday 22nd January 2004
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister when he next plans to meet the Prime Minister and what issues he intends to raise. (S2F-544)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive's Cabinet. (S2F-549)

3. Tommy Sheridan: To ask the First Minister what issues the Scottish Executive's Cabinet will prioritise for discussion and action over the next month. (S2F-558)

4. Christine May: To ask the First Minister what discussions the Scottish Executive has had with the Home Secretary regarding the possibility of a "Scottish Visa" to encourage overseas students to stay in Scotland after graduation. (S2F-555)

5. David Mundell: To ask the First Minister what impact Her Majesty's Government's proposed changes to the regulation of the rail industry will have on rail services in Scotland. (S2F-546)

6. John Farquhar Munro: To ask the First Minister whether the Scottish Executive will support a system of organ donation which requires individuals to opt out. (S2F-561)
