Business Bulletin No. 120/2002

Tuesday 3 September 2002

Section D : Oral Questions selected for answer on 5 September 2002
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive's Cabinet. (S1F-2046)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister whether he has any plans to reshuffle his Cabinet. (S1F-2049)

3. Richard Lochhead: To ask the First Minister what the outcome will be of his visit to the sustainable development summit in South Africa. (S1F-2038)

4. Margaret Jamieson: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive will ensure that public safety is paramount in all decisions relating to the Scottish Prison Service Estates Review. (S1F-2051)

5. Lord James Douglas-Hamilton: To ask the First Minister what consideration the Scottish Executive will give to an amendment to the Criminal Justice (Scotland) Bill to allow prosecutors a statutory right to be heard on behalf of the public interest before convicted prisoners, including those convicted of rape, serious assault, murder or culpable homicide are released. R (S1F-2033)

6. Pauline McNeill: To ask the First Minister what priority the Scottish Executive gives to healthy eating. (S1F-2057)
