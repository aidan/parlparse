Tuesday 4 November 2003

Section A : Daily Business List
  

Meetings of Committees

All meetings take place in Committee Chambers, Edinburgh unless otherwise indicated.

Equal Opportunities Committee

6th Meeting, 2003

The Committee will meet at 10.00 am in Committee Room 1

1. Items in private: The Committee will consider whether to take items 3 and 4 in private.

2. Civil partnership registration: The Committee will hear evidence from:

Panel 1

Mr John Deighan, Parliamentary Officer, Catholic Church in Scotland

Panel 2

4. Budget process 2004-05: The Committee will consider a draft report.

Finance Committee

11th Meeting, 2003

The Committee will meet at 10.00 am in The Chamber, Assembly Hall, The Mound, Edinburgh

1. Work programme: The Committee will consider its future work programme.

2. Cross-Cutting Expenditure Review on Economic Development: The Committee will consider its approach to the review.

3. Criminal Procedure (Amendment) (Scotland) Bill: The Committee will take evidence on the Bill's Financial Memorandum from-

John Ewing, Chief Executive and John Anderson, Principal Clerk of the Session and Justiciary, Scottish Court Service.

Norman McFadyen, Crown Agent and Keith Connal, Business Manager, Crown Office and Procurator Fiscal Service.

Douglas Haggarty, Head of Legal Services (Technical), Scottish Legal Aid Board.

4. Scottish Water: The Committee will consider a position paper collated by Scottish Water.

6th Meeting, 2003

Alasdair Morgan, Group Convener, Scottish National Party;

Bill Aitken, Business Manager, Scottish Conservative and Unionist Party;

Subordinate Legislation Committee

11th Meeting, 2003

The Committee will meet at 10.30 am in Committee Room 3

1. Delegated powers scrutiny: The Committee will consider the response from the Scottish Executive to points raised on the following Bill-

the National Health Service Reform (Scotland) Bill at Stage 1.

2. Executive responses: The Committee will consider responses from the Scottish Executive to points raised on the following-

the Civil Legal Aid (Scotland) Amendment (No.2) Regulations 2003, (SSI 2003/486).

3. Draft instruments subject to approval: The Committee will consider the following-

the Budget (Scotland) Act 2003 Amendment (No.2) Order 2003, (draft).

4. Instruments subject to approval: The Committee will consider the following-

the Food Protection (Emergency Prohibitions) (Amnesic Shellfish Poisoning) (Orkney) (No.4) (Scotland) Order 2003, (SSI 2002/501).

5. Instruments subject to annulment: The Committee will consider the following-

the Horticultural Produce (Community Grading Rules) (Scotland) Regulations 2003, (SSI 2003/502)

the Road Traffic Act 1991 (Special Parking Area) (Scotland) Order 2003, (SSI 2003/508)

the Road Works (Sharing of Costs of Works) (Scotland) Regulations 2003, (SSI 2003/509)

the Scottish Legal Aid Board (Employment of Solicitors to Provide Criminal Legal Assistance) Amendment Regulations 2003, (SSI 2003/511)

the Road Works (Reinstatement) (Scotland) Amendment (No.2) Regulations 2003, (SSI 2003/512)

the Inshore Fishing (Prohibition of Fishing and Fishing Methods) (Scotland) Amendment (No.2) Order 2003, (SSI 2003/514).
