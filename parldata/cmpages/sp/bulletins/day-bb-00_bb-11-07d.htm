BUSINESS BULLETIN 168 / 2000

Tuesday 7 November 2000

Section D - Oral questions selected for answer on 9 November 2000

 

 

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister what the Scottish Executive’s main priorities currently are. (S1F-637)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when the Scottish Executive’s Cabinet will next meet and what issues will be discussed. (S1F-643)

3. Donald Gorrie: To ask the First Minister what measures are already in place to prevent flood damage in Scotland this winter and what further action it intends to take in this respect. (S1F-644)

4. Andrew Wilson: To ask the First Minister what representations the Scottish Executive has made to the Chancellor of the Exchequer in advance of the Green Budget regarding issues which impact on devolved matters. (S1F-641)

5. Dr Elaine Murray: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive intends to take forward economic development at a local level. (S1F-650)

6. Mr Lloyd Quinan: To ask the First Minister what progress has been made on the levying of a fee on voluntary organisations for Scottish Criminal Records Office checks. (S1F-649)
