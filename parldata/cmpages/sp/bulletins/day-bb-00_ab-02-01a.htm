BUSINESS BULLETIN 18 / 2000

Tuesday 1 February 2000

Section A – Daily Business List

 

 

Meetings of Committees

All meetings take place in Committee Chambers, Edinburgh unless otherwise indicated.

 

Justice and Home Affairs Committee

4th Meeting, 2000

The Committee will meet at 9.30 am in the Chamber, Assembly Hall, the Mound

Adults with Incapacity (Scotland) Bill: The Committee will consider the Bill at Stage 2 (Day 3).

Note: If the Committee does not complete consideration of Parts 4 and 6 of the Bill by the end of this meeting, it will meet again on Wednesday 2 February, at either 9.30 am or 10.30 am, for that purpose.

 

Finance Committee

2nd Meeting, 2000

The Committee will meet at 9.45 am in Committee Room 1

1. Determination of Accounting Policies: The Committee will consider the memorandum from the Minister for Finance on the determination of accounting policies.

2. Inquiry into the Finance Functions of the Scottish Executive: The Committee will consider the revised remit and preliminary programme for the inquiry.

3. Future Inquiries: The Committee will consider topics for a future inquiry by the Committee.

4. Annual Budget Process: The Committee will consider a revised framework for the approach subject committees should take at Stage 1 of the annual budget process.

5. Deputy Convener: The Committee will choose a Deputy Convener.

6. Annual Budget Process: The Committee will consider the appointment of advisers.

7. Committee Procedure: The Committee will consider how it wishes to handle agenda items 9 and 10.

Not before 11.30 am

8. Budget (Scotland) Bill: The Convener to move: That the Finance Committee consider the Budget (Scotland) Bill at Stage 2 in the order in which the provisions arise in the Bill except that each schedule be considered immediately after the section that introduces it.

9. Budget (Scotland) Bill: The Committee will consider the Bill at Stage 2

10. The draft Scotland Act 1998 (Transitory and Transitional Provisions) (Appropriations) Amendment Order 2000: The Committee will debate a motion in the name of Mr Jack McConnell
