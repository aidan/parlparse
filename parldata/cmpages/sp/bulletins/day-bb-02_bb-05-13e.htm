Business Bulletin No. 77/2002

Monday 13 May 2002

Section E : Written questions lodged on 10 May 2002
  

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S1W-25714 Andrew Wilson: To ask the Scottish Executive whether any financial irregularities or budgetary losses were identified during the annual audit of departments and divisions in each of the last four years; if so, in which department or division each such loss or irregularity was identified, and what the amount involved was, also expressed as a percentage of the division or department's annual budget.
