Business Bulletin No. 185/2004

Tuesday 14 December 2004

Section D : Oral questions selected for answer on 16 December 2004
  

First Minister’s Question Time

1. Nicola Sturgeon: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive’s Cabinet. (S2F-1291)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Prime Minister and what issues will be discussed. (S2F-1292)

3. Shiona Baird: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he intends to discuss. (S2F-1295)

4. Mrs Mary Mulligan: To ask the First Minister whether the Scottish Executive still intends to introduce a comprehensive ban on smoking in public places. (S2F-1304)

5. Alasdair Morgan: To ask the First Minister what recent discussions the Scottish Executive has had regarding the performance of NHS accident and emergency units. (S2F-1298)

6. Margo MacDonald: To ask the First Minister what action the Scottish Executive plans to take on the findings and recommendations of the Expert Group on Prostitution’s Stage 1 report. (S2F-1308)
