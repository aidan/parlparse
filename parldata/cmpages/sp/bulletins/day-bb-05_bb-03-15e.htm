Section E – Written questions lodged on 14 March 2005

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S2W-15231 Alex Fergusson: To ask the Scottish Executive who has the authority to change the rules of the Hospital Travel Costs Scheme in Scotland.

S2W-15232Δ Karen Whitefield: To ask the Scottish Executive what action it is taking to support financial education.

S2W-15233 Mr Kenny MacAskill: To ask the Scottish Executive what estimate it has made of the extent of the skills shortage, broken down by sector.

S2W-15234 Mr Kenny MacAskill: To ask the Scottish Executive what percentage of the skills shortage is estimated to be in the "low-skilled" category.

S2W-15235 Mr Kenny MacAskill: To ask the Scottish Executive what funding it has provided for English language classes for immigrants in each year since 1999.

S2W-15236 Mr Kenny MacAskill: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-14734 by Mr Tom McCabe on 10 March 2005, what the nature and cost was of all the activities undertaken to promote Scotland as a place to work in the 10 EU accession states, broken down by country in which each activity took place.

S2W-15237 Mr Kenny MacAskill: To ask the Scottish Executive how many enquiries have been made to the Relocation Advisory Service since it was opened, broken down by (a) month and (b) country of origin.

S2W-15238 Mr Kenny MacAskill: To ask the Scottish Executive, with regard to article 2 of the Proposal for a European Council Decision on the exchange of information extracted from the criminal record (COM (2004) 664 final), what central authority it expects will be designated in respect of the exchange of Scottish criminal records.

S2W-15239 Mr David Davidson: To ask the Scottish Executive when it will publish a strategy on the role of community hospitals.

S2W-15240 Mr David Davidson: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it has to assess the opportunities offered by community hospitals to deliver more health care within communities.

S2W-15241 Mr David Davidson: To ask the Scottish Executive how many community hospitals there have been in each of the last four years, broken down by NHS board, and what projections it has made in respect of the number of community hospitals there will be in the next three years.

S2W-15242 Mr David Davidson: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it has to modernise community hospitals.

S2W-15243 Mr David Davidson: To ask the Scottish Executive whether NHS boards receive specific funding to operate community hospitals.

S2W-15244 Fiona Hyslop: To ask the Scottish Executive how many colleges have registered for the Individual Learning Accounts scheme since its relaunch.

S2W-15245 Alex Neil: To ask the Scottish Executive what the average cost of a single school meal under its free school meals programme was in each of the last five years and what the projected average cost will be for each of the next five years.

S2W-15246 Alex Neil: To ask the Scottish Executive what the total cost was of its free school meals programme in each of the last five years; what the projected total cost is for each of the next five years, and what the cost would be if the take-up rate was 100% of pupils entitled to a free school meal.

S2W-15247 Alex Neil: To ask the Scottish Executive how many primary and secondary pupils are entitled to free school meals, expressed also as a percentage of the total school population.

S2W-15248 Alex Neil: To ask the Scottish Executive how it will encourage a greater take-up rate of free school meals amongst those pupils entitled to them.

S2W-15249 Alex Neil: To ask the Scottish Executive how many primary and secondary pupils have been enrolled in schools in each of the last five years and what the estimated number is for each of the next five years.

S2W-15250 Alex Neil: To ask the Scottish Executive how much income local authorities made from the sale of school meals in each of the last five years and what the projected income from schools meals is for local authorities in each of the next five years.

S2W-15251 Alex Neil: To ask the Scottish Executive what proportion of its block grant was spent on providing free school meals in each of the last five years and what proportion it estimates will be spent on free school meals in each of the next five years.

S2W-15252 Phil Gallie: To ask the Scottish Executive what rules are now in place to govern administrative penalties, as incorporated into the December 2004 EU Fisheries Council agreement.

S2W-15254 Phil Gallie: To ask the Scottish Executive what the cost in legal aid has been of appeals against minimum sentences to be served by life prisoners in each of the last four years and what estimates have been made of the legal aid costs of such appeals in each of the next three years.

S2W-15255 Phil Gallie: To ask the Scottish Executive how many (a) male and (b) female life prisoners are currently serving their sentence and what average length of time such prisoners are set to serve.

S2W-15256 Dennis Canavan: To ask the Scottish Executive how many, and what percentage of, S1 and S2 English and mathematics classes have 30 or more pupils, broken down by local authority area.

S2W-15257 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-14071 by Malcolm Chisholm on 14 February 2005, what percentage of people lived in overcrowded housing in (a) each year before 1999 for which figures are available and (b) 2004, broken down by local authority area.

S2W-15259 Stewart Stevenson: To ask the Scottish Executive how many prosecutions there have been for the offence of driving while using a hand-held mobile phone.

S2W-15260Δ Des McNulty: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans the First Minister has to provide more detailed information on the international development policy.
