Business Bulletin No. 79 / 2004

Tuesday 11th May 2004

Section E : Written questions lodged on Monday 10th May 2004
  

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S2W-8115 Shiona Baird: To ask the Scottish Executive what requirements there are on local authorities to recycle ozone-depleting refrigerant gases from discarded fridges.

S2W-8117 Alasdair Morgan: To ask the Scottish Executive what progress is being made in the establishment of the Skipton Fund for compensation for people with hepatitis C who are infected with the disease as a result of the use of contaminated blood products through the NHS.

S2W-8118 Alasdair Morgan: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will publish progress reports on the status of the work to establish the fund for compensation of people with Hepatitis C who are infected with the disease as a result of the use of contaminated blood products through the NHS.

S2W-8119 Shona Robison: To ask the Scottish Executive what the implications are for the NHS of recent legal rulings regarding the European Working Time Directive that resident on-call time will now be considered as work when calculating junior doctors' working hours.

S2W-8120 Donald Gorrie: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-7477 by Mr Andy Kerr on 7 May 2004, what independent information it has in relation to the data provided by Response Handling Ltd regarding response times for the period covered by the answer.

S2W-8121 Stewart Stevenson: To ask the Scottish Executive when it will reply to the letter of 7 April 2004 regarding my constituent, Ms H Whitehouse of Turriff.

S2W-8122 David McLetchie: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answers to questions S1W-34337, S2W-1078, S2W-1079 and S2W-7215 by Mr Andy Kerr on 11 March, 16 July 2003 and 15 April 2004, whether it will publish a consolidated list of publications produced in each year since 1999, giving the cost of each such publication.

S2W-8123 David McLetchie: To ask the Scottish Executive how many consultation documents it has produced in each year since 1999, giving the cost of each such document.

S2W-8124 Mrs Margaret Ewing: To ask the Scottish Executive when it will introduce legislation to widen the criteria by which the Office of the Scottish Charity Regulator can investigate charitable organisations.

S2W-8125Δ Scott Barrie: To ask the Scottish Executive when it will publish its volunteering strategy and the report of the working group established to develop proposals for a full-time volunteer programme for young people.
