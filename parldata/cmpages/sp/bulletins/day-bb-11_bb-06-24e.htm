Business Bulletin No. 81/2011: Friday 24 June 2011
Contents An A B C D E F G H I J

Section E – Written questions lodged on 23 June 2011

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

Questions in which a member has indicated a declarable interest are marked with an "R".

S4W-00999 Jim Hume: To ask the Scottish Executive whether the National Health Service (General Medical Services Contracts) (Scotland) Regulations 2004 allow health practices to dispense drugs to patients who reside within 2.5 miles of a local pharmacy and, if not, whether it plans to amend these regulations to allow them to do so.

S4W-01163 Alison McInnes: To ask the Scottish Executive how many gastric (a) band and (b) bypass operations were performed in each of the last three years, broken down by NHS board.

S4W-01164 Alison McInnes: To ask the Scottish Executive whether bariatric surgery is available to patients in all NHS board areas.

S4W-01165 Alison McInnes: To ask the Scottish Executive how many test purchases of alcohol were carried out in (a) on-sales and (b) off-sales premises in 2010; how many resulted in alcohol being sold to (i) an underage person and (ii) an underage person for the second time; what action was taken against the licensee in each case; and how many licences were suspended or revoked, broken down by police force area.

S4W-01167 Alison McInnes: To ask the Scottish Executive how many retailers were prosecuted for selling alcohol to underage customers in (a) 2009-10 and (b) 2010-11, broken down by police force area, and what fines were issued in each case.

S4W-01168 Alison McInnes: To ask the Scottish Executive how many retailers were prosecuted for selling alcohol to a drunk person in (a) 2009-10 and (b) 2010-11, broken down by police force, and what fines were issued in each case.

S4W-01169 Alison McInnes: To ask the Scottish Executive what the estimated annual (a) quantity and (b) cost of unused drugs and medicines is in the NHS, broken down by NHS board.

S4W-01170 Marco Biagi: To ask the Scottish Executive whether Education Scotland will help embed (a) global citizenship education and (b) sustainable development in schools and, if so, how.

S4W-01171 Marco Biagi: To ask the Scottish Executive to what extent it makes use of open source software.

S4W-01174 Jackie Baillie: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it is considering changing the frequency of breast cancer screening.

S4W-01175 Lewis Macdonald: To ask the Scottish Executive what discussions it had with Carillion or its consortium partners regarding governance of the Borders Railway project prior to Carillion withdrawing from the bidding process.

S4W-01176 Lewis Macdonald: To ask the Scottish Executive what discussions it had with Carillion or its consortium partners regarding the non-profit distributing model for the Borders Railway project prior to Carillion withdrawing from the bidding process.

S4W-01177 Lewis Macdonald: To ask the Scottish Executive what discussions Transport Scotland had with Carillion or its consortium partners regarding governance of the Borders Railway project prior to Carillion withdrawing from the bidding process.

S4W-01178 Lewis Macdonald: To ask the Scottish Executive what discussions Transport Scotland had with Carillion or its consortium partners regarding the non-profit distributing model for the Borders Railway project prior to Carillion withdrawing from the bidding process.

S4W-01179 Lewis Macdonald: To ask the Scottish Executive what its position is on the possibility of securing value for money on the Borders Railway project following Carillion’s withdrawal from the bidding process.

S4W-01180 Lewis Macdonald: To ask the Scottish Executive when it will announce the outcome of the bidding process for the Borders Railway project.

S4W-01181 Lewis Macdonald: To ask the Scottish Executive what requirements it will impose on the use of the Borders railway line by train operating companies.

S4W-01182 Tavish Scott: To ask the Scottish Executive how many times tear gas has been used by the police in incidents involving children under 16 in each of the last five years for which information is available, broken down by police force.

S4W-01183 Tavish Scott: To ask the Scottish Executive what guidance it has issued to the police regarding the use of tear gas in incidents involving children under 16.

S4W-01184 Richard Baker: To ask the Scottish Executive what flexibility it has to maintain the current level of employee contributions in local government pension schemes.

S4W-01185 Richard Baker: To ask the Scottish Executive what assessment it has made of the cost of maintaining local government employee pension contributions at the current level.

S4W-01186 Richard Baker: To ask the Scottish Executive how many people are members of local government pension schemes.

S4W-01187 Richard Baker: To ask the Scottish Executive what its estimate is of how many people would leave local government pension schemes in Scotland if employee contributions were raised in line with the proposals of the UK Government.

S4W-01188 Mary Scanlon: To ask the Scottish Executive how many emergency call-outs there have been for the (a) ambulance, (b) fire and (c) police service in each year since 2001.

S4W-01189 Mary Scanlon: To ask the Scottish Executive for what reason there are target response times for the ambulance service but not for the (a) police and (b) fire service.

S4W-01198 Roderick Campbell: To ask the Scottish Executive how many over-55s have (a) applied for and (b) been awarded state support for higher education since the removal of the age restriction.

S4W-01199 Adam Ingram: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S4W-00611 by Keith Brown on 21 June 2011, what estimates have been made of increased traffic flows on the A77 when the new Loch Ryan ferry terminal and Stena Line vessels become operational, broken down by (a) HGV and (b) other traffic.

S4W-01200 Adam Ingram: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S4W-00551 by Fergus Ewing on 16 June 2011, what progress it has made with the GP Wind project and what outputs have been achieved.
