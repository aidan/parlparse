Business Bulletin No. 48 / 2004

Tuesday 16th march 2004

Section F : Motions and Amendments
  

A full list of current motions is available to view each Monday in paper copy at the Chamber Desk or alternatively on the Scottish Parliament web site as Current Motions. The full text of all motions lodged the previous week will appear in the Business Bulletin the following Monday.

 

Items marked with an asterisk (*) are new or have been altered. Asterisks in the text show the extent of alterations made.

Motions which members wish to be considered for debate as members' business in the Parliament are marked with a hash symbol (#)

*S2M-1045 Alex Johnstone: Congratulations to Monikie Primary School-That the Parliament congratulates Monikie Primary School, Angus, on its recent HM Inspectorate of Education report; notes that the report is one of the best ever received by any school in Scotland with 14 of the assessed categories judged to be very good and one good; commends all the staff, pupils and the whole local school community who contribute to making the school so successful, and believes that Monikie Primary School is an excellent example of a rural primary school that provides a first-class education for all its pupils.
