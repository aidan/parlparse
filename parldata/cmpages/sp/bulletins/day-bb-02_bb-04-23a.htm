Tuesday 23 April 2002

Section A : Daily Business List
  

Meetings of Committees

All meetings take place in Committee Chambers, Edinburgh unless otherwise indicated.

Procedures Committee

7th Meeting, 2002

The Committee will meet at 9.30 am in Committee Room 4

1. CSG Inquiry: The Committee will take evidence from-

Shona Simon, former Equal Opportunities Development Adviser, The Scottish Parliament.

Finance Committee

8th Meeting, 2002

The Committee will meet at 10.00 am in Committee Room 2

1. 2003/04 Budget Process: The Committee will consider the Scottish Executive Administration and Associated Departments' 2003/04 budget proposals and take evidence from-

Graham Owenson, Finance Administration and Pay Policy;

Ian Walford, Director of Corporate Development;

Paul Rhodes, Head of Accommodation Division;

5. PFI/PPP Inquiry: The Committee will take evidence from Andy Kerr, Minister for Finance and Public Services.
