BUSINESS BULLETIN 58 / 2001

Tuesday 3 April 2001

Section D - Oral questions selected for answer on 5 April 2001

 

 

First Minister's Question Time

1. Roseanna Cunningham: To ask the First Minister when the Scottish Executive’s Cabinet last met and what issues were discussed. (S1F-995)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he plans to raise. (S1F-984)

3. Mr Andy Kerr: To ask the First Minister what new action the Scottish Executive is taking to improve the performance of the NHS in Scotland. (S1F-993)

4. Phil Gallie: To ask the First Minister whether the Scottish Executive plans to donate police officers to a potential European police force. (S1F-996)

5. Alex Neil: To ask the First Minister when the Scottish Executive will announce its additional financial allocation arising from the UK 2001 Budget. (S1F-983)

6. Pauline McNeill: To ask the First Minister what steps are being taken to ensure an adequate supply of Gaelic medium teachers. (S1F-992)
