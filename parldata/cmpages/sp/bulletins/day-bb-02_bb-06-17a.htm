Business Bulletin No. 99/2002

Monday 17 June 2002

Section A : Daily Business List
  

Meetings of Committees

All meetings take place in Committee Chambers, Edinburgh unless otherwise indicated.

Enterprise and Lifelong Learning Committee

19th Meeting, 2002

The Committee will meet at 2.00 pm in Lerwick Town Hall, Shetland

1. Local Economic Forums: The Committee will receive a report back on the progress and achievements to date from three Local Economic Forums-

Shetland Local Economic Forum: Mr David Finch, Chief Executive (Shetland Enterprise and Acting Chair, Shetland Local Economic Forum), Councillor Sandy Cluness, Chair (Shetland Islands Council's Development Department), Mr James Smith, Chair (Federation of Small Businesses);

Moray, Badenoch and Strathspey Local Economic Forum: Mr James Johnston, Executive Director (Moray Chamber of Commerce and Chair, Moray, Badenoch and Strathspey Local Economic Forum), Douglas Yule, Chief Executive (Moray, Badenoch and Strathspey Enterprise);

Argyll and the Islands Local Economic Forum: Professor Peter Timms, Chair (Argyll and the Islands Local Economic Forum), Mr Ken Abernathy, Chief Executive (Argyll and the Islands Enterprise).

2. Scotland in Sweden: The Committee will agree its representative to attend the Scotland in Sweden event in October.

3. Tourism Inquiry: The Committee will take evidence from-

Ms Barbara Foulkes, Chief Executive (Orkney Tourist Board)

Mr Maurice Mullay, Chief Executive and Mr Robert Smith, Chair (Shetland Islands Tourism).
