BUSINESS BULLETIN 13 / 2001

Tuesday 23 January 2001

Section D - Oral questions selected for answer on 25 January 2001

 

 

 

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister what the Scottish Executive's main priorities currently are. (S1F-799)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he next plans to meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he intends to raise. (S1F-794)

3. Janis Hughes: To ask the First Minister what plans the Scottish Executive has to assist business by improving transport links between Scotland and the rest of the UK. (S1F-804)

4. Andrew Wilson: To ask the First Minister whether any existing expenditure commitments will be cut to pay for new commitments on health and education spending and, if so, what these are. (S1F-795)

5. Elaine Thomson: To ask the First Minister what progress the Scottish Executive is making in removing barriers to employment. (S1F-805)

6. Christine Grahame: To ask the First Minister how much of the £10 million aid package for the textile industry announced by the Minister for Small Business and E-Commerce at the Department of Trade and Industry on 6 June 2000 has been allocated in Scotland. (S1F-798)
