Business Bulletin No. 94/2002

Monday 10 June 2002

Section E : Written questions lodged on 7 June 2002
  

Questions marked with a triangle () are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S1W-26539 Tavish Scott: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it or Highlands and Islands Airports Ltd (HIAL) has had any discussions with Omniport plc about appointing that company to manage any of the airports operated by HIAL and what the outcome was of any such discussions.

S1W-26540 Tavish Scott: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it has considered any proposals to contract out the management of all or any of the airports operated by Highlands and Islands Airports Ltd and, if so, what proposals have been considered.

S1W-26541 Tavish Scott: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it would consider offering a local authority the opportunity to take over the management of airports owned by Highlands and Islands Airports Ltd sited in the authority’s area.

S1W-26542 Tavish Scott: To ask the Scottish Executive what its long-term plans are for the management of airports owned by Highlands and Islands Airports Ltd.
