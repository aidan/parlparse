Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S2W-27607 Margaret Mitchell: To ask the Scottish Executive how many children have run away from local authority care homes in each of the last 10 years, broken down by local authority.
