BUSINESS BULLETIN 116/1999 

Wednesday 22 December 1999

Section F - Motions and Amendments 

 

 

The full text of all outstanding motions and amendments will appear in the Business Bulletin every Monday.

Items marked with an asterisk (*) are new or have been altered. Asterisks in the text show the extent of alterations made.

Motions which members wish to be considered for debate as members’ business in the Parliament are marked with a hash symbol (#).

 

*S1M-396 Mr Keith Harding: North Lanarkshire Council—That the Parliament expresses its concern over moves by the ruling Labour administration on North Lanarkshire Council to extend its Special Responsibility Allowance (SRA) to a further six councillors, at a cost of £30,000 to local council tax payers; notes that almost eighty per cent of the of the administration’s fifty six members now receive SRAs, and condemns the actions of the Council’s Labour group for putting its own interests ahead of those of local residents and council tax payers.
