Business Bulletin No. 149/2001

Tuesday 23 October 2001

Section D : Oral Questions
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive's Cabinet. (S1F-1313)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Prime Minister and what issues he intends to raise. (S1F-1308)

3. Scott Barrie: To ask the First Minister what steps are being taken to ensure that investment in books and equipment reaches all pupils, including children in local authority care. (S1F-1320)

4. Alex Neil: To ask the First Minister what action the Scottish Executive intends to take to address any allegations of institutional racism within its departments or any of the agencies for which it is responsible. (S1F-1305)

5. George Lyon: To ask the First Minister what assessment the Scottish Executive has made of UK research into the incidence of BSE in sheep. (S1F-1322)

6. Des McNulty: To ask the First Minister what progress is being made in improving and expanding public transport at a local level. (S1F-1319)
