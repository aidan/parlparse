Business Bulletin No. 103/2003

Monday 28 July 2003

Section E : Written questions lodged on 21 to 25 July 2003
  

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S2W-1600 Margo MacDonald: To ask the Scottish Executive how many people have qualified as home economics teachers in each year since 1997.
