Business Bulletin No. 124 / 2003

Tuesday 23rd September 2003

Section D : Oral Questions selected for answer on 25th September 2003
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Roseanna Cunningham: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive’s Cabinet. (S2F-215)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he intends to raise. (S2F-216)

3. Tommy Sheridan: To ask the First Minister what the Scottish Executive’s position is on the fairness of the council tax, particularly in relation to pensioners. (S2F-233)

4. Ms Wendy Alexander: To ask the First Minister what action the Scottish Executive is taking to combat the skills shortage in Scotland by welcoming people who wish to come to live and work here. (S2F-227)

5. Mrs Margaret Smith: To ask the First Minister what discussions the Scottish Executive has had with the Home Office regarding the introduction of identity cards. (S2F-223)

6. Murdo Fraser: To ask the First Minister what the impact of the introduction of top-up tuition fees in England and Wales will be on Scottish universities. (S2F-211)
