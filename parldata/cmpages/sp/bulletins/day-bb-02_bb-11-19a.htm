Public Petitions Committee

17th Meeting, 2002

The Committee will meet at 10.00 am in Committee Room 2

1. New Petitions: The Committee will consider the following new petitions-

PE538, PE547, PE548 and PE549: Definition of Autistic Spectrum Disorder and calls for investigations into the use of certain psychiatric drugs. Mr James Mackie will make a presentation in support of these petitions.

PE558: Improvement to the A96 (Elgin bypass). Ms Pauline Taylor will make a presentation in support of this petition.

PE560: Support for children and young people suffering from domestic abuse. Ms Claire Houghton (Scottish Women's Aid) will make a presentation in support of this petition.

PE561: Review of the Scottish Further Education Council's revenue funding formula. Ms Mary Beck will make a presentation in support of this petition.

PE566: Safeguarding of Title deeds in the judicial system.

2. Current Petitions: The Committee will further consider the following current petitions-

PE432: Independent Complaints Review Panels (social services).

PE504: Publication of criminal memoirs for profit.

PE507: Adoption of roads and pavements by local authorities.

PE563: Maintenance of unadopted roads and pavements.

PE528: Access to public roads at Scottish airports.

PE537: Handling of mental health care complaints.

3. Convener's report.
