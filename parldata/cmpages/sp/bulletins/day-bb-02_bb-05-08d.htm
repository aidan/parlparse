Business Bulletin No. 74/2002

Wednesday 8 May 2002

Section D : Oral Questions
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive's Cabinet. (S1F-1879)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Prime Minister and what issues he intends to raise. (S1F-1888)

3. Cathy Peattie: To ask the First Minister whether the implementation of the McCrone agreement is proceeding satisfactorily. (S1F-1893)

4. Miss Annabel Goldie: To ask the First Minister whether the Scottish Executive's vision of "A Smart, Successful Scotland" will be affected by the resignation of Ms Wendy Alexander as Minister for Enterprise, Transport and Lifelong Learning. (S1F-1891)

5. Mr Gil Paterson: To ask the First Minister what action the Scottish Executive is taking to provide guidance and information to prevent drug rape. (S1F-1882)

6. Donald Gorrie: To ask the First Minister what assistance the Scottish Executive can offer to NHS boards in financial difficulty. (S1F-1890)
