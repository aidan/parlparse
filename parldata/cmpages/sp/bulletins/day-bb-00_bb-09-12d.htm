BUSINESS BULLETIN 134 / 2000

Tuesday 12 September 2000

Section D - Oral questions selected for answer on 14 September 2000

 

 

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr Alex Salmond: To ask the First Minister when he last met the Prime Minister and what issues were discussed. (S1F-535)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he next intends to meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he intends to raise with him. (S1F-523)

3. Alex Neil: To ask the First Minister what progress has been made in reducing poverty among pensioners in Scotland since July 1999. (S1F-519)

4. Patricia Ferguson: To ask the First Minister whether monitoring measures will be introduced in order to ensure that local health boards direct the funding received under the Arbuthnott formula to those areas which have been identified as being of greatest need. (S1F-528)

5. David Mundell: To ask the First Minister what steps the Scottish Executive is taking to ensure that local authorities maintain the network of rural schools. (S1F-521)

6. Mr Duncan McNeil: To ask the First Minister what steps are being taken to use new technologies in promoting social and digital inclusion. (S1F-540)
