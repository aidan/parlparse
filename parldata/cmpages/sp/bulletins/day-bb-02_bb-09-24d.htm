Business Bulletin No. 135/2002

Tuesday 24 September 2002

Section D : Oral Questions

  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr Kenny MacAskill: To ask the First Minister when he next plans to meet the Prime Minister and what issues he intends to raise. (S1F-2110)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he plans to raise. (S1F-2117)

3. Sarah Boyack: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive will assess the potential impact of fluoridation on health. (S1F-2126)

4. Mr Keith Harding: To ask the First Minister whether the introduction of a proportional representation system of voting for local government elections will improve the governance of councils. (S1F-2116)

5. Karen Gillon: To ask the First Minister what impact population change will have on economic growth. (S1F-2124)

6. Christine Grahame: To ask the First Minister what concerns the Scottish Executive has regarding child poverty. (S1F-2121)
