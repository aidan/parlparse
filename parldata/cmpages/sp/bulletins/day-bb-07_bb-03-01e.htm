Business Bulletin No. 39/2007: Thursday 1 March 2007
Contents An A B C D E F G H I J

Section E – Written questions lodged on 28 February 2007

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S2W-32050 Ms Sandra White: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will provide details of the number of carbon sequestration rights that have been granted from 1999 to 2007 and the operations of the companies to whom such rights have been granted.

S2W-32052 Ms Sandra White: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it is considering issuing guidance to accompany Scottish Ministers’ Code of Practice on the Discharge of Functions by Public Authorities Under the Freedom of Information Act 2002, in light of comments by the Scottish Information Commissioner in Decision 149/2006 that the Executive’s interpretation of the words in a freedom of information request were "unreasonable and even perverse".

S2W-32086 Stewart Stevenson: To ask the Scottish Executive when the Minister for Environment and Rural Development intends to reply further to my letter of 18 January 2007 (ref.2006/0040838) regarding the Scottish Agricultural Wages Board.

S2W-32087 Ms Maureen Watt: To ask the Scottish Executive how many people have entered teacher training in each year since 1999, broken down by (a) gender, (b) ethnic background and (c) disability.

S2W-32088 Ms Maureen Watt: To ask the Scottish Executive how many people applied to enter teacher training in each year since 1999 and how many were accepted, broken down by (a) gender, (b) ethnic background and (c) disability.

S2W-32089 Trish Godman: To ask the Scottish Executive how many (a) beds, (b) day care places and (c) residential facilities there are for individuals suffering from chronic eating disorders, broken down by NHS board.

S2W-32090 Trish Godman: To ask the Scottish Executive whether proposals are in place anent the introduction of a system of administrative penalties for fisheries offences committed by the skippers or owners of fishing vessels in fishing grounds policed by the Scottish Fisheries Protection Agency and whether such a legal procedure could be used in respect of offenders whose vessels are registered in other European Union states.

S2W-32091 Trish Godman: To ask the Scottish Executive what recent discussions it has had with appropriate voluntary organisations, other interested parties and police forces anent the care and treatment of women and children who have been rescued from human traffickers.

S2W-32092 Trish Godman: To ask the Scottish Executive what recent discussions it has had with UK ministers anent the encouragement and expansion of community-based credit unions in Scotland.

S2W-32093 Trish Godman: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans are in place to increase the maximum sum that a consumer can claim under the small claims procedure for faulty goods or services from the current £750.

S2W-32094 Mr Kenny MacAskill: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-30117 by Cathy Jamieson on 14 February 2007, whether it will provide a breakdown of the accommodation costs incurred by the Chief Executive of the Scottish Prison Service on official overseas trips in each year since 1999.

S2W-32095 Mr Kenny MacAskill: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-30117 by Cathy Jamieson on 14 February 2007, whether it will provide a breakdown of the flight costs incurred by the Chief Executive of the Scottish Prison Service on official overseas trips in each year since 1999.

S2W-32096 Mr Kenny MacAskill: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-30117 by Cathy Jamieson on 14 February 2007, whether it will provide a breakdown of the flight costs incurred by senior staff of the Scottish Prison Service on official overseas trips in each year since 1999.

S2W-32097 Mr Kenny MacAskill: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-30117 by Cathy Jamieson on 14 February 2007, whether it will provide a breakdown of the flight costs incurred by prison governors on official overseas trips in each year since 1999.

S2W-32098 Mr Kenny MacAskill: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-30117 by Cathy Jamieson on 14 February 2007, how many overseas conferences on prison privatisation were attended by senior staff of the Scottish Prison Service in each year since 1999, showing the costs of flights to each conference.

S2W-32100 Alex Neil: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-31726 by Mr Tom McCabe on 27 February 2007, who met the costs of the First Minister’s flight to London and return rail journey.

S2W-32101 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive how it meets its obligations under the requirements of the United Nations Convention on the Rights of the Child (UNCRC), with specific reference to Article 12.

S2W-32102 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive what cognisance it has taken of the follow-up to the review of the Scottish Youth Parliament by HM Inspectorate of Education and Audit Scotland.

S2W-32103 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive what action it has taken as a result of the follow-up to the review of the Scottish Youth Parliament by HM Inspectorate of Education and Audit Scotland.

S2W-32104 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive what action it is taking to support the Scottish Youth Parliament towards achieving the main points for action identified in the the follow-up to the review of the Scottish Youth Parliament by HM Inspectorate of Education and Audit Scotland.

S2W-32105 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive what funding has been given to the Scottish Youth Parliament in each year since 1999, broken down into (a) core and (b) project funding.

S2W-32106 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive what level of core funding is projected for the Scottish Youth Parliament over the next three years.

S2W-32107 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it expects the Scottish Youth Parliament to seek funding from additional sources.

S2W-32108 Stewart Stevenson: To ask the Scottish Executive when it intends to reply to my letter dated 26 January 2007 regarding NHS dentistry services in the north east.

S2W-32109 Stewart Stevenson: To ask the Scottish Executive when the First Minister will reply to Alex Salmond MP’s letter dated 17 January 2007 regarding Government Expenditure and Revenues in Scotland (GERS) calculation data, which was passed to the First Minister by HM Treasury.

S2W-32110 Stewart Stevenson: To ask the Scottish Executive what the median and mean waiting times were for (a) in-patients and day cases and (b) out-patients in each year since 1999, also including availability status codes.

S2W-32111 Chris Ballance: To ask the Scottish Executive what the procedure will be for assessing bids for coastal and marine national park status and what the projected timescale is for assessments.

S2W-32112 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive what resources have been budgeted for projects arising from the findings of the review of the Scottish Youth Parliament by HM Inspectorate of Education and Audit Scotland.

S2W-32113 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive how it ensures that young people influence national policy.

S2W-32114 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive how it ensures that young people are involved in decision-making at local and national level.

S2W-32115 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive how it ensures that government policy reflects the views and opinions of young people.

S2W-32116 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive which of its departments administers relations with the Scottish Youth Parliament.

S2W-32117 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive from which budget headings and sub-headings the Scottish Youth Parliament is funded.

S2W-32118 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive how it ensures that the Scottish Youth Parliament maintains independence from the Executive.

S2W-32119 Chris Ballance: To ask the Scottish Executive what the impact on school population is estimated to be per 100 houses of new housing developments.

S2W-32120 Donald Gorrie: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it has to recruit and train more teachers of the deaf.

S2W-32121 Donald Gorrie: To ask the Scottish Executive what action it is taking to help to develop a curriculum for users of British Sign Language.

S2W-32122 Donald Gorrie: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it supports the policy of regarding the 70,000 deaf people who use British Sign Language as belonging to a different language and cultural group, rather than as having a disability or communication impairment.

S2W-32123 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-31849 by Rhona Brankin on 26 February 2007, what types of target and measurement mechanism will be included in its national race equality strategy and action plan.

S2W-32124 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive how resources will be allocated to each part of its forthcoming national race equality strategy and action plan.

S2W-32125 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-31839 by Hugh Henry on 28 February 2007, whether it will answer the second part of the question in relation to the cost of advertising the helpline.
