Business Bulletin No. 137/2001

Tuesday 25 September 2001

Section D : Oral Questions selected for answer on 27 September 2001

  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister when he next plans to meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he intends to raise. (S1F-1259)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when the Scottish Executive's Cabinet will next meet and what issues will be discussed. (S1F-1260)

3. David Mundell: To ask the First Minister what assessment the Scottish Executive has made of the impact on the economy of the recent terrorist attacks in the United States of America. (S1F-1256)

4. Dr Richard Simpson: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive's plans for personal care for older people will contribute to social justice. (S1F-1270)

5. Fergus Ewing: To ask the First Minister when the Scottish Executive expects the export ban on meat and livestock imposed by the European Commission to be lifted. (S1F-1267)

6. Marilyn Livingstone: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive intends to prevent the creation of a "digital divide". (S1F-1269)
