BUSINESS BULLETIN 74 / 2001

Wednesday 9 May 2001

Section D - Oral questions selected for answer on 10 May 2001

 

 

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Prime Minister and what issues he plans to raise. (S1F-1066)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he plans to raise. (S1F-1055)

3. Rhoda Grant: To ask the First Minister what benefits the first two years of devolution have delivered for the people of Scotland. (S1F-1071)

4. Ian Jenkins: To ask the First Minister what recent guarantees the Scottish Executive has received from the Scottish Qualifications Authority that the 2001 examination diet will be completed and processed successfully allowing all candidates to receive accurate results on time. (S1F-1065)

5. Lord James Douglas-Hamilton: To ask the First Minister what the potential benefits are for Scotland’s trade and tourism links with North America from Tartan Day. (S1F-1056)

6. Alex Neil: To ask the First Minister what plans the Scottish Executive has to enhance Scotland’s research and development base. (S1F-1072)
