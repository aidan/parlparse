Business Bulletin No. 87/2002

Monday 27 May 2002

Section A : Daily Business List
  

Meetings of Committees

All meetings take place in Committee Chambers, Edinburgh unless otherwise indicated.

Subordinate Legislation Committee

18th Meeting, 2002

The Committee will meet at 11.15 am in Committee Room 3

1. Executive Responses: The Committee will consider the responses from the Scottish Executive to points raised on the following—

The Community Care (Assessment of Needs) (Scotland) Regulations 2002, (draft)

The Community Care (Personal Care and Nursing Care) (Scotland) Regulations 2002, (draft)

The Criminal Justice Act 1988 (Offensive Weapons) Amendment (Scotland) Order, (draft)

The Community Care and Health (Scotland) Act 2002 (Consequential Amendment) Order 2002, (SSI 2002/233)

The Meat (Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Point) (Scotland) Regulations 2002, (SSI 2002/234).

2. Draft Codes: The Committee will consider the following draft codes—

Draft Code of Recommendations for the Welfare of Livestock: Laying Hens, (SE 2002/100)

Draft Code of Recommendations for the Welfare of Livestock: Meat Chickens and Breeding Chickens, (SE 2002/101).

3. Draft Instruments subject to approval: The Committee will consider the following—

The Civil Legal Aid (Financial Conditions) (Scotland) (No.2) Regulations 2002, (draft)

The Advice and Assistance (Financial Conditions) (Scotland) (No.2) Regulations 2002, (draft)

The Welfare of Farmed Animals (Scotland) Amendment Regulations 2002, (draft)

The Criminal Justice Act 1988 (Offensive Weapons) Amendment (Scotland) Order 2002, (draft).

4. Instruments subject to annulment: The Committee will consider the following—

Act of Sederunt (Fees of Solicitors in the Sheriff Court) (Amendment) 2002, (SSI 2002/235)

The Welfare of Animals (Slaughter or Killing) Amendment (Scotland) Regulations 2002, (SSI 2002/238)

The National Health Service (Clinical Negligence and Other Risks Indemnity Scheme) (Scotland) Amendment Regulations 2002, (SSI 2002/239)

The Criminal Legal Aid (Scotland) (Fees) Amendment Regulations 2002, (SSI 2002/246)

The Criminal Legal Aid (Fixed Payments) (Scotland) Amendment Regulations 2002, (SSI 2002/247).
