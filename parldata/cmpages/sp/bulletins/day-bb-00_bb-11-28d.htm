BUSINESS BULLETIN 183 / 2000

Tuesday 28 November 2000

Section D - Oral questions selected for answer on 30 November 2000

 

 

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Prime Minister and what issues he plans to raise. (S1F-697)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when the Scottish Executive’s Cabinet will next meet and what issues will be discussed. (S1F-690)

3. Mr Andy Kerr: To ask the First Minister what steps are being taken to consult over the future of Scotland’s passenger railway services. (S1F-695)

4. Alex Neil: To ask the First Minister whether the Scottish Executive has any plans to establish a national screening programme for hepatitis C. (S1F-685)

5. Mr Mike Rumbles: To ask the First Minister what the cost is of paying tuition fees for all eligible students in Scotland in the current academic year. (S1F-686)

6. Christine Grahame: To ask the First Minister whether the Scottish Executive has any concerns about the level of morale in Scotland’s prisons. (S1F-688)
