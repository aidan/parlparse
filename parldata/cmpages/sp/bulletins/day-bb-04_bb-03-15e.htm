Business Bulletin No. 47/2004

Monday 15 March 2004

Section E : Written questions lodged on 12 March 2004
  

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S2W-6929 Christine Grahame: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it monitors the funding of aids and adaptations by local authorities and, if not, how the provision of aids and adaptations is monitored.

S2W-6930 Christine Grahame: To ask the Scottish Executive whether any funding for aids and adaptations is ring-fenced.

S2W-6931 Christine Grahame: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answers to questions S2W-5962 and S2W-5963 by Mr Tom McCabe on 25 February and 9 March 2004, what it estimates the cost is of providing free personal care to 780 people under 65 with dementia.

S2W-6932 Lord James Douglas-Hamilton: To ask the Scottish Executive when it will invite further bids for grant funding for advocacy services for children with additional support needs and their families; what the criteria will be for considering such bids; what the arrangements for funding will be, and what level of funding will be granted.

S2W-6933 Lord James Douglas-Hamilton: To ask the Scottish Executive when it now estimates that the code of practice relating to the Education (Additional Support for Learning) (Scotland) Bill will be consulted on and implemented.

S2W-6934 Margaret Smith: To ask the Scottish Executive what discussions it has had with Her Majesty's Government concerning the Agreement on the Conservation of Albatrosses and Petrels.

S2W-6935 Chris Ballance: To ask the Scottish Executive how many branch lines have been re-opened for passenger rail services in each year since 1970.

S2W-6936 Miss Annabel Goldie: To ask the Scottish Executive whether there are any plans to change the financial threshold for small claims and summary courts.

S2W-6937 Miss Annabel Goldie: To ask the Scottish Executive what progress has been made in ensuring the delivery of mental health reports to children's hearings following the publication of Children's Hearings Representative Group - Report on Children's Hearings Time Intervals 2002-03.

S2W-6938Δ Pauline McNeill: To ask the Scottish Executive when the report of the Summary Justice Review Committee will be published.
