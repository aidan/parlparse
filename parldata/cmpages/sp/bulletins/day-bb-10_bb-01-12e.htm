Section E – Written questions lodged on 11 January 2010

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

Questions in which a member has indicated a declarable interest are marked with an "R".

S3W-30575 Jackie Baillie: To ask the Scottish Executive how many people who would otherwise have qualified were denied patient transport due to recent winter pressures, broken down by NHS board.
