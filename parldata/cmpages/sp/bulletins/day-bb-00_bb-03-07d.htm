BUSINESS BULLETIN 42 / 2000

Tuesday 7 March 2000

Section D –  Oral questions selected for answer on 9 March 2000

 

  

1. Mr Alex Salmond: To ask the First Minister what issues were discussed at the most recent meeting of the Scottish Executive’s cabinet. (S1F-183)

 

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister whether he has any plans to meet backbench Labour MPs to discuss the performance of the Scottish Executive to date. (S1F-178)

 

3. Karen Whitefield: To ask the First Minister whether joint working across geographic boundaries is necessary in order to combat the problem of drugs. (S1F-187)

 

4. David Mundell: To ask the First Minister whether the Scottish Executive has any plans to tackle potential depopulation in parts of Scotland identified in the recent population projections released by the Registrar General. (S1F-174)

 

5. Dr Sylvia Jackson: To ask the First Minister how social housing in rural areas will be supported. (S1F-186)

 

6. Dorothy-Grace Elder: To ask the First Minister what environmental and agricultural damage, if any, was caused by US Navy Ships shelling the Cape Wrath coast last week. (S1F-185)
