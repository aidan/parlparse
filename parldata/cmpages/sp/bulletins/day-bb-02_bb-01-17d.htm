Business Bulletin No. 9/2002

Thursday 17 January 2002

Section D : Oral Questions selected for answer on 24 January 2002
  

Question Time

1. Donald Gorrie: To ask the Scottish Executive how it is promoting amateur football. (S1O-4516)

2. Shona Robison: To ask the Scottish Executive whether mental health is still a key clinical priority. (S1O-4508)

3. Mr Duncan Hamilton: To ask the Scottish Executive when it last met representatives from the Department of the Environment, Food and Rural Affairs and what issues were discussed. (S1O-4473)

4. Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive whether the Glasgow Airport link and the Glasgow crossrail projects are among its transport priorities. (S1O-4497)

5. Roseanna Cunningham: To ask the Scottish Executive when it last met representatives of Perth and Kinross Council. (S1O-4476)

6. Tommy Sheridan: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it has to issue guidance to the police on dealing with possession of cannabis cases in the light of the Home Secretary’s recent announcement on categorisation of cannabis. (S1O-4486)

7. John Farquhar Munro: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it has to encourage the relocation of aquaculture sites to more exposed offshore locations. (S1O-4503)

8. Susan Deacon: To ask the Scottish Executive what action it plans to take in light of the report by Her Majesty’s Inspectorate of Education, Standards and Quality in Primary and Secondary Schools 1998-2001. (S1O-4488)

9. Margaret Jamieson: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it has to provide young people with information on organ donation. (S1O-4519)

10. Bristow Muldoon: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it has to promote youth work. (S1O-4492)

11. Fiona Hyslop: To ask the Scottish Executive how it will ensure that planning authorities, when developing their strategic planning requirements, give full and proper consideration to the provision of local health services. (S1O-4484)

12. Richard Lochhead: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it has to remove the 21 day rule relating to the movement of livestock off farms. (S1O-4471)

13. Janis Hughes: To ask the Scottish Executive what action it is taking to tackle the misuse of alcohol. (S1O-4507)

14. Mary Scanlon: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it has any plans to stop children attending nursery or school if they have not had the MMR vaccine. (S1O-4515)

15. Alex Neil: To ask the Scottish Executive when it will bring new subordinate legislation forward in relation to the operation of Individual Learning Accounts. (S1O-4481)

16. Mr Alasdair Morrison: To ask the Scottish Executive whether there are plans to promote an interest in genealogy in Scotland and abroad. (S1O-4493)

17. Mr Frank McAveety: To ask the Scottish Executive what progress is being made with the bid to host the European Football Championships in 2008. (S1O-4489)

18. Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive what progress is being made on reducing the numbers of young women suffering from depression. (S1O-4517)

19. Brian Fitzpatrick: To ask the Scottish Executive what action it is taking to promote good practice in integrating asylum seekers and refugee children into schools. (S1O-4480)

20. Robin Harper: To ask the Scottish Executive what contribution is being made to the development of citizenship and global values in education by the teaching of Modern Studies. (S1O-4495)

21. Ms Sandra White: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S1W-21214 by Ms Margaret Curran on 15 January 2002, which projects are currently under investigation because of alleged irregularities in the use of Social Inclusion Partnership funds. (S1O-4514)

22. David Mundell: To ask the Scottish Executive what response it will give to the launch of the South of Scotland Alliance. (S1O-4511)

23. Michael Russell: To ask the Scottish Executive what action it plans to take to address the issues identified in the report by Her Majesty’s Inspectorate of Education, Standards and Quality in Primary and Secondary Schools 1998-2001. (S1O-4491)

24. Rhona Brankin: To ask the Scottish Executive what action it plans to take to promote science in schools. (S1O-4498)

25. Mr Adam Ingram: To ask the Scottish Executive what guidance it has issued on the prescribing of anti-depressants. (S1O-4502)

26. George Lyon: To ask the Scottish Executive what steps it will take to ensure that water authorities and their sub-contractors reinstate Argyll and Bute’s roads to a safe and acceptable standard after work has been completed. (S1O-4522)

27. Mr Brian Monteith: To ask the Scottish Executive when it will next meet representatives of the Scottish Arts Council and what issues will be discussed. (S1O-4469)

28. Lord James Douglas-Hamilton: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will collect information on the number of speakers of the 60 ethnic community languages spoken in Scotland as a first language. (S1O-4475)

29. Mr Gil Paterson: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it has to increase the number of women sexually assaulted who report the assaults. (S1O-4500)

30. Dennis Canavan: To ask the Scottish Executive when it will bring the dissolution order for the Scottish Transport Group pension funds before the Parliament. (S1O-4482)
