BUSINESS BULLETIN No. 148/2001

Monday 22 October 2001

Section A : Daily Business List
  

Meetings of Committees

All meetings take place in Committee Chambers, Edinburgh unless otherwise indicated.

European Committee

11th Meeting, 2001

The Committee will meet at 10.30 am in Committee Room 1

1. Items in private: The Committee will consider whether to discuss its draft report on the state-of-preparation for the euro and possible changes to its remit in private at its meeting on 30 October.

Not before 10.45 a.m:

2. Governance in the European Union and the Future of Europe: The Committee will hear evidence from-

Angus Robertson MP, Frontbench spokesperson on Foreign Affairs for the Scottish National Party

Richard Spring MP, Frontbench Spokesperson for Foreign and Commonwealth Affairs for the Conservative Party.
