Tuesday 3 December 2002

Section A : Daily Business List
  

Meetings of Committees

All meetings take place in Committee Chambers, Edinburgh unless otherwise indicated.

Procedures Committee

17th Meeting, 2002

The Committee will meet at 9.00 am in Committee Room 4

1. Consultative Steering Group Inquiry: The Committee will continue consideration of an issues paper on power sharing.

Public Petitions Committee

18th Meeting, 2002

The Committee will meet at 10.00 am in Committee Room 2

1. New Petitions: The Committee will consider the following new petitions-

PE567: Public bus services in rural communities. Mr Rab Amos (Roslin Bus Action Group) will make a presentation in support of this petition.

PE569: Proposal for the re-regulation of bus services. Mr David Cox will make a presentation in support of this petition.

PE534: The use of doctors' reports in court actions. Mr Duncan Shields (Fathers Fighting Injustice and the International Men's Organisation) will make a presentation in support of this petition.

PE559: Wind farm development in Scotland. Mr Ben Palmer will make a presentation in support of this petition.

PE564: National strategy for wind farms in Scotland. Mr W. R Graham will make a presentation in support of this petition.

PE565: Welfare of minors under Scottish law. Miss Jacqueline Shields will make a presentation in support of this petition.

PE571: Implementation of Complementary Alternative Medicine.

2. Current Petitions: The Committee will further consider the following current petitions-

PE503: Proposal to ban smoking in public places.

PE513: Future of rural sub post offices.

PE542: Support for the Post Office network in Scotland.

PE544: Proposal for an inquiry into the operations of the Scottish Criminal Records Office in relation to fingerprint identifications.

3. Inadmissible Petitions: The Committee will be invited to agree the recommendations contained in a paper on inadmissible petitions.

4. Convener's report.
