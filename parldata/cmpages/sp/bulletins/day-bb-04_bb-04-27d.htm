Business Bulletin No. 70 / 2004

Tuesday 27th April 2004

Section D : Oral questions selected for answer on Thursday 29th April 2004
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive's Cabinet. (S2F-837)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he next plans to meet the Prime Minister and what issues he intends to raise. (S2F-838)

3. Robin Harper: To ask the First Minister when the Cabinet Sub-Committee on Sustainable Scotland will next meet and what matters will be discussed. (S2F-846)

4. Pauline McNeill: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive will respond to the legal judgement regarding sanitation in prisons. (S2F-844)

5. Christine Grahame: To ask the First Minister whether the Scottish Executive has any concerns over delay in implementation of the ex gratia payment scheme for those infected with hepatitis C through contaminated blood products and, if so, what steps are being taken to address the delay. (S2F-849)

6. Phil Gallie: To ask the First Minister what discussions the Scottish Executive has had with Her Majesty's Government in respect of the introduction of identity cards. (S2F-835)
