Business Bulletin No. 32/2010: Tuesday 23 February 2010
Contents An A B C D E F G H I J

Section E – Written questions lodged on 22 February 2010

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

Questions in which a member has indicated a declarable interest are marked with an "R".

S3W-31858 Gavin Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive in how many cases involving domestic violence toward (a) women and (b) men in the Lothians region a custodial sentence of six months or less were given in each of the last five years, also expressed as a percentage.

S3W-31859 Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive how many antisocial behaviour orders have been issued against landlords under the Antisocial Behaviour etc. (Scotland) Act 2004, broken down by local authority area.

S3W-31860 Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive what information is available to help local authorities check whether individuals are acting as landlords and require to be registered under the terms of the Antisocial Behaviour etc. (Scotland) Act 2004.

S3W-31861 Rhona Brankin: To ask the Scottish Executive how many GPs from outwith the United Kingdom are working for the NHS, broken down by NHS board area.

S3W-31862 Rhona Brankin: To ask the Scottish Executive how many doctors from outwith the United Kingdom are working for NHS hospitals, broken down by NHS board area.

S3W-31863 Willie Coffey: To ask the Scottish Executive when it will bring forward proposals to reform conveyancing law and, in particular, procedures for disposals a non domino.

S3W-31864 Jeremy Purvis: To ask the Scottish Executive what actions it is taking to assist third sector organisations in winning procurement contracts in the (a) public, (b) private and (c) social enterprise sector.

S3W-31865 Jeremy Purvis: To ask the Scottish Executive which of its contracts were allocated to third sector organisations in each of the last five years and to which organisation in each case.

S3W-31866 Jeremy Purvis: To ask the Scottish Executive which of its (a) current contracts contain and (b) previous contracts allocated in the last five years have contained community benefit clauses.

S3W-31867 Jeremy Purvis: To ask the Scottish Executive how many social enterprises have contacted the aspire to enterprise consortium for business support since January 2008.

S3W-31868 Jeremy Purvis: To ask the Scottish Executive how many social enterprises the aspire to enterprise consortium has signposted to Business Gateway since January 2008.

S3W-31869 Jeremy Purvis: To ask the Scottish Executive what social enterprises Business Gateway has signposted to (a) Scottish Enterprise and (b) Highlands and Islands Enterprise since January 2008.

S3W-31870 Jeremy Purvis: To ask the Scottish Executive what action Business Gateway is taking to engage with the social enterprise sector.

S3W-31871 Jeremy Purvis: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it is evaluating value for money regarding business support for third sector organisations.

S3W-31872 Jeremy Purvis: To ask the Scottish Executive how it is evaluating the success of business support for third sector organisations.

S3W-31873 Jeremy Purvis: To ask the Scottish Executive how much (a) third sector organisations and (b) social enterprises contributed to the economy in each of the last three years.

S3W-31874 Jeremy Purvis: To ask the Scottish Executive how many people were employed in (a) third sector organisations and (b) social enterprises in each of the last three years.

S3W-31875 Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive how many (a) licence holders and (b) employees were issued fines for supplying alcoholic liquor to a drunken person in licensed premises under section 76 of the Licensing (Scotland) Act 1976 in (i) 2008 and (ii) 2009.

S3W-31876 Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive how many individuals have been (a) prosecuted and (b) convicted for selling alcohol to a drunk person under section 113 of the Licensing (Scotland) Act 2005 since it came into force and what the level of fine was in each case.

S3W-31877 Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive how many (a) young and (b) adult offenders convicted in the last year for which information is available had (i) no, (ii) one to four, (iii) five to 10 and (iv) over 10 previous convictions.

S3W-31878 Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive how many individuals were convicted of carrying a knife or other bladed weapon in each of the last three years, broken down by manner of disposal.

S3W-31879 Robert Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive how many individuals convicted of carrying a knife or other bladed weapon were given a custodial sentence in each of the last three years, broken down by length of sentence.

S3W-31880 Bill Butler: To ask the Scottish Executive how many GP practices use premium rate telephone numbers, broken down by NHS board area.

S3W-31881 Bill Butler: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will publish any correspondence between it and faith communities sent since 1 June 2007 regarding encouraging the twinning of denominational and non-denominational schools.

S3W-31882 Bill Butler: To ask the Scottish Executive how many members of staff it employs in its recruitment section.
