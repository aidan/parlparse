Justice 2 Committee

38th Meeting, 2002

The Committee will meet at 9.45 am in Committee Room 1

1. Item in private: The Committee will consider whether to take item 6 in private.

2. Petitions: The Committee will consider the following petitions—

PE306 by Mr Thomas Minogue on Freemasonry and the judiciary;

PE438 by Mr George McAuley on behalf of UK Men's Movement on the wilful alienation of siblings;

PE476 by Ms Catherine Harper on behalf of Scottish Women Against Pornography on the display of obscene material.

3. Subordinate legislation: The Committee will consider the following negative instrument—

The Combined Police Area Amalgamation Schemes 1995 Amendment (No.2) (Scotland) Order 2002 (SSI 2002/458).

4. Crown Office and Procurator Fiscal Service Inquiry: The Committee will consider the appointment of a reporter.

5. Land Reform (Scotland) Bill: The Committee will consider the Bill at Stage 2 (Day 10).

6. Petition: The Committee will consider person specification and possible candidates for the post of adviser on PE336 by Frank Maguire on civil justice for asbestos victims.

Scottish Executive officials.
