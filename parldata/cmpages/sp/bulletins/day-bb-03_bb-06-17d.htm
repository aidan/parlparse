Business Bulletin No. 91 / 2003

Tuesday 17th June 2003

Section D : Oral questions selected for answer on 19 June 2003
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he intends to discuss. (S2F-94)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Prime Minister and what issues he intends to discuss. (S2F-102)

3. Robin Harper: To ask the First Minister what plans the Scottish Executive has, and what action it has taken in this session of the Parliament, to build a sustainable future in Scotland. (S2F-101)

4. Mr Alasdair Morrison: To ask the First Minister what impact any constitutional changes arising from the recent reshuffle of the cabinet of Her Majesty’s Government will have on the Scottish Executive. (S2F-100)

5. Robert Brown: To ask the First Minister how Glasgow will benefit from the west coast main line railway upgrade. (S2F-99)

6. Brian Adam: To ask the First Minister what the Scottish Executive’s plans are for increasing police numbers. (S2F-89)
