Section E – Written questions lodged from 29 March to 1 April 2005

Questions marked with a triangle ( Δ ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S2W-15636 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive what action it is taking to promote awareness of the symptoms of prostate cancer .

S2W-15637 Irene Oldfather: To ask the Scottish Executive what information it has on the effectiveness of zometa in the treatment of prostate cancer in (a) the rest of the United Kingdom, (b) Europe and (c) the United States of America.
