Business Bulletin No. 8/2002

Wednesday 16 January 2002

Section E : Written questions lodged on 15 January 2002
  

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S1W-21718 Michael Russell: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it undertook any review before 23 February 2001 of the state veterinary service; if so, who carried out the review, what recommendations the review contained, what action was taken by Ministers in the light of the review and whether it (a) contained any assessment of or any other reference to the ability of the service to respond to a major crisis in animal health and (b) specifically mentioned a possible outbreak of foot-and-mouth disease.
