Business Bulletin No. 30/2010: Monday 15 February 2010
Contents An A B C D E F G H I J

Section E – Written questions lodged on 12 February 2010

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

Questions in which a member has indicated a declarable interest are marked with an "R".

S3W-31763 Hugh O’Donnell: To ask the Scottish Executive how many retired teachers working as supply teachers earned more from a combination of pension and supply teaching payments than from pre-retirement in each of the last five years.

S3W-31764 Karen Gillon: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will participate in the Earth Hour event taking place at 8.30 pm on 27 March 2010.

S3W-31765 Karen Gillon: To ask the Scottish Parliamentary Corporate Body whether it will participate in the Earth Hour event taking place at 8.30 pm on 27 March 2010.

S3W-31766 Karen Gillon: To ask the Scottish Executive how much money it allocated to the Coalfields Regeneration Trust in each of the last five years.

S3W-31767 John Scott: To ask the Scottish Executive what the further benefits are of continuing Scotland’s Environment and Rural Services (SEARS).

S3W-31768 John Scott: To ask the Scottish Executive how much money Scotland’s Environment and Rural Services (SEARS) has saved since its formation.

S3W-31769 John Scott: To ask the Scottish Executive how much money Scotland’s Environment and Rural Services (SEARS) can be expected to save in the future.

S3W-31770 John Scott: To ask the Scottish Executive whether a cost-benefit analysis of Scotland’s Environment and Rural Services (SEARS) has been conducted and, if so, what the results were.

S3W-31771 Ross Finnie: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it considers that the National Renewables Infrastructure Plan is a qualifying plan under the terms of the Environmental Assessment (Scotland) Act 2005 and what the reasons are for its position on this matter.

S3W-31772 Ross Finnie: To ask the Scottish Executive what communication it has had with Scottish Enterprise regarding the application of the provisions of the Environmental Assessment (Scotland) Act 2005 to the National Renewables Infrastructure Plan.

S3W-31773 Ross Finnie: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it considers that the habitats regulations apply to the National Renewables Infrastructure Plan and, if so, what consideration was given to the implications of the plan on Natura 2000 sites.

S3W-31774 Ross Finnie: To ask the Scottish Executive what communication it has had with Scottish Enterprise regarding the application of the habitats regulations to the National Renewables Infrastructure Plan.

S3W-31775 Ms Wendy Alexander: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S3W-27502 by Stewart Stevenson on 25 September 2009, how much money it has allocated to the Fastlink initiative.

S3W-31776 Ms Wendy Alexander: To ask the Scottish Executive what its position is on devolving financial services regulation to the Scottish Parliament and a Scottish financial services authority.

S3W-31777 Rob Gibson: To ask the Scottish Executive what its position is on calls from the Scottish Surfing Federation for a Scottish surfing team to take part in international events.

S3W-31778 Rob Gibson: To ask the Scottish Executive how it could support the creation of a Scottish surfing team to take part in international events.

S3W-31779 Rob Gibson: To ask the Scottish Executive how it supports surfing.

S3W-31780 Rob Gibson: To ask the Scottish Executive what its position is on calls from the Scottish Surfing Federation for a Scottish team to take part in International Surfing Association events.
