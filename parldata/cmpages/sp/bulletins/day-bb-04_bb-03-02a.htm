Business Bulletin No. 38 / 2004

Tuesday 2nd March 2004

Section A : Daily Business List
  

Meetings of Committees

All meetings take place in Committee Chambers, Edinburgh unless otherwise indicated.

Audit Committee

4th Meeting, 2004

The Committee will meet at 9.30 am in Committee Room 1

1. Scottish Parliamentary Corporate Body (in private): The Committee will consider lines of questioning for the witnesses at agenda item 3.

2. Items in private: The Committee will consider whether to hold agenda items 7, 8 and 9 in private. The Committee will also consider whether to take in private at a future meeting lines of questioning for witnesses for its evidence sessions on its inquiry into the report by the Auditor General for Scotland entitled "Overview of the National Health Service in Scotland" (AGS/2003/12).

3. Scottish Parliamentary Corporate Body: The Committee will take evidence from-

Mr Paul Grice, Clerk and Chief Executive, Scottish Parliament; and

Mr Derek Croll, Head of Corporate Services, Scottish Parliament.

4. Scottish Parliamentary Corporate Body: The Committee will consider the evidence taken at agenda item 3.

5. Historic Scotland: The Committee will receive a briefing from the Auditor General for Scotland on the report by the Auditor General for Scotland entitled "Performance Management in Historic Scotland" (AGS/2004/1).

6. Financial Scrutiny Inquiry: The Committee will consider a paper on the Convener's meeting with the Convener of the Procedures Committee on the intentions of the Procedures Committee in relation to its legislation inquiry.

7. Historic Scotland: The Committee will consider its approach to the report by the Auditor General for Scotland entitled "Performance Management in Historic Scotland" (AGS/2004/1).

8. Scottish Enterprise: The Committee will consider key issues emerging from the evidence taken on the report by the Auditor General for Scotland entitled "Scottish Enterprise: special audit examination" (AGS/2003/11).

9. Scottish Further Education Funding Council: The Committee will consider a draft report on its inquiry into the report by the Auditor General for Scotland entitled "Scottish Further Education Funding Council - performance management of the further education sector in Scotland" (AGS/2003/7) and arrangements for its publication.

Finance Committee

8th Meeting, 2004

The Committee will meet at 10.00 am in Committee Room 2

1. Inquiry into the Relocation of Public Sector Jobs: The Committee will consider a SPICe briefing paper.

2. Budget process 2005-06: The Committee will consider a paper by the Clerk on its approach to the 2005-006 budget process.

3. Item in private: The Committee will decide whether to consider the draft report on the Fire Sprinklers in Residential Premises (Scotland) Bill Financial Memorandum in private at its next meeting.

Procedures Committee

4th Meeting, 2004

The Committee will meet at 10.15 am in Committee Room 4

1. Non-Executive Bills: The Committee will take evidence from-

Barry Winetrobe, Lecturer in Public Law, Glasgow University; and

Joyce McMillan, Convener of the Scottish Civic Forum and former member of the Consultative Steering Group.

2. Timescales and Stages of Bills - witness expenses: The Committee will be invited to delegate to the Convener responsibility for arranging for the SPCB to pay, under Rule 12.4.3, any expenses of witnesses in the inquiry.
