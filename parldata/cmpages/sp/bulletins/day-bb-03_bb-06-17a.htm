Audit Committee

2nd Meeting, 2003

The Committee will meet at 10.30 am in Committee Room 4

1. Items in private: The Committee will consider whether to hold agenda items 6 and 7 in private.

2. Declaration of interests: Members of the Committee will be invited to declare any relevant interests.

3. Audit Scotland work programme: The Committee will receive a briefing from the Auditor General for Scotland on Audit Scotland’s work programme for the period Autumn 2003 to Spring 2004.

4. Individual Learning Accounts in Scotland: The Committee will receive a briefing from the Auditor General for Scotland on his report on the operation of ILA’s in Scotland entitled ‘Individual Learning Accounts in Scotland’ (AGS/2003/3)

5. Moving to Mainstream: The Committee will receive a briefing from the Auditor General for Scotland on his report on the inclusion of pupils with special educational needs into mainstream schools entitled ‘Moving to Mainstream’ (AGS/2003/4).

6. Individual Learning Accounts in Scotland: The Committee will consider its approach to the report by the Auditor General for Scotland entitled ‘Individual Learning Accounts in Scotland’ (AGS/2003/3).

7. Moving to Mainstream: The Committee will consider its approach to the report by the Auditor General for Scotland entitled ‘Moving to Mainstream’ (AGS/2003/4).

Local Government and Transport Committee

2nd Meeting, 2003

The Committee will meet at 2.00 pm in Committee Room 1

the Scottish Public Services Ombudsman Act 2002 (Consequential Modification of Instruments) Order 2003 (SSI 2003/242).
