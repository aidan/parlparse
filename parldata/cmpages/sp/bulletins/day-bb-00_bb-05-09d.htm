BUSINESS BULLETIN 77 / 2000

Tuesday 9 May 2000

Section D - Oral questions selected for answer on 11 May 2000

 

 

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr Alex Salmond: To ask the First Minister when he next intends to meet the Prime Minister and what issues are likely to be discussed. (S1F-308)

 

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister what issues were discussed at this week’s meeting of the Scottish Executive’s Cabinet. (S1F-307)

 

3. Dorothy-Grace Elder: To ask the First Minister, further to the answer to question S1W-2780 by Susan Deacon on 17 December 1999, what stage consideration of lung transplant services in Scotland has reached as part of the review of cardiothoracic transplantation services in England. (S1F-313)

 

4. Mr Duncan McNeil: To ask the First Minister whether there are any plans for additional Joint Ministerial Committees. (S1F-305)

 

5. Lord James Douglas-Hamilton: To ask the First Minister what discussions the Scottish Executive has had with Her Majesty’s Government on any proposed reduction in the number of members of the Scottish Parliament. (S1F-311)

 

6. Scott Barrie: To ask the First Minister what progress is being made in eliminating child poverty in Fife and in Scotland as a whole. (S1F-306)
