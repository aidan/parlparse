Business Bulletin No. 146/2007: Monday 5 November 2007

Section E – Written questions lodged on 2 November 2007

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

Questions in which a member has indicated a declarable interest are marked with an "R".

S3W-5972 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive whether official cars have been used to transport ministers, for purposes outwith their official capacities, to or from private addresses other than those registered as the relevant minister’s home address and, if so, whether it will provide details of such journeys, since May 2007.

S3W-5983 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive what percentage of its staff are registered disabled; how this relates to its targets for employing disabled staff, and what action it is taking to increase the number of disabled people it employs.

S3W-5984 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive what requests it has received in the last three years to provide financial assistance to hospices and what responses it has given in each case.

S3W-5985 Karen Gillon: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it believes that Scotland should remain a party to the Common Fisheries Policy.

S3W-5986 Murdo Fraser: To ask the Scottish Executive how much it has paid to Caledonia Youth in each year since 1999.

S3W-5987 Murdo Fraser: To ask the Scottish Executive how much it has paid to the Scottish Inter-Faith Council in each year since 1999.

S3W-5988 Murdo Fraser: To ask the Scottish Executive how much it has paid to Stonewall in each year since 1999.

S3W-5989 Charlie Gordon: To ask the Scottish Executive when it expects tender documents to be issued for the operation of a new ferry service from Campbeltown to Ballycastle.

S3W-5990 Elaine Murray: To ask the Scottish Executive what arrangements are in place for the repayment of awards received by Young’s Seafood under the Fisheries Processing and Marketing Awards Scheme in 2003 and 2004 for the upgrading of its production facilities in Annan.

S3W-5991 Elaine Murray: To ask the Scottish Executive when the decision was taken not to award Young’s Seafood a grant of £426,000, intended for the development of deshelling machines for its site in Annan and applied for under the Fish Processing and Marketing Scheme.

S3W-5992 Elaine Murray: To ask the Scottish Executive what the reasons were for the decision not to award Young’s Seafood a grant of £426,000, intended for the development of deshelling machines for its site in Annan and applied for under the Fish Processing and Marketing Scheme.

S3W-5993 Elaine Murray: To ask the Scottish Executive what Executive grants have been applied for by Young’s Seafood since 1999; which applications have been successful, and in which sites such grants been invested.

S3W-5994 Elaine Murray: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S3W-5251 by John Swinney on 30 October 2007, whether Dumfries and Galloway Council has advised Scottish ministers of its estimates of the funding it will require to enable it to freeze council tax in 2008-09 and 2009-10 and, if so, what these estimates are.

S3W-5995 Elaine Murray: To ask the Scottish Executive whether Dumfries and Galloway Council has advised Scottish ministers of its estimates of the funding it will require to reduce class sizes in primary 1 to primary 3 to a maximum of 18 and, if so, what these estimates are.

S3W-5996 Elaine Murray: To ask the Scottish Executive when it will publish its economic strategy.

S3W-5997 Elaine Murray: To ask the Scottish Executive what its targets are for the growth of the economy over the next four years.

S3W-5998 Elaine Murray: To ask the Scottish Executive how it will evaluate the competitiveness of the economy over the next four years.

S3W-5999 Elaine Murray: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it proposes any changes to the statistics available for monitoring the success of the economy.

S3W-6000 Elaine Murray: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it is normal practice to use the Public Expenditure Statistical Analysis outturn figures as the baseline budget for the following financial year.

S3W-6003 Elaine Murray: To ask the Scottish Executive what communications it received from HM Treasury regarding the consequences for the Scottish budget in 2008-09 of UK programmes and projects that would no longer be funded in that financial year and when these were received.

S3W-6004 Elaine Murray: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will increase support to Co-operative Development Scotland.

S3W-6005 John Wilson: To ask the Scottish Executive what the implications are of the police operation at Central Station, Glasgow, on 31 October 2007 under section 44(2) of the Terrorism Act 2000.

S3W-6012 Richard Baker: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will list the community projects in the Grampian area that will benefit in 2007-08 from money recovered under the Proceeds of Crime Act 2002.
