Business Bulletin No. 128/2003

Monday 29 September 2003

Section E : Written questions lodged on 26 September 2003
  

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S2W-2936 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-2266 by Mr Jim Wallace on 24 September 2003, what its position is on the statement by the European Commission Directorate-General for Education and Culture, as expressed in Trends and Learning Structures in European Higher Education III that "Higher education was already included in the 1994 round of GATS negotiations but has assumed a much more prominent and highly disputed position in the current round", in light of the Executive's statement that "the EC has not and does not intend to make any offer in the area of public sector education services".

S2W-2937 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it has a policy on the position of higher education in the General Agreement on Trade in Services, in light of the statement by the European Commission Directorate-General for Education and Culture that only one third of European ministries have developed such a policy.

S2W-2938 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it has considered the possible impacts of the General Agreement on Trade in Services on education systems from a legal and practical perspective, in light of the conclusions of the Bologna follow-up seminar "Exploring the Social Dimensions of the European Higher Education Area" that such impacts should be assessed in each country.

S2W-2939 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it has consulted higher education stakeholders on the General Agreement on Trade in Services (GATS), in light of the conclusions of the Bologna follow-up seminar "Exploring the Social Dimensions of the European Higher Education Area" which expressed "the need for transparency in the GATS negotiations and that GATS negotiators should consult closely the higher education stakeholders".

S2W-2949 Bill Aitken: To ask the Scottish Executive, with regard to the draft Debt Arrangement Scheme (Scotland) Regulations 2003, what the role of money advisers and DAS administrators will be in respect of debt management, collection and the making of payments to creditors.

S2W-2950 Michael Matheson: To ask the Scottish Executive what the underspend in the Justice budget was for 2002-03 and under which budget headings the underspend took place.

S2W-2951 Mike Pringle: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-2140 by Mrs Mary Mulligan on 19 September 2003, whether City of Edinburgh and Midlothian councils would be required to publish the reasons for rejecting the options of redevelopment of the existing ground, development within urban areas or development on vacant, derelict or underused land before approving the adjustment of greenbelt boundaries to allow construction of a new stadium.

S2W-2952 Mike Pringle: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to the question S2W-2110 by Mrs Mary Mulligan on 16 September 2003, what material considerations, other than Executive planning policies, may be taken into account by councils in reaching planning decisions that are not in accordance with the development plan.

S2W-2953 Mike Pringle: To ask the Scottish Executive when the First Minister last met the Member of Parliament for Edinburgh South and what devolved issues were discussed.

S2W-2954 Mike Pringle: To ask the Scottish Executive whether the First Minister has any plans to meet the Member of Parliament for Edinburgh South to discuss the relocation of Scottish Natural Heritage.

S2W-2955 Tommy Sheridan: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will initiate a public inquiry into contaminated blood products in the NHS and what the reasons are for its position on the matter.

S2W-2956 Donald Gorrie: To ask the Scottish Executive what facilities are available in each local authority area for recycling plastics.

S2W-2957 Mike Pringle: To ask the Scottish Executive what specialist assistance is available for victims of miscarriages of justice and whether such support extends to relatives of those found to have been wrongly convicted.

S2W-2958 Mike Pringle: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it has made any analysis of the effects that miscarriages of justice have on those wrongfully convicted.

S2W-2959 Mike Pringle: To ask the Scottish Executive how many people have been released from prison following wrongful imprisonment in each of the last 10 years.

S2W-2960 Mike Pringle: To ask the Scottish Executive what measures of redress a wrongfully-convicted person has following a successful court appeal.

S2W-2961 Mike Pringle: To ask the Scottish Executive whether victims of miscarriages of justice receive an official apology following their release from prison.

S2W-2962 Mike Pringle: To ask the Scottish Executive whether wrongfully-convicted persons and their families would be entitled to any compensatory payment following release from prison.

S2W-2963 Donald Gorrie: To ask the Scottish Executive what disbursement it has made from the Dewar Arts Awards; to whom such disbursements have been made, and in respect of which activities.

S2W-2964 Donald Gorrie: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-1728 by Peter Peacock on 26 August 2003, which junior groups have benefited from sportscotland's lottery sports funding since the inception of the Junior Group Programme.

S2W-2965 Donald Gorrie: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-1724 by Peter Peacock on 19 August 2003, whether it will provide a substantive example of its commitment to the provision of special needs education, as referred to in A Partnership for a Better Scotland.

S2W-2966 Donald Gorrie: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-1728 by Peter Peacock on 19 August 2003, how many children are accessing each of the new centres of excellence referred to, broken down by centre and sphere of activity.
