Business Bulletin No. 135/2010: Tuesday 21 September 2010
Contents An A B C D E F G H I J

Section E – Written questions lodged on 20 September 2010

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

Questions in which a member has indicated a declarable interest are marked with an "R".

S3W-36373 Ross Finnie: To ask the Scottish Executive whether the £1.6 million of government funding for the Family Nurse Partnership pilot in Lothian covers the whole cost of the pilot or whether funding is also being provided by NHS Lothian or other partners.

S3W-36374 Ross Finnie: To ask the Scottish Executive how many complaints were made about the NHS in 2009-10, broken down by NHS board.

S3W-36375 Ross Finnie: To ask the Scottish Executive how many complaints were made about NHS hospital and community services in 2009-10, broken down by NHS board.

S3W-36376 Ross Finnie: To ask the Scottish Executive how many complaints were made about NHS 24 in 2009-10, broken down by NHS board.

S3W-36377 Ross Finnie: To ask the Scottish Executive how many complaints were made about NHS family health services in 2009-10, broken down by NHS board.

S3W-36378 Ross Finnie: To ask the Scottish Executive how many complaints were made about the Scottish Ambulance Service in 2009-10, broken down by NHS board area.

S3W-36379 Ross Finnie: To ask the Scottish Executive what the most recent estimate is of the annual cost to the NHS of treating long-term conditions, also expressed as a percentage of NHS annual expenditure.

S3W-36380 Ross Finnie: To ask the Scottish Executive what the most recent estimate is of the annual cost of hospital-based care for people with long-term conditions.

S3W-36381 Ross Finnie: To ask the Scottish Executive what the most recent estimate is of the annual cost of community-based care for people with long-term conditions.

S3W-36382 Ross Finnie: To ask the Scottish Executive what the most recent estimate is of the annual cost to the NHS of cardiovascular disease.

S3W-36383 Ross Finnie: To ask the Scottish Executive what the cost is of the Active Nation campaign.

S3W-36384 Ross Finnie: To ask the Scottish Executive how many hospital admissions of patients aged 60 and over there have been in each year since 2000, broken down by NHS board.

S3W-36385 Ross Finnie: To ask the Scottish Executive what the cost to the NHS has been of hospital inpatient care for patients aged 60 and over in each year since 2000, broken down by NHS board.

S3W-36386 Ross Finnie: To ask the Scottish Executive what estimate it has made of the impact that free personal care has had in terms of reducing hospital admissions among older patients.

S3W-36387 Margaret Smith: To ask the Scottish Executive what savings it has estimated that there will be for (a) local authorities and (b) if its proposals to cease the collection and publication of a range of national education statistics are enacted and from where these savings will come.

S3W-36388 Margaret Smith: To ask the Scottish Executive what estimate it has made of the number of man-hours that will be saved should its proposals to cease collection and publication of a range of national education statistics be enacted.

S3W-36389 Margaret Smith: To ask the Scottish Executive which local authorities it has consulted regarding its proposals to cease the collection and publication of a range of national education statistics; when the consultation was undertaken; what the findings were, and whether the findings are available publicly.

S3W-36390 Margaret Smith: To ask the Scottish Executive what stakeholders it has consulted regarding its proposals to cease the collection and publication of a range of national education statistics; when the consultation was undertaken; what the findings were, and whether the findings are available publicly.

S3W-36391 Gavin Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive how many people were convicted for an offence with a domestic aggravator in 2009-10, broken down by police force area.

S3W-36392 Gavin Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive how many people have been treated for (a) drug and (b) alcohol addiction in each NHS board in each year since 2007-08.

S3W-36393 Gavin Brown: To ask the Scottish Executive how many offenders are currently incarcerated in HMP Edinburgh for carrying an offensive weapon.

S3W-36394 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive what vacant posts at Transport Scotland have been left unfilled in the last 12 months as part of the cost-saving exercise.

S3W-36395 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive what vacant posts in its directorates with responsibility for the NHS have been left unfilled in the last 12 months as part of the cost-saving exercise.

S3W-36396 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive what vacant posts in its directorates for learning and lifelong learning have been left unfilled in the last 12 months as part of the cost-saving exercise.

S3W-36397 Patricia Ferguson: To ask the Scottish Executive what discussions it has had with the UK Government concerning its review of the Department for International Development.

S3W-36398 Patricia Ferguson: To ask the Scottish Executive what action it is taking to prevent fire hydrants from being vandalised.

S3W-36399 Patricia Ferguson: To ask the Scottish Executive how much water it estimates is lost each year as a result of fire hydrants being vandalised.

S3W-36400 Patricia Ferguson: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will estimate the cost of water lost each year as a result of fire hydrants being vandalised.

S3W-36401 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S3M-35909 by John Swinney on 15 September 2010, whether it will make a significant reduction in the ministerial car pool and what the reasons are for its position on this matter.

S3W-36402 Iain Gray: To ask the Scottish Executive what representations it has made to the UK Government regarding the implementation of a ban on ship-to-ship transfers of oil in UK territorial waters.

S3W-36403 Christina McKelvie: To ask the Scottish Executive what support it gives to the development of rugby.

S3W-36404 Christina McKelvie: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it is satisfied that dawn raids on asylum seeker families have ended.

S3W-36405 Christina McKelvie: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will enhance the assistance that it gives to asylum seekers and, if so, how.

S3W-36406 Christina McKelvie: To ask the Scottish Executive what benefits accrued from the Cabinet’s summer programme.

S3W-36407 Christina McKelvie: To ask the Scottish Executive what steps it is taking to reach the 2020 emissions reduction target of 42% and how it is measuring progress.

S3W-36408 Marlyn Glen: To ask the Scottish Executive how many hospital admissions there have been of people aged 65 and over in Dundee as a result of falls in each of the last three years for which information is available, broken down by intermediate geographical zone, also expressed as a rate per 1,000 of population.

S3W-36409 Marlyn Glen: To ask the Scottish Executive how many hospital admissions there have been of people aged 65 and over in Angus as a result of falls in each of the last three years for which information is available, broken down by intermediate geographical zone, also expressed as a rate per 1,000 of population.

S3W-36410 Elaine Smith: To ask the Scottish Executive how many ministerial engagements have been held in the Coatbridge and Chryston parliamentary constituency since 1 January 2010, broken down by (a) ministerial portfolio, (b) date and (c) location.
