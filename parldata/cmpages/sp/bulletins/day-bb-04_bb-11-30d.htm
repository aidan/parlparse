Business Bulletin No. 176/2004

Tuesday 30 November 2004

Section D : Oral questions selected for answer on 2 December 2004
  

First Minister’s Question Time

1. Nicola Sturgeon: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive’s Cabinet. (S2F-1243)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Prime Minister and what issues will be discussed. (S2F-1244)

3. Robin Harper: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he intends to discuss. (S2F-1253)

4. Mr Duncan McNeil: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive will ensure that the Parole Board for Scotland upholds the rights of victims and their families. (S2F-1252)

5. Brian Adam: To ask the First Minister how many public sector jobs will be cut under its efficient government strategy and how this will impact on services to the public. (S2F-1255)

6. Donald Gorrie: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive is addressing sectarian abuse and violence. (S2F-1260)
