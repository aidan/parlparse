BUSINESS BULLETIN 18 / 2000

Tuesday 1 February 2000

Section D –  Oral questions selected for answer on 3 February 2000

 

 

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr Alex Salmond: To ask the First Minister when he next plans to visit the Westminster Parliament. (S1F-72)

 

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister whether he will outline the Scottish Executive’s priorities. (S1F-74)

 

3. Mr Brian Monteith: To ask the First Minister what the Scottish Executive’s timetable is for the publication of the guidelines with regard to the repeal of Section 2A of the Local Government (Scotland) Act 1986. (S1F-75)

 

4. Maureen Macmillan: To ask the First Minister what plans there are for the future of the Convention of the Highlands and Islands. (S1F-79)

 

5. Nicola Sturgeon: To ask the First Minister what action the Scottish Executive is taking to improve school buildings in Scotland. (S1F-76)

 

6. Dennis Canavan: To ask the First Minister whether he will make a statement about amputations of healthy limbs in National Health Service hospitals. (S1F-70)
