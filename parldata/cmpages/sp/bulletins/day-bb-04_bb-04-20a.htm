Business Bulletin No. 65 / 2004

Tuesday 20th April 2004

Section A : Daily Business List
  

Meetings of Committees

All meetings take place in Committee Chambers, Edinburgh unless otherwise indicated.

Equal Opportunities Committee

7th Meeting, 2004

The Committee will meet at 10.00 am in Committee Room 1

1. Item in private: The Committee will consider whether to take item 5 in private.

2. Budget process 2005-06: The Committee will receive a briefing from Professor Arthur Midwinter, budget adviser to the Finance Committee.

3. Petition PE522: The Committee will consider a response from the Deputy Minister for Health and Community Care in relation to petition PE522 from Carol Main on care homes for young physically disabled people.

4. Reporters: The Committee will hear from its Reporters.

5. Disability inquiry: The Committee will consider a background paper.

Paul Grice, Clerk and Chief Executive, Scottish Parliament;
