Business Bulletin No. 176/2009: Thursday 26 November 2009
Contents An A B C D E F G H I J

Section D - Oral questions selected for answer on 3 December 2009

Question Time

General

1. David Whitton: To ask the Scottish Executive how it responds to the recent statement by six business organisations concerning the importance of the Glasgow Airport Rail Link to Scotland as a whole, its affordability and the case for its reinstatement in the draft Budget for 2010-11. (S3O-8722)

2. Mr Frank McAveety: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it considers that the Glasgow Airport Rail Link would have a positive impact on Scotland’s international connectivity. (S3O-8704)

3. Bill Butler: To ask the Scottish Executive what financial institutions it is working with to improve financial education in schools. (S3O-8686)

4. Derek Brownlee: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it is satisfied with Scotland’s overall performance in education. (S3O-8659)

5. Mike Pringle: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it plans to sign up to the 10:10 campaign pledge to reduce its carbon emissions by 10% in 2010. (S3O-8673)

6. Lewis Macdonald: To ask the Scottish Executive what annual provision will be required to meet the costs of the Aberdeen Western Peripheral Route over the next thirty years, if it is procured under the NPD model. (S3O-8706)

7. Ross Finnie: To ask the Scottish Executive, in light of its announcement that any application for a new coal plant in Scotland will need to demonstrate carbon capture and storage (CCS) on a minimum of 300MW (net) of capacity from its first day of operation, whether all aspects of the CCS chain will be expected to be working from that first day. (S3O-8669)

8. Liam McArthur: To ask the Scottish Executive what assessment it has made of the impact on Orkney of the planned introduction of car parking charges at Kirkwall Airport. (S3O-8671)

9. Hugh Henry: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to its freedom of information reply of 12 February 2008 regarding a meeting with the deans of faculties of education, which ministers were offered the advice on class sizes that "the scale of the commitment does not allow it to be delivered in the lifetime of a parliament". (S3O-8697)

10. Ms Wendy Alexander: To ask the Scottish Executive, if further capital is accelerated in the pre Budget report, whether it will consider reinstating the Glasgow Airport Rail Link as requested by Scotland’s six principal business organisations. (S3O-8680)

Health and Wellbeing

1. Stuart McMillan: To ask the Scottish Executive what its housing priorities are for the next 12 months. (S3O-8742)

2. James Kelly: To ask the Scottish Executive what discussions the Cabinet Secretary for Health and Wellbeing has had with the Cabinet Secretary for Finance and Sustainable Growth relating to the 2010-11 draft budget. (S3O-8699)

3. Duncan McNeil: To ask the Scottish Executive when the NHS Aroma cafe concept will be fully evaluated. (S3O-8709)

4. Johann Lamont: To ask the Scottish Executive when the Cabinet Secretary for Health and Wellbeing last met voluntary sector mental health service providers and what issues were discussed. (S3O-8701)

5. Willie Coffey: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it has discussed with the UK Government the impact on Scotland’s older disabled people of changes proposed to attendance allowance. (S3O-8726)

6. Mary Mulligan: To ask the Scottish Executive what progress has been made in delivering the Low-cost Initiative for First-Time Buyers open market programme. (S3O-8712)

7. Michael Matheson: To ask the Scottish Executive how public authorities will be expected to exercise their duties under the International Covenant on Economic, Social and Cultural Rights when withdrawing funding for mental health services. (S3O-8737)

8. Angela Constance: To ask the Scottish Executive what specialist health services are available to children with epilepsy in West Lothian. (S3O-8727)

9. Mary Scanlon: To ask the Scottish Executive what action has been taken to address problems with the patient transport service in remote and rural areas. (S3O-8664)

10. Malcolm Chisholm: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will issue guidance on the tendering of community care services and the right to direct payments. (S3O-8687)

11. Paul Martin: To ask the Scottish Executive when ministers last met the Chief Executive of NHS Greater Glasgow and Clyde and what issues were discussed. (S3O-8703)

12. John Park: To ask the Scottish Executive what it is doing to support grassroots football. (S3O-8715)

13. Ken Macintosh: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it has to review the regulations with regard to the administration of medicines in schools. (S3O-8707)

14. Christopher Harvie: To ask the Scottish Executive what data are available on possible health effects from bus emissions in Edinburgh. (S3O-8734)

15. Dr Richard Simpson: To ask the Scottish Executive what its position is on the programme undertaken by Glasgow Addiction Services to provide supervised use of disulfiram in the treatment of individuals with alcohol problems. (S3O-8718)

16. Maureen Watt: To ask the Scottish Executive what discussions it has had with COSLA regarding the implications for social care of the UK Government’s proposals to change attendance allowance. (S3O-8747)

17. Bill Kidd: To ask the Scottish Executive what effects it considers the UK Government’s proposed changes to attendance allowance and disability living allowance would have on the provision of services to older and disabled people in Scotland. (S3O-8735)

18. Robin Harper: To ask the Scottish Executive what progress is being made on improving the sexual health of young people. (S3O-8665)

19. Margaret Curran: To ask the Scottish Executive what discussions the Cabinet Secretary for Health and Wellbeing has had with NHS Greater Glasgow and Clyde regarding hospital services in Glasgow. (S3O-8689)

20. Christina McKelvie: To ask the Scottish Executive what percentage of the NHS Scotland budget is represented by prescription drug costs and how that figure compares with those from 2005-06 to 2007-08. (S3O-8741)
