Business Bulletin No. 101 / 2004

Monday 14th June 2004

Section E : Written questions lodged on Friday 11th June 2004
  

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S2W-8953 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive when it was advised of any further proposed extension of Dungavel House Immigration Removal Centre.

S2W-8954 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it has made any representations regarding the impact of any further proposed extension to Dungavel House Immigration Removal Centre.

S2W-8955 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-2928 by Ms Margaret Curran on 23 October 2003, when it first became aware of any proposed further extension to Dungavel House Immigration Removal Centre and whether there are any other plans to (a) extend or (b) make alterations to the centre.

S2W-8956 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive whether South Lanarkshire Council has been advised of any further proposed extension of Dungavel House Immigration Removal Centre.

S2W-8957 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive whether local residents have been advised of any further proposed extension of Dungavel House Immigration Removal Centre.

S2W-8958 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive what information it has on the current capacity of Dungavel House Immigration Removal Centre, broken down into single person units, shared accommodation and individual family units, and what the capacity of the centre will be following any further proposed extension.

S2W-8959 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive what information it has on how many adults and children who are unrelated to each other will be detained in Dungavel House Immigration Removal Centre's shared facilities, following any proposed further extension of the centre.

S2W-8960 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive what the responsibilities are of its ministers in relation to the operation of Dungavel House Immigration Removal Centre, including any responsibilities arising from the detention of children within the centre.

S2W-8961 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-2930 by Ms Margaret Curran on 23 October 2003, what the outcome was of the minister raising with the Home Office the specific issue of fingerprinting and photographing of visitors to Dungavel House Immigration Removal Centre.

S2W-8962 Linda Fabiani: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-2671 by Ms Margaret Curran on 7 October 2003, what the outcome was of the minister conveying to Her Majesty's Government the concerns of the Parliament about the detention of children at Dungavel House Immigration Removal Centre, as referred to in the resolution of the Parliament on 11 September 2003.

S2W-8963 Miss Annabel Goldie: To ask the Scottish Executive how it plans to ensure that there is a fair and sustainable policy in respect of landfill.

S2W-8964 Colin Fox: To ask the Scottish Executive what steps it is taking to prevent demolition of listed buildings in Edinburgh including those on the site of the old Royal Infirmary of Edinburgh at Lauriston.

S2W-8965 Margo MacDonald: To ask the Scottish Executive what work has been undertaken by the Holyrood Evidence Unit of its Legal and Parliamentary Services department.

S2W-8966 Margo MacDonald: To ask the Scottish Parliamentary Corporate Body how many parliamentary staff will be accommodated outwith the new parliamentary complex at Holyrood.

S2W-8967 Margo MacDonald: To ask the Scottish Executive how many staff are employed in the Holyrood Evidence Unit of its Legal and Parliamentary Services department; for how long such staff have been employed in the unit, and at what cost.

S2W-8968 Margo MacDonald: To ask the Scottish Parliamentary Corporate Body what arrangements are in place, or are planned, for the accommodation of parliamentary staff outwith the new Parliament building.

S2W-8969 Mr Duncan McNeil: To ask the Scottish Executive what targets it has set for the processing of applications by Disclosure Scotland and how its performance against these targets is monitored.

S2W-8970 Christine Grahame: To ask the Scottish Executive what consideration has been given during the review of the children's hearings system to how the provisions within the Additional Support for Learning (Scotland) Act 2004 relate to children's panels.

S2W-8971 Christine Grahame: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will review the NHS continuing care criteria introduced in 1996 and what the reasons are for its position on this matter.

S2W-8972 Christine Grahame: To ask the Scottish Executive whether the NHS continuing care criteria are standardised across Scotland.

S2W-8973 Sarah Boyack: To ask the Scottish Executive how it will ensure an increased take-up rate of Scottish Community and Householder Renewables Initiative grants over the next 18 months.

S2W-8974 Sarah Boyack: To ask the Scottish Executive whether there has been any take-up of the extension of Scottish Community and Householder Renewables Initiative grants to builders and developers.

S2W-8975 Sarah Boyack: To ask the Scottish Executive how it will ensure that builders and developers apply for Scottish Community and Householder Renewables Initiative grants.

S2W-8976 John Swinburne: To ask the Scottish Executive, further to the answer to question S2W-5121 by Nicol Stephen on 23 January 2004, whether this matter has now been raised with the Confederation for Passenger Transport and the operator concerned and, if so, what the outcome of these representations was.
