Section E – Written questions lodged on 4 February 2005

Questions marked with a triangle ( Δ ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

S2W-14105 Alasdair Morgan: To ask the Scottish Executive how many businesses appealed against their assessed rateable value following the last revaluation of rateable values and, of these, how many were (a) wholly and (b) partly successful.

S2W-14106 Alasdair Morgan: To ask the Scottish Executive what the total reduction in rateable values was as a result of wholly and partly successful appeals by businesses against their assessed rateable value following the last revaluation of rateable values, broken down by local authority area, expressed also as a percentage of the total rateable value in each case.

S2W-14107 Jeremy Purvis: To ask the Scottish Executive how many community police officers there are in each police force area.

S2W-14109 Carolyn Leckie: To ask the Scottish Executive how many women leaving HM Prison Cornton Vale (a) have and (b) have not received further custodial sentences in each year since 1999.

S2W-14111 Ms Sandra White: To ask the Scottish Executive how many telephone interception applications were made to it and, of these, how many were authorised in (a) 1999, (b) 2000, (c) 2001, (d) 2002, (e) 2003 and (f) 2004, broken down by source of application.
