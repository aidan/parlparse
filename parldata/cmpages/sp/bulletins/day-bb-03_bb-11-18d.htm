Business Bulletin No. 156/2003

Tuesday 18 November 2003

Section D : Oral Questions
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive's cabinet. (S2F-358)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he next plans to meet the Prime Minister and what issues he intends to raise. (S2F-363)

3. Robin Harper: To ask the First Minister what issues were raised at the last meeting of the Cabinet sub-committee on sustainable Scotland. (S2F-371)

4. Elaine Smith: To ask the First Minister what support is being provided for further education colleges attempting to meet the Scottish Further Education Funding Council's requirements for achieving financial security by July 2006. (S2F-357)

5. Nora Radcliffe: To ask the First Minister when the Scottish Executive will announce its decision with regard to the fluoridation of water supplies. (S2F-369)

6. Nicola Sturgeon: To ask the First Minister what matters were discussed at the conference of European regions with legislative power on 11 and 12 November 2003. (S2F-366)
