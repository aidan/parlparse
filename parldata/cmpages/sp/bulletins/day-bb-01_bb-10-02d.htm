Business Bulletin No. 142/2001

Tuesday 2 October 2001

Section D : Oral Questions
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive's Cabinet. (S1F-01275)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he plans to raise. (S1F-01277)

3. Lord James Douglas-Hamilton: To ask the First Minister whether the policy on the introduction of voluntary or compulsory identity cards will be the same in Scotland as in England and Wales and what discussions the Scottish Executive has had with Her Majesty's Government on this matter. (S1F-01276)

4. Mr John Home Robertson: To ask the First Minister what impact the recently announced £5 million funding for social inclusion partnerships will have on the campaign against drug misuse. (S1F-01286)

5. Ian Jenkins: To ask the First Minister what steps are being taken to promote Scotland as a centre of golf tourism in light of the decision that Scotland will not host the Ryder Cup golf championship until 2014. (S1F-01289)

6. Nicola Sturgeon: To ask the First Minister whether the Scottish Executive will introduce legislation to ban tobacco advertising in Scotland should Her Majesty's Government not do so and, if so, when. (S1F-01278)
