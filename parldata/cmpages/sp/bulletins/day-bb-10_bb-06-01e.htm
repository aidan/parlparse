Business Bulletin No. 91/2010: Tuesday 1 June 2010
Contents An A B C D E F G H I J

Section E – Written questions lodged on 27 May 2010

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

Questions in which a member has indicated a declarable interest are marked with an "R".

S3W-33896 Bill Butler: To ask the Scottish Executive how many consultancy contracts have been signed off at director general and cabinet secretary level since the introduction of guidelines on consultancy by the Cabinet Secretary for Finance and Sustainable Growth in December 2008, broken down by (a) date, (b) consultant, (c) amount authorised and length and remit of each contract and (d) director general or cabinet secretary.

S3W-33897 Liam McArthur: To ask the Scottish Executive how many organisations receiving financial support for undertaking waste reduction, landfill diversion or recycling programmes have had their funding reduced by the Scottish Government in the last 12 months and by what amount the funding was reduced in comparison with the total cost of each programme.

S3W-33926 Liam McArthur: To ask the Scottish Executive whether organisations receiving financial support for undertaking waste reduction, landfill diversion or recycling programmes and that have had their funding reduced by the Scottish Government in the last 12 months will be offered a chance to appeal against any such reductions.

S3W-33927 Liam McArthur: To ask the Scottish Executive, where funding for organisations receiving financial support for undertaking waste reduction, landfill diversion or recycling programmes has been reduced in the last 12 months, where this funding has been or will be redirected.

S3W-33932 John Wilson: To ask the Scottish Executive how much each local authority will save in terms of efficiency savings in 2010-11.

S3W-33933 John Wilson: To ask the Scottish Executive how many and what publications have been issued by the Scottish Housing Regulator since it came into existence, broken down by publication date.

S3W-33934 John Wilson: To ask the Scottish Executive what the cost was of each publication produced by the Scottish Housing Regulator since its inception.

S3W-33935 John Wilson: To ask the Scottish Executive how many evictions by social landlords have taken place in each local authority area in each year since 2006.

S3W-33936 John Wilson: To ask the Scottish Executive how many local councillors have been appointed to quangos since 2006, broken down by (a) title, (b) date, (c) political affiliation and (d) local authority.

S3W-33937 John Wilson: To ask the Scottish Executive how many local councillors have been represented on Scottish Government working groups since 2006, broken down by (a) date, (b) title, (c) political affiliation and (d) working group.

S3W-33938 Elaine Murray: To ask the Scottish Executive how many breaches of the diffuse pollution regulations were recorded by Scotland’s Environment and Rural Services (SEARS) in 2009 and which general binding rules those breaches related to.

S3W-33939 Elaine Murray: To ask the Scottish Executive how many of the breaches of the diffuse pollution regulations recorded by Scotland’s Environment and Rural Services (SEARS) 2009 have been remediated and what action is being taken for those that have not been.

S3W-33940 Bill Butler: To ask the Scottish Executive how much it has received in Barnett consequentials since 1 April 2007, broken down by individual amount.

S3W-33941 Malcolm Chisholm: To ask the Scottish Executive what contact there has been between its officials and Forth Energy in relation to the proposed biomass plant at Leith docks.

S3W-33942 Malcolm Chisholm: To ask the Scottish Executive what contact there has been between its officials and Forth Energy in relation to the proposed biomass plant at Leith docks.

S3W-33943 Malcolm Chisholm: To ask the Scottish Executive how many people submitted views to its Energy Consents Unit following the environmental scoping study for the proposed biomass plant at Leith docks.

S3W-33944 Malcolm Chisholm: To ask the Scottish Executive when it will make a decision on the proposed biomass plant at Leith docks if an application is submitted by Forth Energy in summer 2010.

S3W-33945 Malcolm Chisholm: To ask the Scottish Executive what account it will take of local opposition before coming to a decision on whether to grant permission for the proposed biomass plant at Leith docks.

S3W-33946 Ms Wendy Alexander: To ask the Scottish Executive how many jobs it estimates were saved in Scotland in 2009 and have been saved in 2010 by the discretionary fiscal stimulus.

S3W-33947 Cathy Jamieson: To ask the Scottish Executive what discussions it has had with East Ayrshire Council regarding the provision of pre-five education.

S3W-33948 Cathy Jamieson: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will list all meetings attended by the Minister for Children and Early Years relating to the provision of pre-five education.

S3W-33949 Cathy Jamieson: To ask the Scottish Executive what representations it has received from parents regarding the Jacaranda Nursery in East Ayrshire and what action it has taken as a result.

S3W-33950 Cathy Jamieson: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it considers that East Ayrshire Council’s decision to withdraw funding for places at the Jacaranda Nursery in East Ayrshire is in keeping with the principles of the Early Years Framework.

S3W-33951 Patrick Harvie: To ask the Scottish Executive on what dates ministerial directions, as described under section 6.4 of the current Scottish Ministerial Code regarding a course of action that the Accountable Officer considers would breach the requirements of propriety or regularity, have been issued since 1999; which minister each direction was issued by, and what the subject was of each direction.

S3W-33952 Patrick Harvie: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will publish all ministerial directions, as described under section 6.4 of the Scottish Ministerial Code, that have been issued since 2007.

S3W-33953 Patrick Harvie: To ask the Scottish Executive which ministerial directions issued since 2007, as described under section 6.4 of the Scottish Ministerial Code, have been formally discussed at (a) Cabinet, (b) between the issuing minister and other ministers and in which cases the issuing minister received advice on the direction from special advisors.

S3W-33954 Rob Gibson: To ask the Scottish Executive what steps have been taken to limit any damage to the soil on the farm near Coupar Angus where potato seed infected with Dickeya solani imported from the Netherlands via a farm in England has been found.

S3W-33955 Rob Gibson: To ask the Scottish Executive what the new planting rates are in Scottish forests.

S3W-33956 Jamie Stone: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans NHS Highland has to reduce medical services being offered to patients via the Dunbar Hospital in Thurso.

S3W-33957 Jamie Stone: To ask the Scottish Executive what criteria it would use in agreeing to any plans by NHS Highland to reduce medical services being delivered via a local hospital.

S3W-33958 John Park: To ask the Scottish Executive how many adult apprenticeship starts are being funded in 2010-11 by Skills Development Scotland, also broken down by (a) local authority area and (b) sector.

S3W-33959 Tavish Scott: To ask the Scottish Executive what the value to date is of (a) all contracts issued under the Rural Development Contracts - Rural Priorities scheme; (b) payments made in (i) Shetland, (ii) Orkney, (iii) Argyll, (iv) Ayrshire, (v) Borders, (vi) Clyde Valley, (vii) Dumfries and Galloway, (viii) Forth, (ix) Grampian, (x) Highland, (xi) Outer Hebrides and (xii) Tayside.

S3W-33960 Tavish Scott: To ask the Scottish Executive what is the total number of contracts under the Rural Development Contracts - Rural Priorities scheme issued to date; and how many have been paid in (a) part and (b) full in (i) Shetland, (ii) Orkney, (iii) Argyll, (iv) Ayrshire, (v) Borders, (vi) Clyde Valley, (vii) Dumfries and Galloway, (viii) Forth, (ix) Grampian, (x) Highland, (xi) Outer Hebrides and (xii) Tayside.

S3W-33961 Tavish Scott: To ask the Scottish Executive what action has to be taken, or information supplied, by successful applicants for contracts under the Rural Development Contracts - Rural Priorities scheme before they receive (a) part or (b) full payment of the value of their contract.

S3W-33962 Sarah Boyack: To ask the Scottish Executive what discussions it has had, or plans to have, with the UK Government about its proposed white paper on nature; whether the paper is expected to address any matters affecting Scotland and, if so, what opportunities stakeholders in Scotland will have to comment on such matters.

S3W-33963 Cathie Craigie: To ask the Scottish Executive how many applications to its boiler scrappage scheme were made by residents of North Lanarkshire.

S3W-33964 Cathie Craigie: To ask the Scottish Executive how many residents of North Lanarkshire were successful in gaining financial support to replace their boiler through the boiler scrappage scheme.

S3W-33965 Cathie Craigie: To ask the Scottish Executive how many applications to its boiler scrappage scheme were made by residents of the Cumbernauld and Kilsyth parliamentary constituency.

S3W-33966 Cathie Craigie: To ask the Scottish Executive how many residents of the Cumbernauld and Kilsyth parliamentary constituency were successful in gaining financial support to replace their boiler through the boiler scrappage scheme.

S3W-33967 Patrick Harvie: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it has the legal power to prohibit members of organisations such as the British National Party from holding public service posts including that of classroom teacher.

S3W-33968 Patrick Harvie: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it is aware of any members of the British National Party who are currently employed as classroom teachers in Scottish schools.

S3W-33969 Cathie Craigie: To ask the Scottish Executive how many residents of the Cumbernauld and Kilsyth parliamentary constituency has received a new central heating system under the Energy Assistance Package in each year since it was established.

S3W-33970 Johann Lamont: To ask the Scottish Executive what procurement practices it is using to ensure that the companies it awards contracts to pay their employees a living wage.

S3W-33971 Johann Lamont: To ask the Scottish Executive what steps it is taking to improve the provision of data on low pay across Scotland.
