Business Bulletin No. 145/2007: Friday 2 November 2007
Contents An A B C D E F G H I J

Section E – Written questions lodged on 1 November 2007

Questions marked with a triangle (Δ) are initiated by the Scottish Executive in order to facilitate the provision of information to the Parliament.

Questions in which a member has indicated a declarable interest are marked with an "R".

S3W-5891 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive how many classes in each of primary 1 to primary 3 have (a) more than 30, (b) 25 to 30, (c) 20 to 24, (d) 18 to 19 and (e) less than 18 pupils.

S3W-5892 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive what responsibilities (a) chief constables, (b) police boards and (c) Scottish ministers have in relation to the operational deployment of police officers and whether there are any plans to change such responsibilities.

S3W-5893 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive who has responsibility for deciding whether any proposed expenditure by it is ultra vires.

S3W-5894 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive what advice has been given to local authorities regarding proposed school closures and the policy of reducing class sizes to 18 in primary 1 to primary 3.

S3W-5895 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive what sporting events have been attended by the Minister for Communities and Sport since May 2007.

S3W-5896 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive what percentage of people aged 65 and over are in receipt of free personal care, broken down by local authority.

S3W-5897 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive what the cost of free personal care was in 2006-07, broken down by local authority.

S3W-5898 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive what proposals for renewable energy generation have been considered by ministers since May 2007 and what the outcome has been in each case.

S3W-5899 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive what percentage of electricity has been generated from (a) coal, (b) nuclear power, (c) renewables, (d) gas and (e) other means in each month since May 2007.

S3W-5900 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive how many schools in each local authority area have been designated as community schools with extended access and opening hours.

S3W-5901 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive what the difference is between its plan for upgrading the full length of the M8 to motorway standard and the proposals of the previous administration.

S3W-5902 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive which college and university developments will be supported by the funds released by HM Treasury, as announced by the Cabinet Secretary for Education and Lifelong Learning on 26 October 2007.

S3W-5903 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive what progress is being made in establishing the Scottish futures trust; when it is expected to be launched, and when local authorities will be able to apply for funding from the trust.

S3W-5904 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive what meetings it has held with the STUC since May 2007 and which ministers were involved in each case.

S3W-5905 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive what the prison population has been in each month since May 2007.

S3W-5906 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive when it expects bridge tolls to be abolished.

S3W-5907 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive what plans it has to increase support for Scottish music, poetry and painting.

S3W-5910 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive whom the First Minister met on his recent visit to the United States of America and what devolved matters were discussed in each case.

S3W-5911 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive what overseas official visits are planned for the First Minister in the next six months.

S3W-5912 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will list the overseas official visits made by each minister since May 2007, showing the cost of each visit.

S3W-5913 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will list the meetings held between Scottish and UK ministers since May 2007, showing the dates and topics discussed in each case.

S3W-5914 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive who is responsible for supervision of the Scottish Ministerial Code in respect of the conduct of the First Minister.

S3W-5915 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive what the costs of travel by official car were for each minister from May to October 2007.

S3W-5916 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will list the responsibilities for agricultural compensation payments in Scotland of the (a) European Commission, (b) UK Government and (c) Scottish Executive.

S3W-5917 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive whether it will list all the heads of government to whom the First Minister wrote regarding observer status for the Executive at the Nuclear Non-Proliferation Treaty meetings; how many replies have been received and from whom, and what the content has been of any such replies.

S3W-5918 George Foulkes: To ask the Scottish Executive when it will publish the bills to implement the proposals announced in the First Minister’s legislative programme statement on 5 September 2007.

S3W-5928 Jamie Hepburn: To ask the Scottish Executive how many people were diagnosed with influenza in (a) 1950, (b) 1960, (c) 1970 and (d) 1980 and how many have been diagnosed with it in each year since 1990, broken down by (i) NHS board area and (ii) age group.
