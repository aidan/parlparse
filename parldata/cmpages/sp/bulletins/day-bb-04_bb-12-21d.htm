Business Bulletin No.190/2004

Tuesday 21 December 2004

Section D : Oral questions selected for answer on 23 December 2004
  

First Minister’s Question Time

1. Nicola Sturgeon: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Prime Minister and what issues will be discussed. (S2F-1314)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive’s Cabinet. (S2F-1324)

3. Rosie Kane: To ask the First Minister whether he will be receiving any official visitors from Lapland over the Christmas period. (S2F-1318)

4. Dr Elaine Murray: To ask the First Minister how the Protection of Children (Scotland) Act 2003 is affecting volunteering. (S2F-1322)

5. Fergus Ewing: To ask the First Minister whether tolls will still be charged for crossing the Skye bridge on and after 1 January 2005. (S2F-1327)

6. Mr Brian Monteith: To ask the First Minister whether the Scottish Executive will announce its support for either a joint bid with other nations or a single bid by the Scottish Football Association to host the UEFA European Championships in 2012. (S2F-1333)
