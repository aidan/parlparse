Friday 13 October 2000

 


Section A - Daily Business List

Meeting of the Parliament

2.00 pm

Tributes to the Right Honourable Donald Dewar, MP, MSP

S1M-1260 Jim Wallace: Motion of Condolence- That the Parliament expresses its deep regret and sadness at the untimely death of its First Minister, The Rt Hon Donald Dewar MP, MSP; offers its sympathy and sincere condolences to his family and many friends whose grief is shared by all members of this Parliament; agrees that tributes should be paid to a distinguished Parliamentarian and great leader of Scotland; further agrees that as a mark of respect no other business will be taken today.
