Business Bulletin No. 33 / 2004

Tuesday 24th February 2004

Section D : Oral questions selected for answer on Thursday 26th February 2004
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister when he next plans to meet the Prime Minister and what issues he intends to raise. (S2F-656)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive's cabinet. (S2F-660)

3. Karen Gillon: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive ensures that projects specialising in the rehabilitation of young offenders are effective and represent good use of taxpayers' money. (S2F-674)

4. Richard Lochhead: To ask the First Minister whether any new measures are planned to help rural communities access land for affordable housing. (S2F-666)

5. Mr Keith Raffan: To ask the First Minister whether the Scottish Executive intends introducing random drug testing in schools. (S2F-672)

6. Alex Johnstone: To ask the First Minister who will make the final decision about whether GM crops are grown commercially in Scotland. (S2F-655)
