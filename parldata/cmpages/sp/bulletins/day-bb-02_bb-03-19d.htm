Business Bulletin No. 47/2002

Tuesday 19 March 2002

Section D : Oral Questions
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister when he last met the Prime Minister and what issues were discussed. (S1F-01762)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive’s Cabinet. (S1F-01763)

3. Nicola Sturgeon: To ask the First Minister what steps the Scottish Executive intends to take to ensure that patients are informed quickly and sensitively when mistakes affecting them occur in the NHS. (S1F-01774)

4. Janis Hughes: To ask the First Minister what reassurances the Scottish Executive will give regarding the future of community schools. (S1F-01781)

5. Donald Gorrie: To ask the First Minister what the current position is on the banning of tobacco advertising in Scotland. (S1F-01777)

6. Pauline McNeill: To ask the First Minister whether the report of the review of licensing laws will take into account the Scottish Executive’s strategy for tackling binge drinking. (S1F-01773)
