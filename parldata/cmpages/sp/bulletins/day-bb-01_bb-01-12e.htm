BUSINESS BULLETIN 6 / 2001

Friday 12 January 2001

Section E - Written questions lodged on 11 January 2001

 

 

S1W-12384 Alex Neil: To ask the Scottish Executive what steps it is taking to promote the European Year of Languages in schools.
