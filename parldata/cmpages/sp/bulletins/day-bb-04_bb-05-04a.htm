Business Bulletin No. 74/2004

Tuesday 4 May 2004

Section A : Daily Business List
  

Meetings of Committees

All meetings take place in Committee Chambers, Edinburgh unless otherwise indicated.

Equal Opportunities Committee

9th Meeting, 2004

The Committee will meet at 10.00 am in Committee Room 1

1. Item in private: The Committee will consider whether to take item 5 in private.

2. Declaration of interests: The new Member of the Committee will be invited to declare any relevant interests.

3. Budget process 2005-06: The Committee will hear evidence from-

Margaret Curran MSP, Minister for Communities.

4. Widening Access to Council Membership: The Committee will hear evidence from-

Rowena Arshad OBE, Chair of the Widening Access to Council Membership Progress Group

Susan Love, Widening Access to Council Membership Progress Group.

5. Budget process 2005-06: The Committee will agree its approach to its report to the Finance Committee.

Finance Committee

14th Meeting, 2004

The Committee will meet at 10.00 am in Committee Room 2

1. Inquiry into the Relocation of Public Sector Jobs: The Committee will take evidence as part of its inquiry from-

Tavish Scott, MSP, Deputy Minister for Finance and Public Services; and

Paul Rhodes, Head of Facilities and Estates Services and Mike Garden, Head of Corporate Responsibility Unit, Scottish Executive.

2. Budget process 2005-06: The Committee will take evidence as part of its consideration of Stage 1 of the 2005-06 Budget Process from-

Professor David Heald, University of Sheffield and Professor Irvine Lapsley, University of Edinburgh; and

Andrew Goudie, Chief Economic Adviser; Liz Lewis, Head of Ministerial Support Group and Richard Dennis, Finance Co-ordination Team Leader, Scottish Executive.

3. Items in private: The Committee will decide whether to consider the draft reports on the Financial Memoranda of the School Education (Ministerial Powers and Independent Schools) (Scotland) Bill and the Breastfeeding etc. (Scotland) Bill in private at its next meeting.

4. Cross Cutting Expenditure Review on Economic Development: The Committee will consider an approach paper by the Clerk for phase two of its review.

Subordinate Legislation Committee

15th Meeting, 2004

The Committee will meet at 10.30 am in Committee Room 3

1. Delegated powers scrutiny: The Committee will consider the responses from the Scottish Executive to points raised on the following Bill-

the National Health Service Reform (Scotland) Bill as amended at Stage 2.

2. Delegated powers scrutiny: The Committee will consider the delegated powers provisions in the following Bill-

the Emergency Workers (Scotland) Bill at Stage 1.

3. Executive responses: The Committee will consider the responses from the Scottish Executive to points raised on the following-

the Home Energy Efficiency Scheme Amendment (Scotland) Regulations 2004, (SSI 2004/188).

4. Draft instruments subject to approval: The Committee will consider the following-

the Budget (Scotland) Act 2004 Amendment Order 2004; and

the Health Professions (Operating Department Practitioners and Miscellaneous Amendments) Order 2004, (draft).

5. Instruments subject to annulment: The Committee will consider the following-

the European Communities (Services of Lawyers) Amendment (Scotland) Order 2004, (SSI 2004/186);

the Supervised Attendance Order (Prescribed Courts) (Scotland) Order 2004, (SSI 2004/194);

Act of Sederunt (Fees of Solicitors and Witnesses in the Sheriff Court) (Amendment No. 2) 2004, (SSI 2004/196); and

the Special Waste Amendment (Scotland) Amendment Regulations 2004 (SSI 2004/204).

6. Instruments not laid before Parliament: The Committee will consider the following-

Act of Adjournal (Criminal Procedure Rules Amendment) (Miscellaneous) 2004, (SSI 2004/195);

Act of Sederunt (Ordinary Cause, Summary Application, Summary Cause and Small Claim Rules) Amendment (Miscellaneous) 2004, (SSI 2004/197);

the Public Appointments and Public Bodies etc. (Scotland) Act 2003 (Commencement No. 6) Order 2004, (SSI 2004/198); and

the Freedom of Information (Scotland) Act 2002 (Commencement No.3) Order 2004, (SSI 2004/203).

Enterprise and Culture Committee

14th Meeting, 2004

The Committee will meet at 2.00 pm in the Chamber, Assembly Hall, The Mound, Edinburgh

1. Item in private: The Committee will decide whether to consider item 3 in private.

2. Broadband in Scotland: The Committee will take evidence from-

Lewis Macdonald MSP, Deputy Minister Enterprise and Lifelong Learning;

on its inquiry into broadband in Scotland.

3. Broadband in Scotland: The Committee will consider an outline for its report on Broadband in Scotland.

4. Scottish Executive Budget 2005-06 (in private): The Committee will consider an outline for its report on the Scottish Executive budget.

5. Work programme (in private): The Committee will consider its work programme.
