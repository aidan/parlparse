Business Bulletin No. 180/2004

Tuesday 7 December 2004

Section D : Oral questions selected for answer on 9 December 2004
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Nicola Sturgeon: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Prime Minister and what issues will be discussed. (S2F-1269)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive’s Cabinet. (S2F-1275)

3. Colin Fox: To ask the First Minister what the Scottish Executive’s current top priorities are. (S2F-1289)

4. Jackie Baillie: To ask the First Minister whether the Scottish Executive’s strategy in respect of national parks is achieving its aims. (S2F-1287)

5. Stewart Stevenson: To ask the First Minister what contribution the Fresh Talent initiative is making to Scotland. (S2F-1272)

6. Mike Pringle: To ask the First Minister what steps the Scottish Executive is taking to reduce reoffending. (S2F-1278)
