Business Bulletin No. 167/2001

Friday 16 November 2001

Section E : Written questions lodged on 15 November 2001
  

S1W-19959 Karen Whitefield: To ask the Scottish Executive how many local authorities currently ensure that community care assessments of deafblind children and adults are carried out by specialists in dual-sensory impairment.
