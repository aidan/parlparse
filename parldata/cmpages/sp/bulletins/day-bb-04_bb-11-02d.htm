Business Bulletin No. 156/2004

Tuesday 2 November 2004

Section D : Oral questions selected for answer on 4 November 2004
  

First Minister’s Question Time

1. Nicola Sturgeon: To ask the First Minister what issues will be discussed at the next meeting of the Scottish Executive’s Cabinet. (S2F-1164)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Prime Minister and what issues will be discussed. (S2F-1162)

3. Robin Harper: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he intends to discuss. (S2F-1174)

4. Dr Elaine Murray: To ask the First Minister how the proposed changes to the Scottish schools curriculum set out in Ambitious, Excellent Schools will benefit pupils and teachers. (S2F-1166)

5. Richard Lochhead: To ask the First Minister what steps are being taken to ensure that communities neighbouring nuclear power plants are not adversely affected by contaminated land. (S2F-1169)

6. George Lyon: To ask the First Minister how the Scottish Executive will exploit Scotland’s position as European Region of the Future. (S2F-1175)
