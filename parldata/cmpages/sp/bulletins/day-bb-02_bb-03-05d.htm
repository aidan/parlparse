Business Bulletin No. 37/2002

Tuesday 5 March 2002

Section D : Oral Questions
  

First Minister's Question Time

1. Mr John Swinney: To ask the First Minister when he last met the Prime Minister and what issues he raised. (S1F-1716)

2. David McLetchie: To ask the First Minister when he will next meet the Secretary of State for Scotland and what issues he plans to raise. (S1F-1710)

3. Mr Kenneth Macintosh: To ask the First Minister what message it plans to promote to children and young people on illegal drug use. (S1F-1727)

4. Christine Grahame: To ask the First Minister what steps will be taken in order to ensure that the End Year Flexibility of £200 million for 2001-02, identified in the letter from the Minister for Finance and Public Services to COSLA’s finance spokesman on 25 February 2002, is disbursed timeously. (S1F-1717)

5. David Mundell: To ask the First Minister whether foot-and-mouth disease has been eradicated. (S1F-1715)

6. Rhona Brankin: To ask the First Minister, in light of the Delivering for Scotland conference on 1 March 2002, how the Scottish Executive plans to evaluate improvements in the delivery of public services. (S1F-1732)
