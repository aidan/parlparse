Business Bulletin No. 86 / 2003

Tuesday 10th June 2003

Section A : Daily Business List
  

Meetings of Committees

All meetings take place in Committee Chambers, Edinburgh unless otherwise indicated.

Health Committee

1st Meeting, 2003

The Committee will meet at 9.15 am in Committee Room 2

1. Declaration of interests: Members of the Committee will be invited to declare any relevant interests.

2. Choice of Convener: The Committee will choose its Convener.

3. Choice of Deputy Convener: The Committee will choose its Deputy Convener.

4. Legacy paper: The Committee will consider a legacy paper from its predecessor Committee.

3. Choice of Deputy Convener: The Committee will choose a new Deputy Convener.
