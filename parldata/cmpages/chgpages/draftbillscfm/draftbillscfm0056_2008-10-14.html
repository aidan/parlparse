Draft Bills Before Parliament: 2007-08

The government publishes a number of Bills each parliamentary session in draft form, before they are introduced in Parliament as formal Bills. This enables consultation and pre-legislative scrutiny before a Bill is issued formally. Listed on this page are the Draft Bills published by the Government so far in this parliamentary session. Click on the title for the full text of the Draft Bill. Any relevant Select Committee Report will also be listed.

If you want to search for the progress of a formal Bill already before Parliament in the current session, you can use the links set out on the Bills Before Parliament page or alternatively, you can use the Bill Index database.

Other sessions of Draft Bills are listed at the bottom of this page.

Draft Apprenticeships Bill Cm 7452

  • Published by the Department for Children, Schools and Families on 16 July 2008
  • Inquiry by the Innovation, Universities, Science and Skills Committee

Draft Construction Contracts Bill

  • Published by the Department for Business, Enterprise and Regulatory Reform in July 2008.

Draft Counter-Terrorism (Temporary Provisions) Bill

  • Published by the Home Office on 13 October 2008.

Draft Cultural Property (Armed Conflicts) Bill Cm 7298

  • Published by the Department for Culture, Media and Sport in January 2008
  • Inquiry by the Culture, Media and Sport Committee
  • Report by the Culture, Media and Sport Committee, published 22 July 2008 (HC 693, Session 2007-08).

Draft Governance of Britain - Constitutional Renewal  Bill Cm 7342-II

Draft Marine Bill Cm 7351

Draft Marine Navigation Bill Cm 7370

Draft (Partial) Immigration and Citizenship Bill Cm 7373
