Private Bills before Parliament Session 2005-06

Listed below are Private Bills currently before Parliament. Click on each title to access the full text of the Bill. indicates bills in the Commons, those in the Lords and new private bills which will, if they successfully pass a preliminary stage, be introduced in January. Follow the link for further information on the new bills and how to have your views heard if you are directly and specially affected by any of them. A complete list of private bills introduced in Parliament in the current session, plus their progress through Parliament, can be found in the Weekly Information Bulletin.
