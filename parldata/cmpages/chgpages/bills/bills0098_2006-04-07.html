Listed below are public bills currently before Parliament. indicates bills in the Commons, those in the Lords and Royal Assent. Bills which start in the House of Lords have [HL] in their title. Amendments currently proposed to bills in the Lords or the Commons can be found under each bill.

A complete list of public bills introduced in Parliament in the current session, plus their progress through Parliament, can be found in the Weekly Information Bulletin. Bills which have received Royal Assent become Acts of Parliament.
