Jessica Bauly: Looking at the Bill from the CBI’s point of view, and taking a quick step back in terms of the macro-economic climate at a time when we are looking at the private sector to drive the economic recovery and to create jobs, we would say that our main question is how a localism Bill drives the economic recovery.
