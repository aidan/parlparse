Amendment No. 74 would deem either the agent or the principal to be the employer of an agency worker for the purpose of the right that we are considering. The right would apply when either or them asked a worker to attend a disciplinary or grievance hearing. The worker might have a grievance against the agency if he felt that he was being underpaid, and he might have a grievance against the principal if he felt that he was being racially abused at the temporary workplace.

Amendment No. 75 would deem a person who contracted work from a home worker to be that worker's employer. It is a sensible clarification that removes any room for doubt and unnecessary legal dispute.

I hope that the Committee will accept the amendments.
