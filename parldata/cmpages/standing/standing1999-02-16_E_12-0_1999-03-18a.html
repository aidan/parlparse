The time of an act or failure to act is set out in subparagraphs 3(3) and 3(4) of schedule 2. Hon. Members will note that in a series of acts, the last act is to be taken as the time reference. For example, an employer may tell his employee in January that he will not receive the pay rise that is normally due in June.
