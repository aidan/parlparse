My hon. Friend the Member for Chatham and Aylesford quoted from the response to the Utting report. We made it clear that we did not accept the report's recommendation that we should introduce a system of direct regulation of private foster care arrangements, and we tried to spell out in the report why we felt that that was not appropriate. I am sure that he and I would agree that that does not happen-it may be partly a matter of resources for local authorities. I accept that they have other priorities that they need to address. Friend the Member for Chatham and Aylesford was right to say that, too often, local authorities are not notified by private foster carers that they are about to receive a child as part of a private foster care arrangement. We have repeatedly expressed our concerns to local authorities about the operation of the law in this area.
