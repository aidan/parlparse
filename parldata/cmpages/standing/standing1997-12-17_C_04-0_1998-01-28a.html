Under the Destructive Imported Animals Act 1932, the Mink Keeping Order regulates mink farms, which have to purchase a licence—unnecessarily, in my opinion—and are now monitored by the Government. It is said sometimes that the presence of mink in the countryside, which is not their natural terrain, can be blamed on the activities of people fighting for animal rights.
