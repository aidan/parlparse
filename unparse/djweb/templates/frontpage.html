What is this?

undemocracy.com is a volunteer project created to give access to important official UN documents that feature in the news. It is not affiliated to the United Nations, its website, or any political organization or financial interest.

This website gives access to the transcripts (since 1994) of two of the five principle organs of the United Nations: the Security Council and the General Assembly.

Tips for using this site

There is a lof of information represented here, and it can be confusing. It helps if you know what you are looking for! If you know the document code, you can enter it directly into the search box at the top of the page.

To get an idea of the sets of documents available here, take a look at President Bush of the United States, or President A of Iran, as well as all the speeches that refer to Resolution 242.
