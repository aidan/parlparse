Thirteen years of official United Nations meetings of the General Assembly and the Security Council including many supporting documents are available on this site in glorious pastel shades. You will be able to find every publically reported members' vote and resolution (both passed and vetoed) in this time period.

This work is for use as a tool to facilitate browsing and research and to help create citations for articles in places such as wikipedia.

This project has been created by the people behind Public whip using all the latest screen scraping and PDF text parsing technology. Email team@publicwhip.org.uk for details or look for random parts of the story on the Freesteel blog.
