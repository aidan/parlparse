http://www.opsi.gov.uk/acts/acts1988/Ukpga_19880055_en_1.htm 1988 55 An Act to apply certain sums out of the Consolidated Fund to the service of the years ending on 31st March 1989 and 1990. 20th December 1988 be it enacted by the Queen's most Excellent Majesty, by and with the advice and consent of the Lords Spiritual and Temporal, and Commons, in this present Parliament assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows 10 545588 1


        1.    The Treasury may issue

Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending 31st March 1989. out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply towards making good the supply granted to Her Majesty for the service of the year ending on 31st March 1989 the sum of £1,042,249,000.

        2.    The Treasury may issue out

Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending 31st March 1990. of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply towards making good the supply granted to Her Majesty for the service of the year ending on 31st March 1990 the sum of £50,987,976,000.

        3.    This Act may be cited as the Consolidated Fund (No. 2) Act 1988.
