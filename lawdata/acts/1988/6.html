http://www.opsi.gov.uk/acts/acts1988/Ukpga_19880006_en_1.htm 1988 6 An Act to apply certain sums out of the Consolidated Fund to the service of the years ending on 31st March 1987 and 1988. 15th March 1988 be it enacted by the Queen's most Excellent Majesty, by and with the advice and consent of the Lords Spiritual and Temporal, and Commons, in this present Parliament assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows 10 540688 0

Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending 31st March 1987.

        1.    The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply towards making good the supply granted to Her Majesty for the service of the year ending on 31st March 1987 the sum of £48,461,35369.

Issue out of the Consolidated Fund for the year ending 31st March 1988.

        2.    The Treasury may issue out of the Consolidated Fund of the United Kingdom and apply towards making good the supply granted to Her Majesty for the service of the year ending on 31st March 1988 the sum of £1,474,177,000.

Short title.

        3.    This Act may be cited as the Consolidated Fund Act 1988.
