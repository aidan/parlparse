STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

1999 No. 1280 (C. 37)

CRIMINAL LAW, ENGLAND AND WALES

The Criminal Justice Act 1991 (Commencement No. 3) (Amendment) Order 1999

  Made 4th May 1999 
  Coming into force 1st June 1999 

In exercise of the powers conferred upon him by section 102(2) of the Criminal Justice Act 1991[1], the Secretary of State hereby makes the following Order:

     1. This Order may be cited as the Criminal Justice Act 1991 (Commencement No. 3) (Amendment) Order 1999 and shall come into force on 1st June 1999.

     2. Article 2(5) of, and Schedule 3 to, the Criminal Justice Act 1991 (Commencement No. 3) Order 1992[2] are hereby revoked.

     3. The provisions of the Criminal Justice Act 1991 referred to in the left-hand column of the Schedule to this Order (which relate to the matters described in the right-hand column of that Schedule) shall come into force on 1st June 1999.


Jack Straw
One of Her Majesty's Principal Secretaries of State

Home Office
4th May 1999

EXPLANATORY NOTE

(This note is not part of the Order)


This Order amends the Criminal Justice Act 1991 (Commencement No. 3) Order 1992 (S.I. 1992/333) to bring into force on 1st June 1999 minor supplementary provisions relating to the committal for sentence of children and young persons to local authority secure accommodation.
