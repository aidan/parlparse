STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

1998 No. 3238

ROAD TRAFFIC

The Road Traffic (Special Parking Area) (London Borough of Redbridge) (Amendment) Order 1998

  Made 22nd December 1998 
  Laid before Parliament 8th January 1999 
  Coming into force 1st February 1999 

Whereas the council of the London Borough of Redbridge has applied to the Secretary of State for the Environment, Transport and the Regions for an order to be made under section 76 of the Road Traffic Act 1991[1] and the Secretary of State has consulted the Commissioner of Police of the Metropolis in accordance with section 76(2) of that Act:

     NOW, the Secretary of State for the Environment, Transport and the Regions, in exercise of the powers conferred by section 76(1) and section 77(6) of Road Traffic Act 1991 and of all other powers enabling him in that behalf, hereby makes the following Order:-

     1. This Order may be cited as the Road Traffic (Special Parking Area) (London Borough of Redbridge) (Amendment) Order 1998 and shall come into force on 1st February 1999.

     2.  - (1) The Schedule to the Road Traffic (Special Parking Area) (London Borough of Redbridge) Order 1994[2] shall be amended as follows.

    (2) After paragraph 78 there shall be inserted-

         " 78A. Unnamed service road (off Woodford Avenue-north east side) between its junction with (A1400) Woodford Avenue and a point adjacent to the north western wall of No. 42a Woodford Avenue.".



Signed by authority of the Secretary of State


Glenda Jackson
Parliamentary Under Secretary of State, Department of the Environment, Transport and the Regions

22nd December 1998


EXPLANATORY NOTE

(This note is not part of the Order)


This Order amends the Schedule to the Road Traffic (Special Parking Area) (London Borough of Redbridge) Order 1994. That Order designated the whole of the London Borough of Redbridge, excluding those roads referred to in the Schedule, as a special parking area for the purposes of Part II of the Road Traffic Act 1991. This Order adds a new item 78A to that Schedule and has the effect of excluding the road described in that item from the special parking area.

Notes:

 [1] 1991 c. 40. Section 76(3) was amended by the Road Traffic Act 1991 (Amendment of Section 76(3)) Order 1995, S.I. 1995/1437.back

[2] S.I. 1994/1509; relevant amending instrument is 1996/3059.back
