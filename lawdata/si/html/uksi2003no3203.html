      STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

      2003 No. 3203

      BROADCASTING

      The Broadcasting (Jersey) Order 2003

        Made 10th December 2003 
        Coming into force 29th December 2003 

      At the Court at Buckingham Palace, the 10th day of December 2003

      Present,

      The Queen's Most Excellent Majesty in Council

      Her Majesty, in pursuance of section 150(4) of the Broadcasting Act 1996[1], and section 204(6) of the Broadcasting Act 1990[2], as it is applied by section 150(3) of the Broadcasting Act 1996, is pleased, by and with the advice of Her Privy Council, to order, and it is hereby ordered, as follows:

      Citation and commencement
           1. This Order may be cited as the Broadcasting (Jersey) Order 2003 and shall come into force on 29th December 2003 and shall be deemed to have come into force immediately after the Wireless Telegraphy (Jersey) Order 2003[3].

      Extension of the Broadcasting Act 1996
           2. The following provisions of the Broadcasting Act 1996 shall extend, with the modifications specified in Schedule 1 to this Order, to the Bailiwick of Jersey - 

        (a) in Part I (digital terrestrial television broadcasting), sections 1 to 20, 22 to 28, 30, 31, 33 to 39;

        (b) in Part II (digital terrestrial sound broadcasting), sections 40 to 72;

        (c) in Part III (amendments of Broadcasting Act 1990), sections 73 to 76, 78, 79, 82 to 89, 92, 93, and 96;

        (d) Part IV (sporting and other events of national interest);

        (e) in Part V (Broadcasting Standards Commission), sections 106 to 126 and 128 to 130;

        (f) in Part VIII (miscellaneous and general), sections 142 to 148; and

        (g) Schedules 1 to 4, 8 and 10 and Part I of Schedule 11.

      Amendment of the Broadcasting Act 1990 (Jersey) (No. 2) Order 1991
                 3. The Schedule to the Broadcasting Act 1990 (Jersey) (No. 2) Order 1991[4] is hereby amended in accordance with Schedule 2 to this Order.

      Revocations
           4. In the Broadcasting Act 1996 (British Broadcasting Corporation - Transmission Network) (Jersey) Order 1997[5], the following provisions are hereby revoked - 

        (a) in article 2, the words "147(1)"; and

        (b) paragraph 1 of the Schedule.


      A. K. Galloway
      Clerk of the Privy Council

      EXPLANATORY NOTE

      (This note is not part of the Order)


      This Order extends to the Bailiwick of Jersey provisions of the Broadcasting Act 1996 (the 1996 Act) relating to digital terrestrial broadcasting, sporting and other events of national interest, the Broadcasting Standards Commission, and also certain miscellaneous and general provisions. Schedule 1 sets out modifications of provisions of the 1996 Act, and Schedule 2, amending the Broadcasting Act 1990 (Jersey) (No. 2) Order 1991 (S.I. 1991/1710), sets out modifications of the amendments of the Broadcasting Act 1990 made by the 1996 Act.

      The Order also makes consequential amendments of the Broadcasting Act 1996 (British Broadcasting Corporation - Transmission Network) (Jersey) Order 1997 (S.I. 1997/1757).
