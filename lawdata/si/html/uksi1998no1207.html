STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

1998 No. 1207

HIGHWAYS, ENGLAND AND WALES

The A65 Trunk Road (Denton Bridge to Black Bull Farm) (Detrunking) Order 1993 (Revocation) Order 1998

  Made 29th April 1998 
  Coming into force 27th May 1998 

The Secretary of State for the Environment, Transport and the Regions makes this Order in exercise of powers conferred by sections 10 and 12 of the Highways Act 1980[1] and now vested in him[2], and of all other powers enabling him in that behalf: - 

     1. The A65 Trunk Road (Denton Bridge to Black Bull Farm) (Detrunking) Order 1993[3] is hereby revoked.

     2. This Order may be cited as the A65 Trunk Road (Denton Bridge to Black Bull Farm) (Detrunking) Order 1993 (Revocation) Order 1998 and shall come into force on 27th May 1998.



Signed by authority of the Secretary of State for the Environment, Transport and the Regions


Derek Oddy
Divisional Director, Highways Agency

29th April 1998
