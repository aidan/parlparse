STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

1998 No. 3217

EDUCATION, ENGLAND AND WALES

The School Standards and Framework Act 1998(Modification) (No. 2) Regulations 1998

  Made 21st December 1998 
  Laid before Parliament 22nd December 1998 
  Coming into force 12th January 1999 

In exercise of the powers conferred on the Secretary of State by section 144(1) and (3) of the School Standards and Framework Act 1998[1], the Secretary of State for Education and Employment hereby makes the following Regulations:

Citation and commencement
     1. These Regulations may be cited as the School Standards and Framework Act 1998 (Modification) (No. 2) Regulations 1998 and shall come into force on 12th January 1999.

Education development plans and intervention by the Secretary of State
     2. In relation to the period beginning on 1st April 1999 and ending immediately before 1st September 1999 - 

    (a) section 6(2)(a)(i) and (3)(b) of the School Standards and Framework Act 1998 (education development plans), and

    (b) sections 497A(1)(b) and 497B(5) of the Education Act 1996 (intervention by the Secretary of State)[    2],

shall have effect as if the references (however framed) to schools maintained by the authority included grant-maintained schools and grant-maintained special schools maintained by that authority pursuant to regulation 2 of the School Standards and Framework Act 1998 (Modification) Regulations 1998[3].


Tessa Blackstone
Minister of State, Department for Education and Employment

21st December 1998


EXPLANATORY NOTE

(This note is not part of the Regulations)


These Regulations modify section 6 of the School Standards and Framework Act 1998 (education development plans) and sections 497A and 497B of the Education Act 1996 (intervention by the Secretary of State) by providing that, in the period beginning on 1st April 1999 and ending immediately before 1st September 1999 the references (however framed) to schools maintained by a local education authority include grant-maintained schools and grant-maintained special schools maintained by that authority.

Notes:

 [1] 1998 c. 31.back

[2] 1996 c. 56. Sections 497A and 497B were inserted by the School Standards and Framework Act 1998, section 8.back
