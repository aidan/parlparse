STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

1999 No. 2269

EDUCATION, ENGLAND

The Further Education Funding Council for England (Supplementary Functions) Order 1999

  Made 6th August 1999 
  Laid before Parliament 11th August 1999 
  Coming into force 1st September 1999 

In exercise of the powers conferred on the Secretary of State by section 8(4) of the Further and Higher Education Act 1992[1], the Secretary of State for Education and Employment hereby makes the following Order:

Citation, commencement and interpretation
     1.  - (1) This Order may be cited as the Further Education Funding Council for England (Supplementary Functions) Order 1999 and shall come into force on 1st September 1999.

    (2) In this Order "the Dance and Drama Regulations" means the Education (Grants) (Dance and Drama) (England) Regulations 1999[2].

Supplementary Functions of the Further Education Funding Council for England
     2. The following supplementary functions are hereby conferred on the Further Education Funding Council for England - 

    (a) the function of advising the Secretary of State as to the quality of further education provided by institutions to which grants are paid under the Dance and Drama Regulations;

    (b) the function of advising the Secretary of State on whether requirements imposed on institutions which provide further education to which grants are paid under the Dance and Drama Regulations have been complied with; and

    (c) the function of arranging for payment of grant under the Dance and Drama Regulations to institutions which provide further education.


Tessa Blackstone
Minister of State Department for Education and Employment

6th August 1999


EXPLANATORY NOTE

(This note is not part of the Order)


This Order confers additional functions on the Further Education Funding Council for England. The functions conferred by this Order are functions exercisable in connection with Education (Grants) (Dance and Drama) (England) Regulations 1999.
