STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

2001 No. 3219

HOUSING, ENGLAND

The Housing (Right to Buy) (Priority of Charges) (England) (No. 2) Order 2001

  Made 24th September 2001 
  Coming into force 15th October 2001 

The Secretary of State for the Environment, Transport and the Regions, in exercise of the powers conferred on him by section 156(4) of the Housing Act 1985[1], hereby makes the following Order - 

Citation, commencement and extent
     1.  - (1) This Order may be cited as the Housing (Right to Buy) (Priority of Charges) (England) (No. 2) Order 2001 and shall come into force on 15th October 2001.

    (2) This Order extends to England only.

Specified Bodies
     2. The following bodies are hereby specified as approved lending institutions for the purposes of section 156[2] of the Housing Act 1985 (priority of charges) - 

    (a) igroup Mortgages Limited - Company No. 3770776.

    (b) igroup UK Loans Limited - Company No. 3749420.

    (c) igroup 2 Limited - Company No. 3610605.

    (d) igroup 3 Limited - Company No. 3730890.

    (e) igroup 4 Limited - Company No. 3797432.

    (f) igroup 5 Limited - Company No. 3770763.



Signed by authority of the Secretary of State for Transport, Local Government and the Regions.


Sally Keeble
Parliamentary Under-Secretary of State, Department for Transport, Local Government and the Regions

24th September 2001


EXPLANATORY NOTE

(This note is not part of the Order)


This Order specifies six bodies as approved lending institutions for the purposes of section 156 of the Housing Act 1985 (priority of charges on disposals under the right to buy). Other bodies have been specified by previous orders. In being specified for the purposes of that section, the bodies thereby also become approved lending institutions for the purposes of section 36 of the 1985 Act (priority of charges on voluntary disposals by local authorities) and section 12 of the Housing Act 1996 (priority of charges on voluntary disposals by registered social landords).

As section 156 of the Housing Act 1985 is applied by section 171A of that Act for cases in which a tenant's right to buy is preserved and by section 17 of the Housing Act 1996 for cases in which a tenant has the right to acquire under section 16 of the 1996 Act, the specified bodies are also approved lending institutions for the purposes of those rights.
