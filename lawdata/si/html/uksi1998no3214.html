STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

1998 No. 3214

ROAD TRAFFIC

The A41 Trunk Road (Camden) Red Route Traffic Order 1998 Variation Order 1998

  Made 18th December 1998 
  Coming into force 11th January 1999 

The Traffic Director for London, in accordance with a direction given to him by the Secretary of State under section 58(1) of the Road Traffic Act 1991[1] and in exercise of the powers conferred on the Secretary of State by section 6 of the Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984[2], and of all other enabling powers, hereby makes the following Order: - 

Commencement and Citation
     1. This Order may be cited as The A41 Trunk Road (Camden) Red Route Traffic Order 1998 Variation Order 1998, and shall come into force on 11th January 1999.

Variations
     2. The A41 Trunk Road (Camden) Red Route Traffic Order 1998[3] is varied as provided by the following provision of this Order.

     3. In schedule 2B, the following text is added after item 6:

         " 6A Finchley Road, south-west side, from a point 1 metre south-east of the party wall of 303 and 305 Finchley Road south-eastward for a distance of 12 metres."

     4. In schedule 3B, item 11 is substituted as follows:

    " Finchley Road, north-east side, from the party wall of 144 and 146 Finchley Road south-eastwards for a distance of 18 metres."

     5. In schedule 4, item 14 is substituted as follows:

    " Finchley Road, north-east side, between a point 26 metres north-west of the north-western kerb-line of Frognal Court and the party wall of 144 and 146 Finchley Road."

     6. In schedule 4, item 15 is substituted as follows:

    " Finchley Road, north-east side, between a point 18 metres south-east of the party wall of 144 and 146 Finchley Road and a point 2 metres north-west of the north-western boundary of No. 134 Finchley Road."

     7. In schedule 4, item 33 is substituted as follows:

    " Finchley Road, south-west side, from the party wall of 307 and 309 Finchley Road to a point 1 metre south-east of the party wall of 303 and 305 Finchley Road."

     8. In schedule 4 the following text is added after item 33:

         " 33A Finchley Road, south-west side, from a point 13 metres south-east of the party wall of 303 and 305 Finchley Road to a point 16 metres north-west of the party wall of 283 and 285 Finchley Road."



Signed in accordance with a direction from the Secretary of State.


Derek Turner
Traffic Director for London

18th December 1998
