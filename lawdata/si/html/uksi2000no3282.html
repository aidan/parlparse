STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

2000 No. 3282

VETERINARY SURGEONS

The Veterinary Surgeons and Veterinary Practitioners (Registration) (Amendment) RegulationsOrder of Council 2000

  Made 12th December 2000 
  Coming into force 1st April 2001 

At the Council Chamber, Whitehall, the 12th day of December 2000

By the Lords of Her Majesty's Most Honourable Privy Council

Whereas in pursuance of section 11 of the Veterinary Surgeons Act 1966[1] the Council of the Royal College of Veterinary Surgeons has made the Veterinary Surgeons and Veterinary Practitioners Registration (Amendment) Regulations 2000 as set out in the Schedule to this Order.

     And whereas by section 25(1) of the said Act such Regulations shall not have effect unless approved by the Privy Council:

     Now, therefore, Their Lordships, having taken the said Regulations into consideration, are hereby pleased, in exercise of the power conferred by section 25(1) of the said Act, to order as follows: - 

     1. This Order may be cited as the Veterinary Surgeons and Veterinary Practitioners (Registration) (Amendment) Regulations Order of Council 2000, and shall come into force on 1st April 2001.

     2. The Regulations set out in the Schedule hereto are approved.


A. K. Galloway
Clerk of the Privy Council

EXPLANATORY NOTE

(This note is not part of the Order)


The Regulations approved by this Order amend the Veterinary Surgeons and Veterinary Practitioners Registration Regulations 2000 by increasing, with effect from 1st April 2001, the fees payable for the registration and annual retention of names on the Registers of the Royal College of Veterinary Surgeons. The increases in the existing fees range from under 7 per cent. to a little over 29 per cent.
