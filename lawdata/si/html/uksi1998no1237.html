STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

1998 No. 1237

LOCAL GOVERNMENT, ENGLAND AND WALES

The Local Government Act 1988 (Defined Activities) (Exemptions) (Northumberland County Council and Suffolk Coastal District Council) Order 1998

  Made 15th May 1998 
  Laid before Parliament 22nd May 1998 
  Coming into force 12th June 1998 

The Secretary of State, in exercise of the powers conferred on him by sections 2(9) and 15(5) of the Local Government Act 1988[1], and of all other powers enabling him in that behalf, hereby makes the following Order:

Citation and commencement
     1. This Order may be cited as the Local Government Act 1988 (Defined Activities) (Exemptions) (Northumberland County Council and Suffolk Coastal District Council) Order 1998 and shall come into force on 12th June 1998.

Exemption of other cleaning by Northumberland County Council
     2. Other cleaning[2] by the council of the county of Northumberland shall not be treated as a defined activity so long as it is carried out in their area during the period beginning with 1st August 1998 and ending on 31st March 1999.

Exemption of sports and leisure facilities management by Suffolk Coastal District Council
     3. The management of sports and leisure facilities[3] by the Suffolk Coastal District Council shall not be treated as a defined activity so long as it is carried out at The Brackenbury Sports Centre, High Road East, Felixstowe, Suffolk, during the period beginning with 1st January 1999 and ending on 31st December 2000.



Signed by authority of the Secretary of State,


Hilary Armstrong
Minister of State Department of the Environment, Transport and the Regions

15th May 1998


EXPLANATORY NOTE

(This note is not part of the Order)


Under Part I of the Local Government Act 1988 (competition), work falling within certain defined activities may be carried out by local authorities only if particular conditions are fulfilled.

Article 2 of this Order exempts from the requirements of Part I the other cleaning work of Northumberland County Council so long as it is carried out in their area during the period beginning with 1st August 1998 and ending on 31st March 1999. Article 3 exempts the sports and leisure facilities management work of Suffolk Coastal District Council so long as that work is carried out at The Brackenbury Sports Centre, High Road East, Felixstowe, Suffolk during the period beginning with 1st January 1999 and ending on 31st December 2000.
