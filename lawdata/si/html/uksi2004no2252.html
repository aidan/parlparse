      STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

      2004 No. 2252

      PUBLIC PASSENGER TRANSPORT

      The Community Bus (Amendment) Regulations 2004

        Made 31st August 2004 
        Laid before Parliament 9th September 2004 
        Coming into force 1st October 2004 

      The Secretary of State for Transport, in exercise of the powers conferred upon him by sections 52(1) and 60(1)(e) of the Public Passenger Vehicles Act 1981[1], and after consultation with representative organisations in accordance with section 61(2) of the Act, hereby makes the following Regulations:

      Citation and commencement
           1. These Regulations may be cited as the Community Bus (Amendment) Regulations 2004 and shall come into force on 1st October 2004.

      Amendments
                 2.  - (1) The Community Bus Regulations 1986[2] are amended as follows.

          (2) In regulation 4(1), substitute "£44" for "£39".



      Signed by authority of the Secretary of State for Transport


      Tony McNulty
      Parliamentary Under Secretary of State, Department for Transport

      31st August 2004


      EXPLANATORY NOTE

      (This note is not part of the Regulations)


      These Regulations increase the fee for a community bus permit granted under section 22 of the Transport Act 1985 from £39 to £44.

      A full regulatory impact assessment of the effect that this instrument will have on the costs of business is available from the Licensing, Roadworthiness and Insurance Division of the Department for Transport, Zone 5/20, Southside, 105 Victoria Street, London SW1P 4DR.
