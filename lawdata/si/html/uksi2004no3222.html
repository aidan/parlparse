      STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

      2004 No. 3222

      ROAD TRAFFIC

      The Goods Vehicle Operators (Qualifications) (Amendment) Regulations 2004

        Made 7th December 2004 
        Laid before Parliament 9th December 2004 
        Coming into force 1st January 2005 

      The Secretary of State for Transport, being a Minister designated[1] for the purposes of section 2(2) of the European Communities Act 1972[2] in relation to the regulation and supervision of the qualifications of persons engaged in road transport, in exercise of the powers conferred upon him by that section hereby makes the following Regulations:

           1.  - (1) These Regulations may be cited as the Goods Vehicle Operators (Qualifications) (Amendment) Regulations 2004.

          (2) These Regulations shall come into force on 1st January 2005.

          (3) These Regulations do not extend to Northern Ireland.

                 2.  - (1) The Goods Vehicles (Licensing of Operators) Act 1995[3] shall be amended as follows.

          (2) In paragraph 6(2) of Schedule 3 the words "authorising the use of vehicles for international transport operations" shall be omitted.



      Signed by authority of the Secretary of State for Transport


      David Jamieson
      Parliamentary Under Secretary of State Department for Transport

      7th December 2004


      EXPLANATORY NOTE

      (This note is not part of the Regulations)


      These Regulations amend paragraph 6 of Schedule 3 to the Goods Vehicles (Licensing of Operators) Act 1995. Paragraph 6, as currently worded, prescribes the requirements which an applicant for, or holder of, a standard operator's licence must meet in order to be of appropriate financial standing but does not apply to national operations.

      The amendment applies the prescribed minimum amounts for capital and reserves to all standard licences, national as well as international.

      The amendment is made in implementation of Council Directive 98/76/EC of 1st October 1998 (O.J. L277 14th October 1998, page 17), which amended Council Directive 96/26/EC of 29th April 1996 on the admission to the occupation of road haulage operator and road passenger transport operator in national and international transport operations (O.J. L124 23rd May 1996, page 1).

      A Regulatory Impact Assessment of the affect of this instrument, and of the Public Service Vehicle Operators (Qualifications) (Amendment) Regulations 2004, on the costs of business has been prepared and is available from the Department for Transport, Logistics Policy Division, Great Minster House, 76 Marsham Street, London SW1P 4DR.
