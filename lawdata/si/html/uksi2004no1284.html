      STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

      2004 No. 1284

      MERCHANT SHIPPING

      The Merchant Shipping (Confirmation of Legislation and Repeals) (Jersey) Order 2004

        Made 6th May 2004 
        Coming into force 1st June 2004 

      At the Court at Buckingham Palace, the 6th day of May 2004

      Present,

      The Queen's Most Excellent Majesty in Council

      Her Majesty, in pursuance of section 735(1) of the Merchant Shipping Act 1894[1], and in exercise of the powers conferred upon her by section 315(2) of the Merchant Shipping Act 1995[2], is pleased, by and with the advice of Her Privy Council, to order, and it is hereby ordered, as follows: - 

           1. This Order may be cited as the Merchant Shipping (Confirmation of Legislation and Repeals) (Jersey) Order 2004, and shall come into force on 1st June 2004.

                 2. The Shipping (Jersey) Law 2002 adopted by the States of Jersey on 6th December 2001 is hereby confirmed.

                 3. The repeal by section 314(1) of and Schedule 12 to the Merchant Shipping Act 1995 of the Merchant Shipping (Registration, etc) Act 1993[3] is hereby extended to the Bailiwick of Jersey.

           4. The Orders listed in the Schedule to this Order are revoked insofar only as they apply to the Bailiwick of Jersey.


      A. K. Galloway
      Clerk of the Privy Council

      EXPLANATORY NOTE

      (This note is not part of the Order)


      This Order confirms the Shipping (Jersey) Law 2002, a copy of which can be viewed on the Jersey Legal Information website: http://www.jerseylegalinfo.je, adopted by the States of Jersey. The Jersey Law repeals provisions of the Merchant Shipping Act 1894 which continued to apply to Jersey despite the repeal of the 1894 Act by the Merchant Shipping Act 1995. The Order also provides for the repeal, as regards Jersey, of the Merchant Shipping (Registration, etc) Act 1993. The Schedule lists certain Orders in Council consequentially revoked in their application to Jersey.
