STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

2002 No. 3267 (C. 110)

HIGHWAYS, ENGLAND

The New Roads and Street Works Act 1991 (Commencement No. 7) (England) Order 2002

  Made 20th December 2002 

The Secretary of State for Transport, in exercise powers conferred by section 170 of the New Roads and Street Works Act 1991[1], hereby makes the following Order:

Citation and application
     1.  - (1) This Order may be cited as the New Roads and Street Works Act 1991 (Commencement No. 7) (England) Order 2002.

    (2) This Order applies as respects England only.

Provision coming into force on 1st May 2003
     2. With the exception of subsection(1)(c), section 79 of the New Roads and Street Works Act 1991 (records of location of apparatus) shall come into force, as respects England, on 1st May 2003.



Signed by authority the Secretary of State for Transport


John Spellar
Minister of State Department for Transport

20th December 2002


EXPLANATORY NOTE

(This note is not part of the Order)


This Order brings into force on 1st May 2003 in England the provisions of section 79 of the New Roads and Street Works Act 1991, except subsection (1)(c). Section 79 of the Act imposes a duty on undertakers to keep records of the location of apparatus in streets in certain circumstances. Subsection (1)(c) imposes the duty when the undertaker is informed of the apparatus' location under section 80 of the Act, which is not yet in force.
