STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

1998 No. 1210

SEA FISHERIES

The Sussex Sea Fisheries District (Constitution of Committee and Expenses) (Variation) Order 1998

  Made 6th May 1998 
  Coming into force in accordance with article 1(1)

The Minister of Agriculture, Fisheries and Food, on the application of the local fisheries committee for the Sussex Sea Fisheries District and after consultation with every council concerned, in exercise of the powers conferred on him by section 1 of the Sea Fisheries Regulation Act 1966[1] and of all other powers enabling him in that behalf, hereby makes the following Order, a draft of which has lain before Parliament for forty days prior to its being made:

Title, commencement and interpretation
     1.  - (1) This Order may be cited as the Sussex Sea Fisheries District (Constitution of Committee and Expenses) (Variation) Order 1998 and shall come into force on the day after the day on which it is made.

    (2) In this Order "the principal order" means the order made by the Board of Trade on 15 February 1893[2] (which created the Sussex Sea Fisheries District and constituted a local fisheries committee for the regulation of the sea fisheries within that district).

Variation of the principal order
     2. For the schedule to the principal order there shall be substituted the schedule set out in the Schedule to this Order.


Elliot Morley
Parliamentary Secretary, Ministry of Agriculture, Fisheries and Food

6th May 1998

EXPLANATORY NOTE

(This note is not part of the Order)


This Order varies the constitution of the local fisheries committee for the Sussex Sea Fisheries District (originally constituted in 1893 by order of the Board of Trade under the Sea Fisheries Regulation Act 1888 (c.54)) to take account of the changes made to the structure of local government in England under the Local Government Act 1992 (c.19) and, in particular, the East Sussex (Boroughs of Brighton and Hove) (Structural Change) Order 1995 (S.I. 1995/1770). Since the number and size of the constituent councils has altered, the number of representatives from each council and their proportionate contributions to the expenses of the committee have been revised (article 2 and Schedule).
