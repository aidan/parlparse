      STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

      2004 No. 3235 (C.143)

      CHRISTMAS DAY TRADING

      The Christmas Day (Trading) Act 2004 (Commencement) Order 2004

        Made 8th December 2004 

      The Secretary of State, in exercise of the powers conferred upon her by section 6(3) of the Christmas Day (Trading) Act 2004[1] hereby makes the following Order:

           1. This Order may be cited as the Christmas Day (Trading) Act 2004 (Commencement) Order 2004.

                 2. 9th December 2004 is the appointed day for the coming into force of the Christmas Day (Trading) Act 2004.


      Gerry Sutcliffe,
      Parliamentary Under Secretary of State for Employment Relations, Postal Services and Consumers Department of Trade and Industry

      8th December 2004


      EXPLANATORY NOTE

      (This note is not part of the Order)


      This Order brings into force on 9th December 2004 the Christmas Day (Trading) Act 2004.
