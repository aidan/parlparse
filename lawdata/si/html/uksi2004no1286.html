      STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

      2004 No.1286

      EDUCATION, ENGLAND

      The Education (Inspectors of Schools in England) (No. 2) Order 2004

        Made 6th May 2004 
        Coming into force 7th May 2004 

      At the Court at Buckingham Palace, the 6th day of May 2004

      Present,

      The Queen's Most Excellent Majesty in Council

      Her Majesty, in pursuance of section 1(2) of the School Inspections Act 1996[1], is pleased, by and with the advice of Her Privy Council, to order, and it is hereby ordered, as follows:

           1. This Order may be cited as the Education (Inspectors of Schools in England) (No.2) Order 2004 and shall come into force on 7th May 2004.

                 2. The persons named in the Schedule to this Order are hereby appointed Her Majesty's Inspectors of Schools in England with effect from 7th May 2004.


      A. K. Galloway
      Clerk of the Privy Council


      SCHEDULE
      Article 2


      Persons Appointed


      Anita Wade

      Lisa Williams


      EXPLANATORY NOTE

      (This note is not part of the Order)


      This Order appoints the persons named in the Schedule as Her Majesty's Inspectors of Schools in England.
