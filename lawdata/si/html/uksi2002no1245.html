STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

2002 No. 1245 (C. 33)

SOCIAL CARE, ENGLAND

The Care Standards Act 2000 (Commencement No. 12) (England) Order 2002

  Made 24th March 2002 

The Secretary of State for Health, in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 118(4) to (6) and 122 of the Care Standards Act 2000[1] and of all other powers enabling him in that behalf, hereby makes the following Order:

Citation, interpretation and extent
     1.  - (1) This Order may be cited as the Care Standards Act 2000 (Commencement No. 12) (England) Order 2002.

    (2) In this Order, references to sections and Schedules are references to sections of, and Schedules to, the Care Standards Act 2000.

    (3) This Order extends to England only.

Appointed days
     2.  - (1) 25th March 2002 is the day appointed for the coming into force of - 

    (a) section 54(2) (General Social Care Council);

    (b) the following provisions in so far as they relate to the General Social Care Council and are not already in force - 

      (i) section 62 (codes of practice);

      (ii) section 63 (approval of courses);

      (iii) section 66 (visitors for certain social work courses);

      (iv) section 71 (rules);

    (c) section 67(1) to (4) in so far as it confers functions on the Secretary of State, section 67(5) and section 67(7) in so far as it relates to section 67(5) and to the Secretary of State;

    (d) section 55 (interpretation), in so far as it relates to any provision mentioned in sub-paragraphs (a) to (c) above;

    (e) section 113(1), (3) and (4) (default powers of appropriate Minister) in so far as it confers powers on the Secretary of State in relation to the functions of the General Social Care Council.

    (2) 1st April 2002 is the day appointed for the coming into force of section 70(1) (abolition of Central Council for Education and Training in Social Work).



Signed by authority of the Secretary of State for Health


Jacqui Smith
Minister of State, Department of Health

24th March 2002


EXPLANATORY NOTE

(This note is not part of the Order)


This order provides for the coming into force on 25th March 2002 of the following provisions of the Care Standards Act 2000 (C. 14) - 

    section 54(2);

    sections 62, 63, 66 and 71, in so far as they relate to the General Social Care Council and are not already in force;

    section 67 (in part) in so far as it relates to functions conferred on the Secretary of State;

    section 55 (interpretation) in so far as relevant to the above provisions;

    section 113(1), (3) and (4) in so far as it relates to the default powers of the Secretary of State over the General Social Care Council.

The Order also provides for the coming into force on 1st April 2002 of Section 70(1).

The provisions are commenced in England only.
