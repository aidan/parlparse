STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

2002 No. 1253

ANIMALS, ENGLAND

ANIMAL HEALTH

The TSE (England) (Amendment) Regulations 2002

  Made 3rd May 2002 
  Laid before Parliament 3rd May 2002 
  Coming into force 27th May 2002 

The Secretary of State for Environment, Food and Rural Affairs, being designated[1] for the purposes of section 2(2) of the European Communities Act 1972[2] in relation to the common agricultural policy of the European Community, in exercise of the powers conferred on her by that section, and of all other powers enabling her in that behalf, makes the following Regulations - 

Title, extent and commencement
     1. These Regulations may be cited as the TSE (England) (Amendment) Regulations 2002, shall extend to England and shall come into force on 27th May 2002.

Amendment of the TSE (England) Regulations 2002
     2.  - (1) The TSE (England) Regulations 2002[3] shall be amended in accordance with the following provisions of this regulation.

    (2) In regulation 3(1), for the definition of "cutting premises" there shall be substituted the following definition - 

      " "cutting premises" means premises used for the purpose of cutting up fresh meat intended for sale for human consumption;".

    (3) In regulation 9(2), the words "Subject to paragraph (3) below," are deleted.

    (4) In regulation 71(2), sub-paragraph (p) is revoked.

    (5) In regulation 99(3), for the words "referred to in paragraph (2) above" there shall be substituted the words "of these Regulations".


Elliot Morley
Parliamentary Under Secretary of State, Department for Environment, Food and Rural Affairs

3rd May 2002
