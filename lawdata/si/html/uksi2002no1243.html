STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

2002 No. 1243

NATIONAL HEALTH SERVICE, ENGLAND

The Blackpool, Fylde and Wyre Hospitals National Health Service Trust (Establishment) and the Blackpool, Wyre and Fylde Community Health Services National Health Service Trust and the Blackpool Victoria Hospital National Health Service Trust (Dissolution) Order 2002

  Made 24th March 2002 
  Coming into force 1st April 2002 

The Secretary of State for Health, in exercise of the powers conferred upon him by section 126(3) of the National Health Service Act 1977[1] and by section 5(1) of, and paragraphs 1, 3 and 29(1) of Schedule 2 to, the National Health Service and Community Care Act 1990[2] and of all other powers enabling him in that behalf, considering it appropriate in the interests of the health service to dissolve the Blackpool, Wyre and Fylde Community Health Services National Health Service Trust and the Blackpool Victoria Hospital National Health Service Trust[3], and having completed the consultation prescribed under section 5(2) of and paragraph 29(3) of Schedule 2 to that Act[4], makes the following Order:

Citation, commencement and interpretation
     1.  - (1) This Order may be cited as the Blackpool, Fylde and Wyre Hospitals National Health Service Trust (Establishment) and the Blackpool, Wyre and Fylde Community Health Services National Health Service Trust and the Blackpool Victoria Hospital National Health Service Trust (Dissolution) Order 2002 and shall come into force on 1st April 2002.

    (2) In this Order, unless the context otherwise requires - 

    "the Act" means the National Health Service and Community Care Act 1990;

    "community health services" means any services which the Secretary of State may provide under section 3(1)(d) or (e) of, or Schedule 1 to, the National Health Service Act 1977 and any service which he has a duty to provide under section 5(1) or (1A) of that Act[5];

    "operational date" has the meaning assigned to it in paragraph 3(1)(e) of Schedule 2 to the Act;

    "the trust" means the National Health Service Trust established by article 2 of this Order.

Establishment and name of trust
     2. There is established an NHS trust which shall be called the Blackpool, Fylde and Wyre Hospitals National Health Service Trust.

Nature and functions of the trust
     3.  - (1) The trust is established for the purposes specified in section 5(1) of the Act.

    (2) The trust's functions shall be to provide goods and services, namely hospital accommodation and services, and community health services, for the purposes of the health service at or from the trust's headquarters at Blackpool Victoria Hospital, Whinney Heys Road, Blackpool, Lancashire, FY3 8NR and at or from Clifton Hospital, Devonshire Road Hospital, Fleetwood Hospital, Lytham Hospital, Rossall Hospital, South Shore Hospital, Wesham Park Hospital, Bispham Hospital and at or from any associated hospitals, establishments and facilities.

Directors of trust
     4. The trust shall have in addition to the chairman 5 executive directors and 5 non-executive directors.

Operational date and accounting date of the trust
     5.  - (1) The operational date shall be 1st April 2002.

    (2) The accounting date of the trust shall be 31st March.

Dissolution of NHS Trust
     6.  - (1) The Blackpool, Wyre and Fylde Community Health Services National Health Service Trust established by the Blackpool, Wyre and Fylde Community Health Services National Health Service Trust (Establishment) Order 1993[6] is hereby dissolved, and accordingly that Order is revoked.

    (2) The Blackpool Victoria Hospital National Health Service Trust established by the Blackpool Victoria Hospital National Health Service Trust (Establishment) Order 1993[7] is hereby dissolved, and accordingly that Order is revoked.



Signed by authority of the Secretary of State for Health


Hunt
Parliamentary Under Secretary of State, Department of Health

24th March 2002


EXPLANATORY NOTE

(This note is not part of the Order)


This Order establishes the Blackpool, Fylde and Wyre Hospitals National Health Service Trust as an NHS trust, provided for in section 5 of the National Health Service and Community Care Act 1990.

This Order also dissolves on 1st April 2002;

    the Blackpool, Wyre and Fylde Community Health Services National Health Service Trust; and

    the Blackpool Victoria Hospital National Health Service trust.

The new trust will provide certain services previously provided by the Blackpool, Wyre and Fylde Community Health Services National Health Service Trust and the Blackpool Victoria Hospital National Health Service Trust which are dissolved by this Order.

The Order provides for the functions of the trust (article 3) and the number of executive and non-executive directors (article 4). It specifies the operational date (the date on which the trust assumes all its functions), and the accounting date of the trust (article 5).

This Order does not impose a charge on businesses.
