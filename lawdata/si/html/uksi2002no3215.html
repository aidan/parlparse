STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

2002 No. 3215

ANIMALS, ENGLAND

PREVENTION OF CRUELTY

The Protection of Animals (Anaesthetics) Amendment Order 2002

  Made 22nd December 2002 
  Laid 23rd December 2002 
  Coming into force 23rd December 2002 

The Secretary of State, in exercise of the powers conferred on her by section 1 of the Protection of Animals (Anaesthetics) Act 1954[1] and now vested in her makes the following Order:

Title and commencement
     1. This Order may be cited as the Protection of Animals (Anaesthetics) Amendment Order 2002 and shall come into force on 23rd December 2002.

Revocation of the Protection of Animals (Anaesthetics) Amendment Order 2003
     2. The Protection of Animals (Anaesthetics) Amendment Order 2003[2] shall be revoked.


Alun Michael
Minister of State Department for Environment Food and Rural Affairs

Date 22nd December 2002


EXPLANATORY NOTE

(This note is not part of the Order)


This Order revokes the Protection of Animals (Anaesthetics) Amendment Order 2003 to correct a defect in that Order.
