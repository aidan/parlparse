      STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

      2004 No. 3257 (C. 146)

      INDUSTRIAL AND PROVIDENT SOCIETIES

      The Co-operatives and Community Benefit Societies Act 2003 (Commencement No. 2) Order 2004

        Made 9th December 2004 

      The Treasury, in exercise of the powers conferred upon them by section 7 of the Co-operatives and Community Benefit Societies Act 2003[1], hereby make the following Order:

      Citation
           1. This Order may be cited as the Co-operatives and Community Benefit Societies Act 2003 (Commencement No. 2) Order 2004.

      Appointed day
                 2. The day appointed for the coming into force of section 1 of the Co-operatives and Community Benefit Societies Act is 13th December 2004.


      Joan Ryan

      Nick Ainger
      Two of the Lords Commissioners of Her Majesty's Treasury

      9th December 2004


      EXPLANATORY NOTE

      (This note is not part of the Order)


      This Order brings into force on 13th December 2004 the remaining provision of the Co-operatives and Community Benefit Societies Act 2003 (namely section 1) which was not already in force. Sections 7-9 were brought into force on Royal Assent, on 10th July 2003. The Co-operatives and Community Benefit Societies Act 2003 (Commencement No. 1) Order 2003 (S.I. 2003/2678) brought sections 2 and 3 into force on 1st April 2004 and sections 4-6 on 20th October 2003.
