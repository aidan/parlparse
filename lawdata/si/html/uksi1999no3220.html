STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

1999 No. 3220

JUSTICES OF THE PEACE, ENGLAND AND WALES

The Petty Sessions Areas (Amendment) Order 1999

  Made 1st December 1999 
  Coming into force 1st January 2000 

The Lord Chancellor, in exercise of the powers conferred on him by section 4 of the Justices of the Peace Act 1997[1], makes the following Order:

Citation and commencement
     1. This Order may be cited as the Petty Sessions Areas (Amendment) Order 1999 and shall come into force on 1st January 2000.

Petty sessions areas
     2. In Part I of the Schedule to the Petty Sessions Areas Order 1999[2] - 

    (a) for the entries in column 1 opposite the entry "BEDFORDSHIRE" in column 2 there shall be substituted - "Bedford and Mid BedfordshireLuton and South Bedfordshire"[3];

    (b) for the entries in column 1 opposite the entry "ESSEX" in column 2 there shall be substituted - "Mid-North EssexMid-South EssexNorth-East EssexNorth-West EssexSouth-East EssexSouth-West Essex"[4];

    (c) for the entries in column 1 opposite the entry "KENT" in column 2 there shall be substituted - "Canterbury and St. AugustineChannelDartford and GraveshamFaversham and SittingbourneMedwayMid KentThanetWest Kent"[5];

    (d) for the entries in column 1 opposite the entry "STAFFORDSHIRE" in column 2 there shall be substituted - "Burton upon TrentCannock and SeisdonLichfield and TamworthMid Staffordshire and RugeleyNorth StaffordshireStoke-on-Trent"[6];

    (e) for the entries in column 1 opposite the entry "WILTSHIRE" in column 2 there shall be substituted - "North West WiltshireSouth East WiltshireSwindon"[7].



Signed by the authority of the Lord Chancellor


Jane Kennedy
Parliamentary Secretary Lord Chancellor's Department

Dated 1st December 1999


EXPLANATORY NOTE

(This note is not part of the Order)


This Order amends the list of petty sessions areas in the Petty Sessions Areas Order 1999 (which was the list of petty sessions areas in existence immediately before 27th September 1999) to take account of changes that were effected by Orders made before 27th September 1999, but coming into force on 1st January 2000.

Notes:

 [1] 1997 c. 25. A new version of section 4 was substituted by section 75 of the Access to Justice Act 1999 (c. 22).back

[2] S.I. 1999/3009.back

[3] These petty sessions areas were constituted by S.I. 1999/150, as amended by S.I. 1999/2466.back

[4] These petty sessions areas were constituted by S.I. 1998/1353 and 1354.back

[5] The petty sessions area of Dartford and Gravesham was constituted by S.I. 1999/2105. The other petty sessions areas are unchanged.back

[6] The petty sessions areas of Cannock and Seisdon, Lichfield and Tamworth, Mid Staffordshire and Rugeley, and North Staffordshire were constituted by S.I. 1999/2399. The other petty sessions areas are unchanged. back

[7] The petty sessions areas of North West Wiltshire and South East Wiltshire were constituted by S.I. 1999/1500 as amended by S.I. 1999/1850. The other petty sessions area is unchanged. back
