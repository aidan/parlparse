EASTERN RENT ASSESSMENT PANEL

 

 

SUMMARY STATEMENT OF REASONS FOR THE DECISION OF THE COMMITTEE WHICH MET ON 11TH APRIL 2003 TO DETERMINE A FAIR RENT IN RESPECT OF 12 CASLON WAY, LETCHWORTH, HERTS

 

 

 

Landlord                                                                     :           Gayclean Freeholds Ltd

 

Tenant                                                                                    :           Mr S Howard

 

Rent at date of application                                       :           £65.50 per week

 

Rent proposed by applicant                                     :           £198.46 per week

 

Rent determined by Rent Officer                             :           £71.50 per week

 

Rent determined by Committee                              :           £71.50 per week

 

 

MEMBERS OF THE COMMITTEE

 

Mr W G Handford FRICS (Chairman)

Lady Auld LL.B

Mr P A Tunley

 

1.                  The Property

 

The Committee inspected the property in the presence of the Tenant and found it to be a three bedroom centre terrace house as described in the Rent Register.

 

 2.        Condition

 

Fair but outside paintwork neglected and rear patio sunken.  Porch recently rebuilt.  Landlord has not provided central heating nor fitted carpets.  Tenant has improved kitchen and bathroom.

 

3.        Location

 

On the Grange estate, close to local shops and bus service.  One and a half miles from Letchworth town centre.

 

4.         Scarcity

 

                  Assessed at 10%.

 

5.                  The Law Applied

 

See attached.

 

 

6.                  Open Market Rent

 

Taking into account the evidence and Committee members knowledge and experience, the open market rent was decided at £650 per calendar month for a similar property in good repair with modern facilities and fitted carpets.

 

 

7.                  Committees Calculations

 

Open Market rent                                                                                    £650 per month

 

Less global deduction for condition, lack of

modern facilities and fitted carpets and for                                         

tenants improvements                                                                           £200 per month

 

Open Market rent for subject property                                                  £450 per month

                                                                                                                 

10% deduction for scarcity                                                                    £  45.00 per month

 

£405.00 per month

 

= £93.46 per week or say      £  93.50 per week

 

 

8.                  The Rent Acts (Maximum Fair Rent Order) 1999

 

The capped rent calculated in accordance with the formula set out in the Order is £71.50 per week.  The re-building of the porch did not, in the Committees view, increase the rental value of the property by more than 15%.

 

9.                  Decision

 

As the capped rent is less than the Committees calculations, the fair rent is £71.50 per week.
