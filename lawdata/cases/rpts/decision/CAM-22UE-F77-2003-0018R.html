THE PREMISES:                                                      The property comprises a detached two-storey chalet style property containing three rooms, kitchen, bathroom and conservatory to the ground floor with two bedrooms to the first floor.  There is no central heating.

 

CONDITION:                                                            Extremely poor with severe dampness shown to the conservatory, upper bedroom windows in possibly dangerous condition and with much rotten timber to the conservatory and veranda.

 

TENANT’S IMPROVEMENTS:                             Some minor improvements have been carried out by the tenant from time-to-time.

 

LOCATION:                                                              Medium class residential area.

 

SCARCITY:                                                               Assessed at 20%.

 

THE LAW APPLIED:                                               See attached.

 

 

 

OPEN MARKET RENT:                                         Assessed at £550 per calendar month for a similar property but in good condition and with central heating, carpets, curtains and modern fittings to kitchen and bathroom.
