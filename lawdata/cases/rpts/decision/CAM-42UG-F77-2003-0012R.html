EASTERN RENT ASSESSMENT PANEL

 

SUMMARY STATEMENT OF REASONS FOR THE DECISION OF THE COMMITTEE WHICH MET ON 10 APRIL 2003 TO DETERMINE A FAIR RENT IN RESPECT OF 2 KISSINGATE COTTAGES, BRUISYARD, SAXMUNDHAM, SUFFOLK

 

 

 

Landlord: Mr R C Rous

 

Tenant: Mr R Hayward

 

Rent at Date of Application: 242.00 per calendar month

 

Rent Proposed by Landlord: 425.00 per calendar month

 

Rent Determined by Rent Officer: 260.00 per calendar month

 

Rent Determined by the Committee: 315.00 per calendar month

 

Members of the Committee: Mr V C Raywood (Chair)

Mr E A Pennington FRICS

Mr R S Rehan

 

 

THE PREMISES: The Committee inspected the property in the presence of the Tenant and found it as described in the Rent Register.

 

CONDITION: Reasonably good, but ill fitting and rotting windows, leaking gutters and external decorations poor.

 

TENANTS IMPROVEMENTS: Electric heaters in hall and landing.

 

LOCATION: Rural and on the edge of the village of Bruisyard. Usual amenities available in Framlingham and Saxmundham.

 

SCARCITY: Assessed at 12%.

 

THE LAW APPLIED: See attached.

 

OPEN MARKET RENT: Taking into account evidence and Committee Members knowledge and experience decided at 450 per calendar month for similar property in good condition with modern facilities, carpets, curtains and some white goods.

 

COMMITTEES CALCULATIONS: Open market rent 450.00 pcm

Less global deduction for condition and

Lack of modern facilities 90.00 pcm

 

360.00 pcm

 

Less deduction for scarcity 45.00 pcm

 

315.00 pcm

 

THE RENT ACTS (MAXIMUM FAIR This Order has no application in this case.

RENT ORDER) 1999

 

DECISION: The fair rent is 315 per calendar month.

This is a summary of the Committees reasons for its decision. Should you require more detailed reasons, they can be supplied, but only if a written request for such reasons is received by the Panel Office within 21 days from the receipt of this document. The papers will then be returned to the Chair for such detailed reasons to be prepared.

 

CAUTION: For the purpose of reaching a decision the Committee inspected the subject property. Such inspection is not a structural survey and only takes a few minutes. Any comments about the condition of the property in this Statement of Reasons are made as a result of casual observation rather than a detailed inspection. Please do not rely upon such comments as a guide to the structural condition of the property.
