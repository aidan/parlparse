CAM/42UD/MDR/2003/009

 

 

 

 

 EASTERN RENT ASSESSMENT PANEL

 

Summary Statement Of Reasons for the decision of the Committee

which met on 3 November 2003 to consider a request for Determination of a Rent under Section 22 of the Housing Act 1988 in respect of Flat 3, 24 Fonnereau Road Ipswich IP1 3JP   

 

 

        Landlord:                                              Pearl & Coutts Ltd  

        Tenant:                                                  Ms C Foulger     

        Rent at Date of Application:             £360.00 per Calendar Month   

         

 

Members of the Committee:            Mrs J H Lancaster                              Chairman

     Mr   E Pennington FRICS                  Valuer

                                                                        Mr D Wilson                                        Lay Member

 

 

 

 

THE PREMISES                                Small and rather dark first floor flat in converted period house. Shared garden and entrance hall.

 

ACCOMODATION                             Small entrance lobby, double bedroom, sitting room off which are kitchen and bath/WC. Central heating. Landlord provided carpets but no curtains or white goods.

 

CONDITION:                                     Tenant said sitting room window ill-fitting and draughty. Kitchen

                                                        units in poor condition, as is lino in kitchen. Neither entrance hall

                                                        nor garden appear to be properly maintained/cleaned.

 

 

TENANT’S IMPROVEMENTS:           None of significance.

 

 

LOCATION:                                      Close to centre of Ipswich in sought-after residential area.  

 

 

 

 

THE LAW APPLIED:                         Section 22 Housing Act 1988.

 

 

 

 

 

 

                                                           

DECISION:                                                       Taking into account the evidence of both the Landlord

        and the Tenant the Committee decided that the rent of                    £360.00pcm is not significantly higher than the rent                       which the Landlord might reasonably be expected to                     obtain under the Tenancy, and so made no                                   determination.

Caution:                 For the purpose of reaching a decision the Committee inspected the subject properties.  Such inspection is not a structural survey and only takes a few minutes.  Any comments about the condition of the properties in this Statement of Reasons are made as a result of casual observation rather than a detailed inspection. Please do not rely upon such comments as a guide to the structural condition of the properties.
