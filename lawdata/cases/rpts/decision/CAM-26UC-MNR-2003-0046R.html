EASTERN RENT ASSESSMENT PANEL

 

 

 

SUMMARY STATEMENT OF REASONS FOR THE DECISION OF THE COMMITTEE WHICH MET ON 30TH JUNE 2003 TO DETERMINE AN OPEN MARKET RENT IN RESPECT OF

 46 EASTWICK ROW, HEMEL HEMPSTEAD, HERTS

 

 

 

Landlord                                                                     :           Hightown, Praetorian & Churches

                                                                                                Housing Association Ltd

 

Tenant                                                                                    :           Mr Chong Tiong Ng

 

Rent at date of application                                       :           £70.46 per week

 

Rent proposed by applicant                                     :           £71.91 per week

 

Rent determined by Committee                              :           £71.91 per week

 

 

MEMBERS OF THE COMMITTEE

 

Mr W G Handford FRICS (Chairman)

Mrs Indira Butcher

Mr Barry Tyers

 

 

1.                  The Property

 

The Committee inspected the property in the presence of the Tenant and found it to be a first floor studio flat in a purpose built block.

 

 2.        Condition

 

Satisfactory but kitchen and bathroom fittings are worn.  No central heating, no white goods and no fitted carpets.

 

3.        Location

 

Residential area in part of the new town about one mile from the centre of Hemel Hempstead.

 

4.          The Law Applied

 

Sections 13 & 14 Housing Act 1988.

 

 

 

 

 

5.                  Open Market Rent

 

Taking into account the evidence and Committee members’ knowledge and experience, decided at £98 per week for a similar property with modern fittings, central heating, fitted carpets and white goods.

 

6.                  Committee’s Calculations

 

Open Market rent                                                                                    £98 per week

 

Less global deduction for condition                                                      £26 per week

 

Open Market rent for subject property                                                  £72 per week

                                                                                                                 

 

7.                  Decision

 

Market rent is £72 per week to include service charge of £14.32 per week.
