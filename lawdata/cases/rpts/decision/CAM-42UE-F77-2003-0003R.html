THE PREMISES                                                     The Committee inspected the property in the presence of the Tenant and found it as described in the Rent Register.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

CONDITION:                                                          Average condition for age but suffers a variety of minor    defects common to properties of this age.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

TENANT’S IMPROVEMENTS:                            Kitchen refitted with extensive wall tiling.   

                                                                                                   Bathroom substantially refitted with extensive wall tiling.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

LOCATION:                                                           Located in the centre of the village of Occold adjacent to  the local school and 2 miles from the market town of Eye.

 

 

 

 

 

 

SCARCITY:                                                           Assessed at  10%

 

THE LAW APPLIED:                                     Rent Act 1977 section 70

 

 

OPEN MARKET RENT:                                              Taking into account evidence and Committee members’ knowledge and experience, decided at £ 450 for similar property in good condition with modern facilities, carpets, curtains and some white goods.
