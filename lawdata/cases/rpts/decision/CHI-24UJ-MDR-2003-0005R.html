1. Background

On 22nd April 2003 the tenant of the above property applied to the Committee for a determination of a rent under an assured shorthold tenancy under section 22(1) of the Housing Act 1988 as amended by section 100 of the Housing Act 1996.

The tenancy commenced on 1 st December 2002 for a term of five years. The tenant

remains in occupation as a tenant under an assured shorthold tenancy agreement dated 28th November 2002.. The current rent is £1200 per month.

2. Inspection

The Committee inspected the property on the 20th May 2003 and found it to be in poor condition. The property is an old cob constructed cottage in need of general repair and decoration. Some downpipes were detached from the its buildings. Some of the windows were in poor condition. There was no central heating.

 

 

 

The following qualifying tenant's improvements had been made to the property. The installation of the range cooker and four cupboards in the kitchen area.

3. Evidence

The committee received written representations from the tenant and these were copied to the parties.

Neither party requested a hearing at which oral representations could be made.

4. The law

In accordance with the terms of section 22 Housing Act 1988 the Committee

proceeded to determine the rent at which it considered that the subject property might reasonably be expected to be let on the open market by a willing landlord under an assured shorthold tenancy.

In coming to its decision the Committee had regard to the evidence supplied by the

Tenant and the members' own general knowledge of market rent levels in the area of New Milton, Christchurch and the area between Wareham & Poole and concluded that an appropriate market rent for the property would be £975 per month.

Note: the Committee may want to indicate here any particularly compelling market rental evidence on which it relied.

5. The decision

The Committee therefore concluded that the rent at which the property might

reasonably be expected to be let on the open market would be £975 per month.

 

 

 

This rent will take effect from the 20th May 2003. Chairman (signed) Mr T George Dated 20th May 2003
