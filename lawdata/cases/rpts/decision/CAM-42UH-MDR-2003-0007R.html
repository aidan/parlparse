EASTERN RENT ASSESSMENT PANEL

 

Summary Statement Of Reasons for the decision of the Committee

which met on 6 August 2003 to determine a Rent under the Housing Act 1988 in respect of 4 Lowestoft Road, Carlton Colville, Lowestoft ,Suffolk NR33 8JE

 

 

        Landlord:                                              Mr B Glanfield

        Tenant:                                                  Ms M Rose   

        Rent at Date of Application:             £400.00    per calendar month 

       Rent Determined by Committee:    Non determination.

 

Members of the Committee:            Mrs J H Lancaster                                    Chairman

     Mr E A Pennington FRICS                      Valuer

                                                                        Mr R Rehahn                                              Lay Member

 

 

 

 

THE PREMISES:                               Semi-detached rendered brick bungalow with asbestos tiled roof, built in 1920’s. Gardens front and rear, off road parking, vehicular access to rear. Nissan garage converted to store.

 

ACCOMODATION:                            Hall, sitting room, kitchen/diner, bath/WC, 2 bedrooms ( 1   double and 1 large single). Gas fired central heating. No curtains or white goods provided.

 

CONDITION:

 

EXTERNAL:                                      Satisfactory, except for gable end, soffit boards and guttering.      

 

INTERNAL:                                       Good.     

 

 

TENANT’S IMPROVEMENTS:           None of significance.

 

 

LOCATION:                                      In centre of Carlton Colville, convenient for shops, local transport etc. 

 

 

                                                       

THE LAW APPLIED:                         Section 22 Housing Act 1988 as amended.

 

 

 

OPEN MARKET RENT:                      Taking into account evidence and Committee members’ knowledge and experience, decided at £420.00 pcm for similar property in good condition with modern facilities, carpets, curtains and some white goods.   

 

 

COMMITTEE’S CALCULATIONS:      Open market rent                                                           £420.00pcm

                                                        less global deduction for condition                      

 

                                                  lack of  carpets and white goods etc                     £20.00pcm

 

                                                  Open market for subject property                          £400.00pcm

 

                                                                                                                                                                                  

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 

 

DECISION:                                       As the Committee calculation of rent was equal to the rent at  the date of application no determination was made.
