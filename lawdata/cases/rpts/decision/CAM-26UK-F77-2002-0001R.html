EASTERN RENT ASSESSMENT PANEL

 

Summary Statement of Reasons for the decision of the Committee which met on 16th January 2003 to determine a Fair Rent in respect of 2 Sydney Road, Watford, Herts

 

 

 

Landlord                                                                     :           Mrs H Rickwood

 

Tenant                                                                                    :           Mrs B L Setterfield

 

Rent at date of application                                       :           £  54.00 per week

Rent proposed by applicant                                     :           £500.00 per month

Rent determined by Rent Officer                             :           £  54.00 per week

Rent determined by Committee                              :           £  54.00 per week

 

 

MEMBERS OF THE COMMITTEE

 

Mr W G Handford FRICS (Chairman)

Mr J J Sims

Mr B Tyers

 

 

The Property                                                                       The Committee inspected the property in the presence of the tenant and found it to be a three bedroom end of terrace house.

 

 

Condition                                                                             The property is in fair condition although there is structural movement in the end wall.  Tenant has provided the kitchen and bathroom fittings.  Landlord provided double glazed windows in 1998. No central heating.

 

 

Location                                                                               Older residential/industrial area in West Watford.

 

 

Scarcity                                                                                 Assessed at 10%.

 

 

The Law Applied                                                                See attached.

 

 

 

 

 

 

Open Market Rent                                                              Taking into account evidence and Committee members knowledge and experience, decided at £170 per week for similar property in good condition with modern facilities, fitted carpets etc.

 

 

Committees Calculations                                                Open market rent                  £170 p.w.

                                                                                                Less global deduction for

                                                                                                condition and lack of

modern facilities and

fitted carpets.                         £100 p.w.

 

                                                                                                Open market rent for

                                                                              subject property                     £  70 p.w.

 

                                                                                                10% deduction for scarcity  £    7 p.w.

 

                                                                                                                                                £  63 p.w.

 

The Rent Acts

(Maximum Fair Rent Order) 1999                                   The capped rent calculated in accordance with the formula set out in the Order is £54.50 per week.

 

 

Decision                                                                               As no substantial expenditure has been undertaken to the property by the Landlord since the last rent registration and as the capped rent is less than the Committees calculations the Fair Rent is £54.50 per week.
