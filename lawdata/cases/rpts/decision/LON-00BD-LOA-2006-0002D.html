Preliminary

 

 

  1. An application was made under s 85 Commonhold and Leasehold Reform act 2002 to appoint the Applicant RTM company as the manager of the premises known as 209 St Margarets Road Twickenham Middlesex TW11LU.
  2. The application was received by the Tribunal on 24 March 2006 following which Directions were made by the Tribunal on 5 April 2006.
  3. Those directions have been complied with by the Applicant.
  4. The Tribunal is satisified by  the Applicant’s evidence that the Respondent is absent and   cannot be traced and that appropriate measures have been employed to trace her.
  5. The Respondent was served with the application and Tribunal’s Directions. No response has been received from her.

6.         The Tribunal is satisfied that the Applicant is a          properly  constituted  

RTM company having the right to make an application under s 79 of the 2002 Act.

7. In these circumstances the Tribunal makes an  order that  the Applicant is hereby appointed and is entitled to manage the premises situate and known as  209 St Margarets Road Twickenham Middlesex TW11LU.
