HOUSING ACT 1988 Section 14

Premises: 23 Barnsbury Gardens, Newport Pagnell, Bucks

 

I refer to the Assured Tenancy Agreement dated 24th June 1993 between Warden Housing Association Ltd (Landlord) and Ms A Adams and Mr P Metcalf (Tenants).  I also refer to the notice, proposing a decrease in rent of £4 per month, served under Sec.13(2) of the Housing Act 1988 by the landlord on Ms Adams and dated 26th February 2003.

 

On 8th March 2003 Ms Adams made application to the Rent Assessment Committee who met on 1st May 2003, viewed 23 Barnsbury Gardens and subsequently listened to representations from both parties on the amount of rent payable for the property.

 

Having carefully considered the matter the Committee members came to the conclusion that they had no jurisdiction in this case.  Although the Landlords notice was served under Sec.13(2) of the Housing Act 1988 this section refers only to increases in rent and there is no provision for a tenant to object to a decrease.

 

During the hearing the Tenant referred to the increase in rent which occurred in April 2002 and in order to make a valid objection to that increase the Tenant would have had to lodge an application to the Committee before that increase took effect.  Should the Landlord serve notice of an increase in April 2004 the tenant would of course be entitled to make application to the Committee before the date of any proposed increase.
